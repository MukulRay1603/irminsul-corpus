# Artifact System and Optimization

Artifacts are a fundamental system in Genshin Impact, providing the primary source of character stat amplification and unique set bonuses that define combat roles and optimize damage output. Each character can equip five artifacts, one for each distinct slot: Flower of Life, Plume of Death, Sands of Eon, Goblet of Eonothem, and Circlet of Logos.

## Artifact Slots and Main Stats

Each of the five artifact slots has a fixed primary stat type or a selection of possible primary stats, which significantly influences character builds.

*   **Flower of Life:** Always provides a flat HP main stat. This stat scales with artifact level, offering a substantial HP boost.
*   **Plume of Death:** Always provides a flat ATK main stat. Similar to the Flower, this offers a significant flat ATK increase.
*   **Sands of Eon:** Can have one of five possible main stats:
    *   ATK%
    *   DEF%
    *   HP%
    *   Energy Recharge%
    *   Elemental Mastery
*   **Goblet of Eonothem:** Offers the widest variety of main stats, including all elemental damage bonuses:
    *   Pyro DMG Bonus%
    *   Hydro DMG Bonus%
    *   Anemo DMG Bonus%
    *   Geo DMG Bonus%
    *   Electro DMG Bonus%
    *   Dendro DMG Bonus%
    *   Physical DMG Bonus%
    *   ATK%
    *   DEF%
    *   HP%
    *   Elemental Mastery
*   **Circlet of Logos:** Focuses on critical stats, healing, and other utility:
    *   CRIT Rate%
    *   CRIT DMG%
    *   Healing Bonus%
    *   ATK%
    *   DEF%
