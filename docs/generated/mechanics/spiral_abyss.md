# Spiral Abyss Guide

The Spiral Abyss is Genshin Impact's primary endgame challenge, testing a player's roster depth, character building, and combat proficiency. It is structured as a series of combat encounters across multiple floors, culminating in the "Abyssal Moon Spire" (Floors 9-12) which resets biweekly, offering valuable Primogem rewards.

## Abyss Structure and Progression

The Spiral Abyss is divided into two main sections:

### Floors 1-8: Abyssal Corridor
These floors are a one-time clear challenge. Each floor consists of three chambers, and players must clear all three chambers of a floor to unlock the next. Rewards for these floors are primarily Mora, Hero's Wit, and Mystic Enhancement Ore, granted upon achieving specific star thresholds. Starting from Floor 5, players are required to field two distinct teams for alternating halves of each chamber.

### Floors 9-12: Abyssal Moon Spire
This section represents the true endgame challenge. Like the Abyssal Corridor, each floor has three chambers, but the enemy lineups, Ley Line Disorders, and Abyssal Blessings rotate every 1st and 16th day of each month (biweekly reset). Players must field two distinct teams for each chamber, one for the first half and one for the second half. Rewards for clearing these floors are the most significant, offering up to 600 Primogems for a full 36-star clear, alongside Mora, Hero's Wit, and Mystic Enhancement Ore. Progress and stars obtained in the Abyssal Moon Spire are reset with each rotation.

## Star System and Rewards

Each chamber within the Spiral Abyss offers up to 3 stars, awarded based on the time taken to complete the combat challenge. A total of 36 stars can be achieved across Floors 9-12.

### Star Conditions per Chamber:
*   **3 Stars:** Clear within 180 seconds (3 minutes)
*   **2 Stars:** Clear within 240 seconds (4 minutes)
*   **1 Star:** Clear within 300 seconds (5 minutes)

The timer for each chamber starts upon engaging the first enemy wave and pauses between waves. If a character falls, they cannot be revived within the chamber, but they will be available for subsequent chambers. Players can retry any chamber to improve their star count without affecting previously earned stars.

### Primogem Rewards (Floors 9-12):
*   **Floor 9:** 50 Primogems for 3/6/9 stars (total 150)
*   **Floor 10:** 50 Primogems for 3/6/9 stars (total 150)
*   **Floor 11:** 50 Primogems for 3/6/9 stars (total 150)
*   **Floor 12:** 50 Primogems for 3/6/9 stars (total 150)
