# Resin and Progression System

## Original Resin

Original Resin is the primary energy resource in Genshin Impact, gating access to most endgame content and character progression materials. The maximum capacity for Original Resin is 160. Resin regenerates at a rate of 1 Resin every 8 minutes, meaning a full refill from 0 to 160 takes 21 hours and 20 minutes. Players can hold a maximum of 160 Resin at any time; any Resin gained beyond this cap will be lost. This regeneration rate is constant and unaffected by player level or world level.

Original Resin is consumed in various activities:

*   **Domains:** Most Domains of Forgery (for Weapon Ascension Materials) and Domains of Blessing (for Artifacts) cost 20 Original Resin per run. Some Domains of Mastery (for Talent Books) also cost 20 Original Resin. Certain Domains of Forgery, like those for Character Ascension Materials, are not gated by Resin but by World Level.
*   **World Bosses:** Elite Bosses such as the Cryo Hypostasis, Electro Hypostasis, Geo Hypostasis, Pyro Hypostasis, Anemo Hypostasis, Oceanid, Jadeplume Terrorshroom, Electro Forest Eagle, Perpetual Mechanical Array, Bathysmal Vishap Herd, and Ruin Serpent cost 40 Original Resin to claim rewards from.
*   **Weekly Bosses:** These include the Stormterror (Dvalin), Andrius (Wolf of the North), Childe (Tartaglia), Azhdaha, and the Golden Slime Emperor. Each weekly boss costs 30 Original Resin to claim rewards from, with the first three claims per week being discounted to 30 Resin. After the third claim, subsequent claims for the week will cost 60 Original Resin. There is a cap of 3 weekly boss claims per week, resetting every Monday at 4:00 AM server time.
*   **Ley Line Outcrops:** These provide Character EXP materials (Crimson Agate or Azure Agate) or Mora. They cost 20 Original Resin per run.

## Condensed Resin

Condensed Resin is a craftable item that allows players to store up to 5 Original Resin and receive double rewards from Domains of Forgery and Domains of Blessing in a single run, while consuming 40 Original Resin. To craft Condensed Resin, players need 100 Lumina Crystals (obtained from Crystal Chunks mined with Geo damage or from the Crystal Core merchant) and 1 Original Resin. Condensed Resin can only be crafted at an Alchemy Bench. It can be used in Domains of Forgery and Domains of Blessing, but not in Ley Line Outcrops or Boss encounters. Using Condensed Resin in a Domain provides rewards equivalent to two normal runs, effectively saving time by reducing the number of runs required. The maximum number of Condensed Resin a player can hold is 5.

## Fragile Resin

Fragile Resin is a consumable item that instantly restores 60 Original Resin. Fragile Resin is a highly valuable resource, primarily obtained through:

*   **Adventure Rank Rewards:** Players receive Fragile Resin at specific Adventure Rank milestones, typically starting from AR 10 and continuing through higher ranks.
*   **Battle Pass:** The Gnostic Hymn and Gnostic Patron tiers of the Battle Pass offer Fragile Resin as rewards.
*   **Archon Quests and Story Quests:** Certain significant quests may reward Fragile Resin upon completion.
*   **Limited-Time Events:** Many in-game events offer Fragile Resin as part of their reward pool.
*   **Primogem Purchases:** Players can exchange 60 Primogems for 1 Original Resin, up to a maximum of 6 times per day. This is generally considered an inefficient use of Primogems compared to other uses like acquiring characters or weapons, but it can be a last resort for players desperate for Resin. Fragile Resin obtained from other sources bypasses this Primogem conversion limit.

## Daily Commissions

Daily Commissions are a set of four randomized quests that players can complete each day. They reset daily at 4:00 AM server time. Completing all four commissions grants 10 Primogems per commission, totaling 40 Primogems. Additionally, players receive 200 Adventure Rank EXP and 500 Mora. After completing all four commissions, players must visit Katheryne at any Adventurers' Guild to claim an additional reward, which includes 500 Adventure Rank EXP, 20 Primogems, and a small amount of Mora and character EXP materials. This makes Daily Commissions a crucial source of Primogems and Adventure Rank EXP, totaling 100 Primogems and 1000 Adventure Rank EXP per day if all commissions and the Adventurers' Guild bonus are claimed.

## Weekly Bosses

Weekly Bosses are powerful opponents that offer unique materials essential for character ascension and talent upgrades. There are currently five Weekly Bosses: Stormterror (Dvalin), Andrius (Wolf of the North), Childe (Tartaglia), Azhdaha, and the Golden Slime Emperor. Each of these bosses can be challenged once per week.

The cost to claim rewards from Weekly Bosses is structured as follows:

*   **First Three Claims:** 30 Original Resin each.
*   **Subsequent Claims:** 60 Original Resin each.

There is a hard cap of three reward claims per week across all Weekly Bosses. For example, if a player claims rewards from Stormterror once, Andrius once, and Childe once, they have used all three of their weekly claims. They can still challenge these bosses again, but will not receive any rewards. This weekly reset occurs every Monday at 4:00 AM server time. The materials dropped by Weekly Bosses are specific to certain characters and are often required for their higher talent levels (Level 7 and above).

## Battle Pass

The Battle Pass system, known as the "Gnostic Hymn" (paid version) and "Gnostic Patron" (premium version), offers a tiered progression system that rewards players with various resources as they accumulate Battle Pass EXP. Battle Pass EXP is earned by completing Daily Commissions, Weekly Bosses, and event-specific quests, as well as by spending Resin. The Battle Pass has a total of 50 levels, and players can choose one of four 4-star weapons upon reaching level 30. Higher tiers in the Battle Pass also offer Fragile Resin, Crown of Insight, Talent Books, Mora, and Mystic Enhancement Ore. The Gnostic Hymn unlocks additional rewards at each level, while the Gnostic Patron unlocks all rewards from the Gnostic Hymn and provides an additional 10 Battle Pass levels and unique cosmetic items. The Battle Pass is available for a limited duration, typically lasting for 6 weeks.

## Resin Spending Priority by Account Progression Stage

Efficiently spending Original Resin is crucial for maximizing character progression and overall account strength. The priority shifts significantly based on a player's Adventure Rank (AR) and stage of progression.

### Early Game (AR 1-35)

In the early stages of the game, the primary goal is to increase Adventure Rank to unlock more content and raise World Level. Resin should be spent on materials that directly facilitate this.

*   **Ley Line Outcrops:** Focus on spending Resin on Ley Line Outcrops for Character EXP books (Wanderer's Advice, Adventurer, Hero's Wit) and Mora. These are essential for leveling up characters and their weapons, which are required to clear early-game quests and increase AR.
*   **World Bosses:** Claiming rewards from World Bosses is important for character ascension materials needed to break the level caps at AR 25, 35, and 45. Prioritize bosses whose materials are needed for your main damage dealers.
*   **Domains (Early):** Early on, artifact sets are less impactful due to lower stats and the difficulty of obtaining good substats. However, if specific character ascension materials are needed from Domains of Forgery, those should be prioritized. Talent Book domains are generally not a priority until much later.
*   **Fragile Resin:** Use Fragile Resin to push AR milestones if needed, but generally conserve it for later stages.

### Mid Game (AR 36-45)

As players approach AR 45, their focus shifts towards building a solid core team. World Level increases become more significant, and higher-tier materials become available.

*   **Talent Book Domains:** This is where Talent Book domains become a high priority. Talent levels are a significant source of damage and utility scaling, especially for the main DPS and supports. Prioritize the books needed for your core team's key talents.
*   **Artifact Domains (AR 45+):** At AR 45, the highest level of Artifact Domains guarantees at least one 5-star artifact. This is the point where serious artifact farming should begin. Prioritize the artifact sets that best suit your main damage dealers and supports. Use Condensed Resin here to maximize efficiency.
*   **Weekly Bosses:** Continue to claim rewards from Weekly Bosses to obtain character-specific ascension materials and talent materials.
*   **World Bosses:** Continue to farm World Bosses for ascension materials as needed.

### Late Game (AR 45+)

The late game is characterized by the pursuit of optimal character builds and pushing challenging content like the Spiral Abyss.

*   **Artifact Domains:** This remains the primary Resin sink. Players will spend the vast majority of their Resin here, aiming for perfect substats on 5-star artifacts. Farming specific sets for multiple characters and their supports is a continuous process. Using Condensed Resin is highly recommended.
*   **Talent Book Domains:** Continue to farm Talent Books as needed for further talent level upgrades, especially for talents that scale exceptionally well.
*   **Weekly Bosses:** Continue to claim rewards weekly for talent materials and character ascension materials for new characters or to level up existing ones.
*   **Event Materials:** During limited-time events, prioritize spending Resin on event-specific rewards if they offer valuable resources like Primogems, Fragile Resin, or Crown of Insight.
*   **Conserving Fragile Resin:** Fragile Resin should be saved for AR 45 and beyond, when artifact farming becomes the most Resin-efficient activity. Using it before AR 45 means receiving lower-tier artifacts, which is generally a waste.

By understanding the Resin system and adapting spending priorities based on progression, players can more effectively build their characters and overcome the challenges Genshin Impact presents.
