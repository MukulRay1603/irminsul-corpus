# Team Building Fundamentals

Team building in Genshin Impact is a complex interplay of character roles, elemental reactions, energy management, and strategic synergy, forming the bedrock of effective combat. A well-constructed team maximizes damage output, ensures survivability, and optimizes resource generation to clear challenging content efficiently.

## Core Team Roles

Every character in a four-person team typically fulfills one or more distinct roles, contributing to the team's overall strategy.

### Main DPS (Damage Per Second)
The Main DPS is the primary on-field damage dealer, spending the most time actively attacking enemies. Their kit is designed for sustained or burst damage, often relying on their Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, or Elemental Skill/Burst when on-field. They are typically the most heavily invested character in terms of artifacts and talents, and the team is often built to buff their damage.
*   **Examples:** Hu Tao, Raiden Shogun (Hypercarry), Ayaka, Neuvillette.

### Sub-DPS (Sub-Damage Per Second)
Sub-DPS characters contribute significant damage while off-field, typically through their Elemental Skill or Burst. They are swapped in briefly to cast their abilities and then swapped out, allowing the Main DPS to return to the field. Their off-field damage often synergizes with the Main DPS's on-field presence, contributing to elemental reactions or consistent damage pressure.
*   **Examples:** Xingqiu, Yelan, Fischl, Xiangling, Albedo.

### Support
Support characters enhance the team's performance without being primary damage dealers themselves. Their utility can include:
*   **Buffing:** Increasing allies' ATK, Elemental DMG, CRIT Rate/DMG (e.g., Bennett, Kazuha, Shenhe, Faruzan).
*   **Healing:** Restoring HP to allies (e.g., Bennett, Kokomi, Jean, Baizhu).
*   **Shielding:** Providing damage mitigation and interruption resistance (e.g., Zhongli, Layla, Diona).
*   **Crowd Control (CC):** Grouping enemies for easier targeting (e.g., Kazuha, Sucrose, Venti).
*   **Elemental Application:** Consistently applying an element to enable reactions (e.g., Nahida, Kokomi).
*   **Debuffing:** Reducing enemy resistances or ATK (e.g., Zhongli, Viridescent Venerer users).
*   **Examples:** Bennett, Kazuha, Zhongli, Nahida, Furina.

### Battery
A Battery character's primary function is to generate Elemental Particles or Orbs, which provide energy to other team members, allowing them to use their Elemental Bursts more frequently. This is crucial for teams that rely heavily on Burst damage or have characters with high energy costs. Batteries often share the same element as the character they are powering to maximize energy gain, as same-element particles grant significantly more energy.
*   **Examples:** Favonius weapon users (any element), Raiden Shogun (universal battery), Fischl (Electro), Xiangling (Pyro, via Bennett), Sucrose (Anemo).

## Elemental Reactions: Enablers and Triggers

Elemental reactions are fundamental to Genshin Impact's combat system. Understanding the roles of "enablers" and "triggers" is crucial for maximizing reaction damage.
*   **Enabler (Aura):** The character who applies the initial element to an enemy, creating an elemental aura (e.g., applying Hydro to an enemy).
