# Sigewinne

## Lore

Sigewinne is a Melusine who serves as the chief nurse within Meropide Fortress, the underwater penal colony of Fontaine. Her presence in this harsh environment underscores the evolving societal integration of Melusines in Fontaine, moving beyond their historical perception as mere assistants to the Gardes. Sigewinne embodies the Melusine capacity for empathy and dedication, traits that have earned her a position of trust and respect among both the inmates and the Fortress's administration.

Her relationship with Duke Wriothesley, the Administrator of Meropide Fortress, is notably warm and built on mutual respect. Wriothesley, despite his imposing demeanor and the nature of his work, demonstrates a genuine care for the well-being of those under his charge, and Sigewinne is a key figure in facilitating this. She acts as a confidante and a pragmatic advisor to him, often offering a gentler perspective that complements his more direct approach. This dynamic highlights Wriothesley's unconventional leadership style and his appreciation for Sigewinne's unique skills and compassionate nature. She is one of the few individuals with whom Wriothesley appears to be truly at ease, sharing moments of quiet understanding.

Being a Melusine in Fontaine carries specific societal connotations. Historically, Melusines were often relegated to roles of servitude or support, their unique physiology and origins setting them apart from humans. However, as Fontaine has progressed, particularly under the current Archon Furina's reign and the advancements in societal understanding, Melusines have gained greater acceptance and opportunities. Sigewinne's position as chief nurse is a testament to this progress. She represents a new generation of Melusines who are not defined by their past limitations but by their individual talents and contributions. Her existence challenges lingering prejudices and showcases the potential for Melusines to excel in any field they choose, contributing significantly to Fontainian society. Her species' innate connection to water and their distinctive physical traits, such as their large eyes and pointed ears, are often viewed with curiosity and, increasingly, with admiration, rather than suspicion. Sigewinne, through her actions and demeanor, actively shapes this perception, embodying the best qualities of her kind.

## Kit Mechanics

Sigewinne's kit revolves around her unique HP-scaling Hydro application and healing capabilities, making her a potent support character focused on enhancing team survivability and damage through Hydro-infused mechanics.

### Elemental Skill: Rebound Hydrotherapy

**Rebound Hydrotherapy** is Sigewinne's Elemental Skill, which allows her to throw a "Special Styptic" that bounces between opponents. Upon hitting an opponent, it deals Hydro DMG based on Sigewinne's Max HP. The Special Styptic can bounce up to 5 times between opponents, and each hit applies Hydro. After the bouncing sequence ends, it will create a "Soothing Bubblebalm" at its final location.

The Special Styptic's bouncing behavior is crucial for its effectiveness. It prioritizes opponents within a certain range and will bounce to up to 5 different targets. If there are fewer than 5 opponents, it will bounce between the available opponents, potentially hitting the same opponent multiple times if they are the only target or if the bounce mechanics allow for it within the target pool. The Hydro DMG dealt scales directly with Sigewinne's Max HP, making HP% and HP substats highly valuable for her offensive contribution.

The Soothing Bubblebalm generated at the end of the bounce sequence is a key utility. When an active character within its AoE consumes HP to trigger effects like those from the Fontemer Blade or certain character abilities, the Bubblebalm will heal them. The amount of healing is also based on Sigewinne's Max HP. This mechanic provides sustained healing to characters who intentionally lower their HP for damage amplification, synergizing well with characters like Neuvillette or those utilizing mechanics that consume HP. The Bubblebalm's healing is triggered by HP consumption, not just by being within its radius, meaning it's a reactive heal that rewards proactive HP management by the team.

The cooldown for Rebound Hydrotherapy is 12 seconds, and its duration for the bouncing effect is tied to the number of bounces or targets hit. The Soothing Bubblebalm persists for a set duration, allowing for continuous healing opportunities during combat encounters.

### Elemental Burst: Bolstering Bubblebalm

**Bolstering Bubblebalm** is Sigewinne's Elemental Burst, a powerful AoE Hydro ability that provides both significant healing and a substantial buff to the team's Max HP. Upon activation, Sigewinne deals Hydro DMG to all opponents in a large AoE based on her Max HP. This initial damage is a strong burst of Hydro application.

Following the initial damage, the Burst generates a "Sea of Plenty" effect. This effect heals all nearby party members based on Sigewinne's Max HP. More importantly, it grants all nearby party members a significant buff to their Max HP. This Max HP increase is a percentage of Sigewinne's own Max HP, meaning the higher Sigewinne's HP, the greater the survivability and potential damage output for the entire team, especially for characters whose damage scales with HP.

The healing from the Sea of Plenty is a potent burst heal, capable of restoring substantial amounts of HP to the entire party. The Max HP buff is a multiplicative increase, meaning it stacks with existing HP values and buffs from other sources, making it incredibly valuable for HP-scaling characters. This buff lasts for a considerable duration, providing a sustained advantage throughout the combat encounter.

The energy cost for Bolstering Bubblebalm is 70, and its cooldown is 18 seconds. The substantial energy requirement necessitates attention to Energy Recharge for Sigewinne to ensure consistent Burst uptime, especially in teams where her Burst is crucial for healing and buffs. The combination of AoE Hydro damage, powerful healing, and a significant Max HP buff makes her Burst a cornerstone of her supportive capabilities.

## Builds

Sigewinne's builds prioritize maximizing her Max HP to enhance her healing, Hydro application, and buffing capabilities. Crit stats are secondary but can contribute to her personal damage.

### Signature Weapon: Silvershower Heartstrings

**Silvershower Heartstrings** is Sigewinne's signature 5-star Bow. It directly increases the wearer's Max HP by a significant percentage. Its passive further enhances the DMG dealt by the wearer's Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst based on their Max HP. Additionally, it provides a further HP% bonus after the wearer's Elemental Skill hits an opponent, stacking up to 3 times. This weapon is tailor-made for Sigewinne, maximizing all aspects of her kit that scale with HP. The substantial HP boost from both its base stat and passive, combined with the DMG bonus to her Skill and Burst, makes it her undisputed Best-in-Slot.

### Strong 5-Star Alternative: Polar Star

While not HP-focused, **Polar Star** can be a strong option if Sigewinne is built with significant HP from artifacts. Its Crit Rate substat and ATK% passive can boost her personal damage. However, its passive's focus on Elemental Skill and Burst DMG bonus, coupled with its ATK scaling, means it's less synergistic with Sigewinne's HP scaling than dedicated HP weapons. It would require substantial HP from artifact substats to compensate for the lack of direct HP scaling.

### Strong 4-Star Option: Prototype Amber

**Prototype Amber** is an excellent craftable 4-star Bow for Sigewinne. It provides a substantial amount of Max HP as its secondary stat, directly boosting her core scaling. Its passive generates Elemental Orbs upon using an Elemental Burst, which then regenerates HP for all party members. This provides additional healing and energy regeneration for the team, further enhancing Sigewinne's supportive utility. While it doesn't offer the same offensive buffs as Silvershower Heartstrings, its HP bonus and team-wide healing make it a very strong and accessible option.

### Budget/F2P Options: Sacrificial Bow

The **Sacrificial Bow** series (Sacrificial Bow, Sacrificial Fragments - though the latter is for catalyst users) can be a viable option, particularly the 4-star Sacrificial Bow. Its secondary stat provides Elemental Mastery, which is not ideal for Sigewinne's scaling. However, its passive, which has a chance to reset the cooldown of the wearer's Elemental Skill after dealing damage, can allow for more frequent use of Rebound Hydrotherapy. This can lead to more Hydro application and more frequent generation of Soothing Bubblebalms, increasing her overall utility if her HP is already sufficiently built through artifacts. The R5 Sacrificial Bow's cooldown reset chance is 80%, making it quite reliable.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-Piece Ocean-Hued Clam:** This set is a top-tier choice for Sigewinne. The 2-piece bonus grants Healing Bonus, directly increasing her healing output. The 4-piece bonus creates a "Sea-Dyed Foam" after healing a character, which accumulates healing received. Upon expiration, the Foam explodes, dealing Physical DMG based on the accumulated healing. While the Physical DMG is not ideal for Sigewinne's Hydro focus, the massive healing potential it unlocks, combined with the healing bonus from the 2-piece, makes it a very strong generalist support set for her. The extra damage from the Foam can be significant.
*   **4-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith:** The 2-piece bonus grants Max HP%, which is excellent for Sigewinne. The 4-piece bonus triggers when the Elemental Skill hits an opponent, granting a 20% ATK buff and 15% Shield Strength buff to all party members for 3 seconds, refreshing every 0.5 seconds. If Sigewinne's Elemental Skill can consistently hit opponents (which it can through its bounces), this set provides a valuable party-wide ATK buff, indirectly boosting team damage. This set is particularly good if Sigewinne is not the sole healer or if the team benefits greatly from the ATK boost.
*   **2-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith / 2-Piece Vourukasha's Glow:** A combination of two 2-piece sets that grant Max HP% is a straightforward way to boost Sigewinne's HP. This provides a solid foundation for her scaling and is easy to farm if dedicated 4-piece sets are not available or optimal for the specific team composition.
*   **4-Piece Song of Days Past:** This set provides Healing Bonus with its 2-piece effect. Its 4-piece effect records the healing provided by the character and then unleashes that healing as DMG when the character leaves the field. This can be very effective for Sigewinne, as it allows her healing to contribute to team damage after she switches out, similar to the concept of Ocean-Hued Clam but focused on recorded healing rather than a separate damage instance.

**Stat Priorities:**

*   **Sands:** HP%
*   **Goblet:** HP%
*   **Circlet:** HP% or Healing Bonus% (HP% is generally preferred for maximizing buffs and overall scaling, but Healing Bonus can be situationally better if healing is the absolute priority and HP is already high)
*   **Substats:** HP% > Energy Recharge (aim for ~180-200% ER depending on team) > Crit Rate/DMG (for personal damage) > Flat HP

## Team Compositions

Sigewinne excels as a Hydro support and buffer, particularly in teams that benefit from HP scaling, consistent Hydro application, and enhanced survivability.

### Hydro Support and Buffer

Sigewinne's primary role is to provide consistent Hydro application through her Elemental Skill and significant healing and Max HP buffs via her Elemental Burst. She can slot into various teams where Hydro is needed for reactions or to enable specific character mechanics.

*   **Neuvillette Teams:** Sigewinne is an exceptional companion for Neuvillette. Her Elemental Burst provides a substantial Max HP buff, directly increasing Neuvillette's damage output from his Charged Attack. Her healing from both her Skill (via Soothing Bubblebalm) and Burst ensures Neuvillette stays healthy, allowing him to maintain his HP for maximum damage. The consistent Hydro application from her Skill can also help with Hydro resonance and potentially trigger Hydro-related reactions if other elements are present. A common setup could be Neuvillette, Sigewinne, Kazuha/Sucrose (for Anemo buffs and grouping), and a Flex character (e.g., Fischl for Electro-Charged, Zhongli for shielding and resistance shred).
*   **Wriothesley Teams:** In Freeze or Melt teams featuring Wriothesley, Sigewinne can provide Hydro application for Freeze and consistent healing. Her Max HP buff from her Burst can also indirectly benefit Wriothesley if he has any HP scaling mechanics or simply by increasing his survivability. However, her Hydro application might be less consistent for constant Freeze compared to dedicated Hydro applicators like Mona or Kokomi in some scenarios. A team could be Wriothesley, Sigewinne, an Anemo unit (e.g., Kazuha, Venti), and a Cryo support (e.g., Layla, Rosaria).
*   **Other HP-Scaling Teams:** Sigewinne's kit is designed to empower characters who scale with HP. This includes characters like Arlecchino (if she benefits from increased Max HP for her Bond of Life mechanics), or any team that can leverage increased Max HP for survivability or damage. Her ability to heal characters who intentionally consume HP (like those using the Fontemer Blade or certain character skills) makes her invaluable in teams that operate at lower HP thresholds for damage.

### HP-Scaling Teams

Sigewinne's presence is most impactful in teams where HP is a primary stat for damage or survivability.

*   **Neuvillette Hypercarry:** As mentioned, Neuvillette is a prime candidate. The synergy is almost perfect: Neuvillette deals massive damage based on his Max HP and the number of different Hydro sources in the team, and Sigewinne provides both a significant HP buff and healing. The team composition often includes Neuvillette, Sigewinne, an Anemo character for crowd control and Elemental RES Shred (like Kazuha or Venti), and a fourth slot that can provide additional Hydro application, buffs, or utility (e.g., another Hydro like Furina for Hydro Resonance and buffs, or a character like Fischl for Electro-Charged).
*   **General HP-Focused Support:** Sigewinne can function as a general HP buffer and healer in teams where the primary damage dealer benefits from increased Max HP. This could include teams where characters have mechanics that consume HP for damage, or simply where increased survivability is paramount. For example, in a team with Arlecchino, if her Bond of Life mechanics can be further amplified or sustained by Sigewinne's buffs and healing, it could be a viable strategy. The exact composition would depend on the specific needs of the damage dealer and the elemental reactions desired.
*   **Mono-Hydro Teams:** In a Mono-Hydro setup, Sigewinne's Hydro application, healing, and Max HP buff can significantly enhance the damage and survivability of other Hydro characters. For instance, a team of Neuvillette, Sigewinne, Furina, and Kazuha would benefit from multiple Hydro sources for Neuvillette's damage, Furina's Fanfare stacks, Sigewinne's buffs, and Kazuha's crowd control and elemental damage bonus. Sigewinne's healing would also help offset the HP drain from Furina's abilities.
