# Xiangling

## Lore and Background

Xiangling is a prodigious and adventurous chef from Liyue Harbor, renowned for her daring culinary experiments and her unwavering passion for discovering new ingredients and flavors. As the head chef of the Wanmin Restaurant, a beloved establishment in Liyue, she is responsible for creating innovative dishes that push the boundaries of traditional Liyue cuisine. Her insatiable curiosity extends beyond the kitchen; she is an avid explorer, often venturing into dangerous territories like the Guyun Stone Forest or the peaks of Mt. Aocang in search of rare ingredients, unafraid of the monsters that inhabit these regions. This adventurous spirit is a core part of her character, driving her to constantly seek out new culinary challenges and experiences.

Xiangling's dedication to her craft is evident in her approach to cooking, which she views as an art form and a science. She believes that the best dishes are born from a deep understanding of ingredients and a willingness to experiment with unique combinations. Her signature dish, the "Spicy Crispy Fish," is a testament to her innovative style, incorporating ingredients that many would deem too dangerous or unusual to consume. This bold approach has earned her both acclaim and a reputation for being somewhat eccentric.

Despite her sometimes reckless pursuit of culinary excellence, Xiangling possesses a kind heart and a strong sense of loyalty. She is a close friend to many in Liyue, including Xingqiu, Chongyun, and the adepti. Her connection to the adepti is particularly noteworthy; she has a friendly rivalry with the adeptus Mountain Shaper and is known to have a close relationship with Cloud Retainer. Her ability to befriend and interact with these ancient beings highlights her unique charm and her ability to bridge the gap between the mortal and divine realms.

Xiangling is also a free character obtained by clearing the third chamber of the Spiral Abyss, making her accessible to a vast majority of players relatively early in their Genshin Impact journey. This accessibility, combined with her exceptional combat capabilities, is a primary reason for her status as one of the most powerful and widely used characters in the game, often considered a cornerstone of many top-tier team compositions despite being a 4-star unit. Her kit's synergy with other characters, particularly Bennett, allows her to achieve damage outputs that rival or even surpass many 5-star characters, solidifying her position as a meta-defining unit.

## Combat Kit Mechanics

Xiangling's combat effectiveness stems from her powerful Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst, both of which deal Pyro DMG.

### Elemental Skill: Guoba Attack

Guoba Attack is Xiangling's Elemental Skill. Upon activation, Guoba, her loyal pyro spirit companion, appears and breathes fire in a cone-shaped AoE in front of Xiangling. Guoba attacks periodically, dealing Pyro DMG. The range and AoE of Guoba's fire breath are moderate, making it effective for applying Pyro to groups of enemies or for consistent damage against single targets. Guoba's attacks have a cooldown of 2 seconds between each breath. The skill itself has a cooldown of 9 seconds and lasts for 10 seconds. While Guoba is active, Xiangling can reposition or switch to another character to prepare for her Elemental Burst. Guoba's attacks can trigger Elemental Reactions, such as Vaporize or Melt, if an enemy is affected by Hydro or Cryo respectively.

### Elemental Burst: Pyronado

Pyronado is Xiangling's signature ability and the primary source of her immense damage output. Upon activation, Xiangling summons a large spinning Pyro tornado around her that follows her movement. The Pyronado deals Pyro DMG to enemies it passes through and continues to spin for a duration of 10 seconds. The Pyronado has a base duration of 10 seconds and a cooldown of 20 seconds, meaning it can be active for half of the combat time if energy is managed correctly.

A crucial mechanic of Pyronado is **snapshotting**. This means that Pyronado will snapshot the ATK and Pyro DMG Bonus buffs that Xiangling has at the moment of casting her Elemental Burst. Any buffs applied *after* Pyronado has been summoned will not affect its damage. This mechanic is particularly important for maximizing Xiangling's damage, as it allows her to benefit from powerful buffs like Bennett's "Inspiration Field" ATK buff, which is applied when Bennett casts his Elemental Burst. If Xiangling casts Pyronado while standing within Bennett's buffing circle, the Pyronado will deal significantly more damage throughout its duration, as it retains the ATK bonus from Bennett's Burst. Similarly, buffs from artifacts like the 4-piece Noblesse Oblige set, if triggered by another party member, will also be snapshotted if they are active when Pyronado is cast.

The Pyronado's damage scales with Xiangling's ATK and Pyro DMG Bonus. Its damage instances occur rapidly as it spins, making it highly effective for both single-target and AoE situations. The tornado's radius is substantial, allowing it to hit multiple enemies simultaneously.

### Passive Talents

*   **Crispy Thighs:** When a chili-infused dish is cooked, Xiangling has a 12% chance to receive double the product. This is a utility passive with no combat application.
*   **T Ingredient:** When Guoba Attack hits an enemy, it has a 50% chance to grant Xiangling an ATK Bonus of 10% for 10s. This is a minor self-buff that can slightly increase her personal damage.
*   **Serious, Gourmet Shifu:** Increases the DMG dealt by Pyronado by 10%. Additionally, Pyronado's Pyro DMG is converted into **100% Pyro DMG**. This is a significant damage-boosting talent, increasing the Pyronado's overall output and ensuring all its damage is Pyro, which is crucial for reactions like Vaporize.

## Builds and Artifacts

Xiangling's build focuses on maximizing her Elemental Burst damage, as Pyronado is her primary damage source. This typically involves prioritizing ATK, Pyro DMG Bonus, CRIT Rate, and CRIT DMG. Energy Recharge is also a critical stat due to Pyronado's high energy cost.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **The Catch (4-star Polearm):** This F2P weapon, obtainable from the Inazuma Fishing Association, is widely considered Xiangling's BiS. It provides a significant amount of **Energy Recharge** on its secondary stat and its passive increases Elemental Burst DMG by 32% and CRIT Rate against Elemental Bursts by 12%. This combination of ER and Burst DMG/CRIT is perfectly suited for Xiangling's needs.

### Strong Alternative Weapons

*   **Staff of the Scarlet Sands (5-star Polearm):** While a premium 5-star option, its high CRIT Rate substat and passive that converts Elemental Mastery into ATK make it exceptionally strong, especially in reaction-heavy teams.
*   **Staff of Homa (5-star Polearm):** Provides a massive CRIT DMG substat and a strong ATK bonus, with an additional HP bonus that converts to ATK when HP is below 50%. Excellent for raw damage.
*   **Primordial Jade Winged-Spear (5-star Polearm):** Offers CRIT Rate and an ATK-boosting passive that stacks with hits.
*   **Wavebreaker's Fin (4-star Polearm):** Its ATK% substat and passive that scales with the party's maximum Energy Capacity can provide a substantial ATK boost, making it very powerful in teams with high Burst cost characters.
*   **Deathmatch (4-star Battle Pass Polearm):** Offers CRIT Rate and an ATK% bonus that increases based on the number of enemies. A solid choice if The Catch is unavailable.
*   **Kitain Cross Spear (4-star Craftable Polearm):** A strong F2P craftable option that provides **Elemental Mastery** and an effect that regenerates Energy upon hitting an enemy. This can help with ER requirements and provides a decent damage boost, especially in Vaporize teams.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This is Xiangling's definitive best-in-slot artifact set. The 2-piece bonus grants 18% Energy Recharge, and the 4-piece bonus increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of the wearer's Energy Recharge. This set directly addresses Xiangling's high ER needs and amplifies her Pyronado damage significantly. With sufficient ER, the 4-piece bonus can provide a massive damage increase to her Burst.
*   **2-piece Crimson Witch of Flames + 2-piece Emblem of Severed Fate / 2-piece Noblesse Oblige:** A viable alternative if a full Emblem set is difficult to farm. The 2-piece Crimson Witch boosts Pyro DMG, while the other 2-piece set provides ER or Burst DMG.
*   **4-piece Noblesse Oblige:** Can be used if another character in the team is not utilizing it, providing a significant ATK boost to the entire party after casting her Burst. However, this sacrifices personal damage from Emblem.

### Artifact Main Stats and Substats

*   **Sands:** ATK% or Energy Recharge (if ER needs are not met by weapon and substats).
*   **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus.
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio).
*   **Substats:** Energy Recharge (until threshold is met), CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%.

## Energy Recharge Threshold

Xiangling's Elemental Burst, Pyronado, has a high energy cost of 80. To consistently use her Burst every rotation, especially in teams where she is not the primary battery, a substantial amount of Energy Recharge is required.

*   **General Threshold:** **180% - 200% ER** is the typical range for Xiangling when using The Catch and in teams with a strong Pyro battery like Bennett.
*   **Lower Thresholds (160-180%):** Achievable in teams with excellent Pyro particle generation (e.g., Raiden Shogun in Raiden National) or if using a weapon with ER substat and a high ER substat roll on artifacts.
*   **Higher Thresholds (200%+):** May be necessary in teams with less consistent energy generation or when using weapons with ATK% or CRIT substats.

The exact ER requirement can vary based on the team composition, the presence of a Pyro resonance, the number of enemies (more enemies mean more particles generated from defeating them), and the weapon equipped. It is crucial to test her energy regeneration in practice to ensure a smooth rotation.

## Team Compositions

Xiangling's versatility allows her to fit into a wide array of powerful team compositions, primarily excelling in reaction-based teams that can leverage her off-field Pyro application.

### The National Team

This is arguably the most iconic and powerful 4-star team in Genshin Impact, with Xiangling as its core damage dealer.

*   **Core:** Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu
*   **Flex Slot:** Often filled by a Anemo character for VV shred and grouping (e.g., Sucrose, Kazuha) or a strong DPS to enable reactions (e.g., Raiden Shogun, Childe).

**Example Comp:** Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu, Kazuha
**Role of Xiangling:** Primary off-field damage dealer with Pyronado, enabling Vaporize reactions with Xingqiu's Hydro application. Bennett's ATK buff and Pyro Resonance further amplify her damage. Kazuha groups enemies and shreds resistances.

### Childe-Xiangling (International)

This team focuses on enabling Childe to trigger multiple Vaporize reactions with his Hydro application, amplified by Xiangling's Pyronado.

*   **Core:** Childe (Tartaglia), Xiangling, Bennett
*   **Flex Slot:** Typically an Anemo character for grouping and resistance shred (e.g., Kazuha, Venti, Sucrose).

**Example Comp:** Childe, Xiangling, Bennett, Kazuha
**Role of Xiangling:** Childe applies Hydro to enemies, and Xiangling's Pyronado vaporizes these Hydro-infused targets. Bennett provides crucial ATK buffs and healing. Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Hydro/Pyro damage. Childe's Burst can also Vaporize Xiangling's Pyronado hits for massive damage.

### Raiden National

This variation of the National team utilizes the Raiden Shogun to provide consistent Electro application and significantly boost the team's energy regeneration, allowing for more frequent Bursts.

*   **Core:** Raiden Shogun, Xiangling, Bennett
*   **Flex Slot:** Xingqiu or a Hydro applicator.

**Example Comp:** Raiden Shogun, Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu
**Role of Xiangling:** Still the primary off-field Pyro damage dealer. Raiden Shogun's Electro application enables Overload and her Burst helps recharge everyone's energy, making Xiangling's 80-cost Burst much more manageable. Bennett's buff is snapshotted by Pyronado. Xingqiu enables Vaporize.

## Critical Interaction: Bennett's ATK Buff and Pyronado Snapshot

The synergy between Xiangling's Pyronado and Bennett's Elemental Burst ("Inspiration Field") is the cornerstone of her top-tier performance and a critical mechanic to understand for maximizing her damage.

When Bennett casts his Elemental Burst, he creates an Inspiration Field that provides a significant **flat ATK bonus** to all characters within its radius. This bonus is based on Bennett's base ATK (from his weapon and character level) and is not affected by Bennett's artifact stats.

Xiangling's Pyronado has the property of **snapshotting buffs**. This means that when Pyronado is cast, it records the ATK value Xiangling possesses at that exact moment, and all subsequent damage instances from Pyronado will use that snapshotted ATK value.

**The Interaction:**
1.  Bennett casts his Elemental Burst, creating the Inspiration Field.
2.  Xiangling enters the Inspiration Field. Her ATK increases due to Bennett's buff.
3.  Xiangling casts her Elemental Burst (Pyronado) *while still within the Inspiration Field*.
4.  Pyronado snapshots Xiangling's current ATK, which includes the substantial bonus from Bennett's Burst.
5.  As Pyronado spins, it deals damage based on this snapshotted ATK value, even if Xiangling leaves the Inspiration Field or Bennett's Burst duration ends.

This interaction allows Xiangling's Pyronado to deal exceptionally high damage, often surpassing that of many 5-star characters, because it effectively benefits from Bennett's powerful, consistent ATK buff for its entire duration. This is why positioning Xiangling within Bennett's Burst circle *before* casting Pyronado is a fundamental technique for any player utilizing Xiangling in teams with Bennett. The higher Bennett's base ATK (achieved through high-level weapons), the more potent this snapshotting effect becomes. This synergy is so potent that it forms the basis of the "National Team" and its many variations, cementing Xiangling's status as a meta-defining character.
