# Navia

## Lore

Navia is the current President of the Spina di Rosula, a prominent organization in Fontaine responsible for handling various tasks, from bodyguarding to investigation. Her leadership is marked by a profound sense of justice and a deep-seated empathy, traits forged in the crucible of personal tragedy. Navia's father, a respected member of the Spina di Rosula, was falsely accused of treason and subsequently executed. This devastating event plunged Navia into despair, but instead of succumbing to it, she dedicated herself to uncovering the truth behind the accusation.

Her investigation revealed a complex conspiracy involving powerful figures within Fontaine who sought to frame her father to conceal their own illicit activities. This harrowing experience instilled in Navia a fierce determination to protect the innocent and uphold the integrity of her organization. Despite the immense pain she endured, Navia cultivated a warm and generous demeanor, often extending aid to those in need. This outward kindness is not a facade but a genuine expression of her empathy, born from her own suffering and her desire to prevent others from experiencing similar injustices. She believes that true strength lies not in brute force, but in compassion and unwavering resolve.

Navia shares a complex and deeply loyal relationship with Clorinde, a formidable Champion Duelist and one of her closest confidantes. Their bond is characterized by mutual respect and a shared commitment to justice, often working in tandem to resolve difficult cases. Clorinde's unwavering support has been a cornerstone of Navia's leadership, providing a steady presence amidst the political machinations of Fontaine.

Her relationship with Furina, the former Hydro Archon, is also significant. While their interactions were initially strained due to Furina's public persona and Navia's personal vendetta against the system that wronged her family, a deeper understanding and respect eventually developed. Navia recognizes the immense burden Furina carried and the sacrifices she made, leading to a more nuanced appreciation of her character. This evolution in their relationship highlights Navia's capacity for forgiveness and her ability to see beyond initial judgments.

Navia's leadership of the Spina di Rosula is not merely a position of authority but a sacred trust, inherited from her father. She strives to embody the ideals he represented, ensuring that the organization remains a beacon of hope and justice for the people of Fontaine. Her personal quest for truth has transformed her into a formidable leader, respected for her courage, integrity, and unwavering dedication to her cause.

## Kit Mechanics

Navia is a 5-star Geo Polearm user whose gameplay revolves around the **Crystalline Cyst Dust** mechanic and her **Ceremonial Crystallization** Elemental Burst. She functions as a unique Geo DPS that actively leverages the **Crystallize** reaction, a stark departure from typical Geo characters who often focus on defense or direct damage independent of elemental reactions.

### Crystalline Cyst Dust

Navia accumulates **Crystalline Cyst Dust** stacks by picking up Elemental Shards generated by the **Crystallize** reaction. Each Elemental Shard picked up grants Navia one stack of Crystalline Cyst Dust. She can hold a maximum of 6 stacks. The duration of Crystalline Cyst Dust is 15 seconds.

The crucial aspect of this mechanic is that Navia's Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks deal increased damage based on the number of Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks she possesses when casting her Elemental Skill, **As the Sun Sets, So Shall Rise**. Specifically, for each stack of Crystalline Cyst Dust she possesses when using her Elemental Skill, the damage dealt by the summoned **Surging Blade** is increased. This damage bonus scales significantly, with each stack providing a substantial multiplier. The stacks are consumed when her Elemental Skill is used.

### Elemental Skill: As the Sun Sets, So Shall Rise

Navia's Elemental Skill is her primary damage-dealing ability and the core of her kit. Upon activation, she summons a **Surging Blade**, which deals Geo DMG to opponents in front of her. The damage of this Surging Blade is directly amplified by the number of Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks Navia possesses at the time of casting.

Furthermore, if Navia's Elemental Skill hits an opponent, it will trigger a **Cannonball Barrage** after a short delay. This barrage consists of multiple coordinated attacks that deal Geo DMG. The number of Cannonball Barrages fired depends on the number of Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks consumed. For every 1 stack consumed, 1 Cannonball Barrage is fired. This means that with a full 6 stacks, Navia's Elemental Skill can deal a massive amount of burst damage through both the initial Surging Blade and the subsequent Cannonball Barrages.

The cooldown for her Elemental Skill is 10 seconds. The energy cost is 60.

### Elemental Burst: Ceremonial Crystallization

Navia's Elemental Burst is instrumental in generating the Elemental Shards necessary for her Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks. When activated, **Ceremonial Crystallization** calls down a **Salvo of Assured Subduing**, dealing multiple instances of AoE Geo DMG to opponents in front of her over its duration.

A key passive talent, **The More the Merrier**, allows Navia to gain 1 stack of Crystalline Cyst Dust whenever an Elemental Shard created by a Crystallize reaction is picked up. This passive is essential for efficiently building stacks for her Elemental Skill. Additionally, if Navia's Elemental Burst hits an opponent, it generates 2 Elemental Shards of the corresponding element. This self-sufficiency in generating Crystallize shards makes her less reliant on other party members to trigger the reaction.

The duration of her Elemental Burst is 12 seconds, and it has an energy cost of 60. The cooldown is 15 seconds.

### Unique Geo DPS Playstyle

Navia's playstyle is characterized by a cycle of generating Crystallize shards, collecting them to gain Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks, and then unleashing a devastating Elemental Skill amplified by these stacks. This makes her a highly active and engaging DPS. Unlike characters like Itto or Ningguang who primarily focus on their own Geo damage output, Navia's damage is intrinsically tied to the Crystallize reaction. This necessitates a team composition that can consistently apply elements to enemies, allowing Navia to trigger Crystallize and subsequently benefit from the shards.

Her kit encourages a rotation where her Elemental Burst is used to set up shard generation, followed by swapping to other characters to apply elements, then collecting the shards with Navia before unleashing her amplified Elemental Skill. This dynamic gameplay loop, combined with her significant Geo damage output, positions her as a premier Geo DPS.

## Builds

Navia's build prioritizes **CRIT Rate**, **CRIT DMG**, and **ATK%**. Since her damage scales heavily with ATK and the amplification from Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks, maximizing these stats is crucial. Geo DMG Bonus is also important but often comes from her weapon or artifact set.

### Signature Weapon: Verdict

**Verdict** is Navia's signature 5-star Claymore. It provides a substantial base ATK and a secondary stat of **CRIT DMG**. Its unique passive grants ATK% bonuses and, more importantly, increases the DMG dealt by her Elemental Skill based on the combined total ATK of all party members. This synergy makes Verdict her BiS weapon, significantly boosting her overall damage output, especially her Elemental Skill.

### Strong 4-Star Options

*   **The Bell:** While a defensive weapon, its ATK% bonus from its passive can be surprisingly effective for Navia, especially if she needs more survivability. However, its CRIT-focused stats are lacking.
*   **Prototype Archaic:** A craftable 4-star Claymore that offers ATK% as its secondary stat and a passive that deals additional AoE damage. It's a solid F2P option that provides good damage.
*   **Serpent Spine:** A Battle Pass weapon that offers CRIT Rate as its secondary stat and a passive that increases DMG dealt by a significant amount as the character remains on the field. This can be excellent for Navia if she can maintain its stacks.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Nighttime Whispers in the Echoing Woods:** This is Navia's dedicated artifact set and her Best-in-Slot.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** ATK +18%.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** Increases DMG dealt by the equipping character by 8%. After being affected by Crystallize for 0.3s, DMG dealt increases by 30% for 8s. This effect can be triggered once every 10s. This set directly buffs Navia's damage and synergizes perfectly with her reliance on the Crystallize reaction. The 8% DMG bonus is constant, and the 30% bonus after Crystallize is active for a significant portion of her damage window.

*   **4-piece Retracing Bolide:** While often associated with shield-reliant characters, Navia can benefit from this set if she is consistently protected by a strong shield.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** Increases Shield Strength by 20%.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** Increases DMG dealt by the equipping character by 35% when protected by a shield. This set offers a substantial DMG boost, but it requires a consistent shield, which might limit team flexibility.

*   **2-piece ATK% + 2-piece ATK%:** Combining two 2-piece sets that grant ATK% (e.g., 2pc Gladiator's Finale, 2pc Shimenawa's Reminiscence, 2pc Echoes of an Offering) is a viable option if the dedicated set is not available. This provides a straightforward ATK% boost.

*   **2-piece ATK% + 2-piece Geo DMG Bonus:** A mix of 2pc ATK% and 2pc Noblesse Oblige (for Burst DMG) or 2pc Archaic Petra (for Geo DMG) can also be considered.

**Stat Prioritization:**

*   **Sands:** ATK%
*   **Goblet:** Geo DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio)
*   **Substats:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Energy Recharge (ER)

**Energy Recharge:** Navia has a relatively low Burst cost of 60, but she benefits greatly from having her Burst available consistently for shard generation. Aim for approximately **130-140% ER** if she is the sole Geo character and has a battery. If she has another Geo character or a strong ER weapon, this can be lower.

## Team Compositions

Navia's team compositions are designed to maximize the number of **Crystallize** reactions triggered, ensuring she has ample **Crystalline Cyst Dust** stacks for her Elemental Skill. This generally involves characters who can apply elements off-field consistently.

### Maximizing Crystallize Shards

The core principle is to have at least one character applying an element (Hydro, Pyro, Electro, Cryo) consistently to enemies while Navia is on the field or during her Burst. Having two characters from different elements that can react with each other, or with enemies, can further increase shard generation.

**Key Roles in Navia Teams:**

1.  **Navia (Geo DPS):** The primary damage dealer.
2.  **Off-field Elemental Applicator(s):** Characters who can apply elements consistently from off-field to trigger Crystallize.
3.  **Support/Utility:** Characters that provide buffs, healing, shielding, or further elemental application.

**Example Team Compositions:**

*   **Navia, Bennett, Xiangling, Fischl:**
    *   **Bennett:** Provides ATK buffs and healing. His Pyro application can trigger Crystallize.
    *   **Xiangling:** Her Pyronado provides consistent off-field Pyro application, triggering Crystallize and dealing significant damage.
    *   **Fischl:** Her Oz provides consistent off-field Electro application, triggering Crystallize and dealing additional damage.
    *   **Synergy:** This team offers strong Pyro and Electro application, allowing Navia to generate numerous Crystallize shards. Bennett's buff and Xiangling's damage are also substantial.

*   **Navia, Furina, Bennett, Kazuha/Sucrose:**
    *   **Furina:** Her Fanfare stacks and off-field Hydro application are excellent for triggering Crystallize. She also provides a significant DMG buff.
    *   **Bennett:** Buffs ATK and heals.
    *   **Kazuha/Sucrose:** Anemo crowd control and Elemental DMG buffs. Kazuha can also provide Hydro/Pyro/Electro RES shred and an Elemental DMG bonus for the element he swirls.
    *   **Synergy:** This team offers high damage potential through Furina's buffs and Hydro application, combined with Bennett's ATK boost and Anemo support.

*   **Navia, Yelan, Fischl, Zhongli:**
    *   **Yelan:** Provides off-field Hydro application and a DMG buff.
    *   **Fischl:** Provides off-field Electro application.
    *   **Zhongli:** Offers a powerful shield, preventing interruption and providing Geo Resonance if another Geo character is present. His shield also shreds enemy RES.
    *   **Synergy:** This team focuses on consistent Hydro and Electro application for Crystallize, with Zhongli providing crucial survivability and RES shred.

*   **Navia, Raiden Shogun, Xiangling, Bennett:**
    *   **Raiden Shogun:** Her Eye of Stormy Judgment provides Electro application. Her Burst can also contribute damage.
    *   **Xiangling:** Consistent off-field Pyro application.
    *   **Bennett:** ATK buffs and healing.
    *   **Synergy:** This team leverages the strong Pyro and Electro resonance, with Raiden's consistent Electro application and Xiangling's Pyronado creating ample opportunities for Crystallize.

Navia's flexibility allows her to slot into various teams as long as consistent off-field elemental application is present. The key is to ensure enough elemental shards are generated to maximize her Crystalline Cyst Dust stacks before unleashing her Elemental Skill.

## Position in Geo DPS Meta

Navia has firmly established herself as one of the premier Geo DPS characters in Genshin Impact, rivaling and in some contexts surpassing established characters like Arataki Itto. Her unique reliance on the Crystallize reaction, rather than direct Geo constructs or Geo Resonance, allows her to excel in teams that might not traditionally focus on Geo.

While Itto often requires a dedicated Geo team with Gorou for maximum effectiveness, Navia's kit enables her to function optimally in diverse elemental teams. Her ability to generate her own Elemental Shards with her Burst, coupled with the significant damage amplification from Crystalline Cyst Dust, makes her a potent damage dealer even without a full Geo Resonance setup.

Her damage ceiling, particularly with her signature weapon and optimal artifact set, is exceptionally high. The burst damage potential of her Elemental Skill, amplified by 6 stacks of Crystalline Cyst Dust, can rival or exceed the sustained damage of other top-tier DPS characters. This makes her a highly desirable option for players seeking a powerful and engaging Geo damage dealer. Her playstyle is more active and reactive compared to Itto's more straightforward setup-and-smash approach, offering a different but equally effective way to play a Geo DPS.
