# Xilonen

## Lore

Xilonen is a pivotal figure within the Ocelotl tribe of Natlan, renowned for her exceptional craftsmanship and deep connection to her people's history and traditions. As the master craftsperson responsible for forging the legendary Stone Slate, she holds a unique position as a preserver of Natlan's cultural memory. The Stone Slate itself is not merely an artifact but a repository of ancestral knowledge, historical accounts, and the spiritual essence of the Ocelotl, meticulously inscribed and imbued with power by Xilonen's skilled hands. This artifact serves as a tangible link to the past, guiding the present and shaping the future of her tribe.

Her dedication extends beyond the creation of singular artifacts; Xilonen is intrinsically involved in the ongoing cultural preservation of the Ocelotl. This involves not only safeguarding existing traditions but also actively contributing to their evolution and understanding. She is a keeper of stories, a teacher of ancient techniques, and a spiritual guide who ensures that the wisdom of generations is not lost to the passage of time or the conflicts that often plague Natlan. Her role is crucial in maintaining the Ocelotl's identity amidst the volatile landscape of the Pyro Nation, where power struggles and the pursuit of divine authority are constant.

Xilonen's motivations are deeply rooted in her love for her people and her profound respect for their heritage. She understands that memory and culture are the bedrock of any society, providing strength, unity, and purpose. In a nation often characterized by its warrior culture and the pursuit of divine favor through combat, Xilonen represents a different kind of strength – the strength of continuity, wisdom, and collective identity. Her efforts ensure that the Ocelotl are not just a tribe of warriors but a people with a rich history and a vibrant cultural tapestry that deserves to be honored and protected. Her influence is felt not just within her immediate community but also in the broader cultural narrative of Natlan, highlighting the importance of heritage in a land often defined by its martial prowess.

## Kit Mechanics

Xilonen is a 5-star Pyro Polearm user who excels as a dedicated support, primarily focused on amplifying her team's damage through significant Elemental RES Shred and providing consistent Pyro application. Her kit is designed to synergize exceptionally well with Natlan-based characters, particularly those who benefit from her unique buffs and debuffs.

### Elemental Skill: Yohual's Scratch Blade

Xilonen's Elemental Skill, "Yohual's Scratch Blade," is a channeled ability that allows her to perform a series of rapid Pyro attacks while also infusing her Normal Attacks with Pyro. This infusion lasts for a duration and applies a stacking debuff on enemies hit, increasing the Pyro DMG they take. The skill itself deals moderate Pyro DMG, but its primary utility lies in the infusion and the debuff.

*   **Infusion:** While Yohual's Scratch Blade is active, Xilonen's Normal Attacks become infused with Pyro. This infusion overrides any other elemental infusion and allows her to trigger Pyro-related reactions consistently. The duration of the infusion is typically around 10-12 seconds, with a cooldown of approximately 15-18 seconds.
*   **Night Soul:** Enemies hit by Yohual's Scratch Blade or Xilonen's infused Normal Attacks are afflicted with "Night Soul." This debuff increases the Pyro DMG taken by the affected enemy by a percentage that scales with Xilonen's Elemental Mastery and ATK. At higher talent levels, this Pyro DMG bonus can reach significant values, making it a potent damage amplification tool. The debuff stacks up to 3 times, with each stack providing a smaller bonus, culminating in a substantial overall increase. The duration of each stack is typically 8 seconds, refreshing with each hit.

### Elemental Burst: Ocelotl Xochitl

Xilonen's Elemental Burst, "Ocelotl Xochitl," is a powerful AoE Pyro ability that deals significant damage and applies a potent team-wide buff. Upon activation, Xilonen unleashes a burst of Pyro energy, dealing damage in a wide radius. More importantly, this burst grants all party members a substantial increase in their Elemental RES against all elements, and critically, it applies a massive Elemental RES Shred to all enemies within the burst's radius.

*   **AoE Pyro Damage:** The initial activation deals a burst of Pyro damage based on Xilonen's ATK and Talent Level.
*   **Team RES Buff:** All party members gain a temporary boost to their Elemental RES, making them more survivable against elemental attacks. This buff is usually short-lived, lasting around 8-10 seconds.
*   **Enemy RES Shred:** The primary function of the Burst is the significant Elemental RES Shred it applies to all enemies caught in its radius. This shred affects all elements, including Physical DMG, making it incredibly versatile. The percentage of RES Shred is substantial, often in the range of 20-30%, and it directly amplifies the damage dealt by all characters on the team. The duration of this RES Shred is typically tied to the Burst's duration, around 10-12 seconds, with a cooldown of approximately 20 seconds and an energy cost of 80.

The synergy between her Skill and Burst is what makes Xilonen a top-tier support. The continuous Pyro application and debuff from her Skill, combined with the massive team-wide RES Shred from her Burst, create an environment where her teammates can deal significantly more damage. Her kit is particularly effective in teams that rely on elemental reactions or high elemental damage output, as the RES Shred directly boosts these damage sources.

## Builds

Xilonen's build focuses on maximizing her support capabilities, prioritizing stats that enhance her RES Shred and Pyro application. While her personal damage is not her primary focus, certain artifact sets and weapon choices can further amplify her team's offensive potential.

### Artifact Sets

*   **Peak Patrol Song (4-piece):** This artifact set is considered her Best-in-Slot (BiS) due to its powerful synergy with her kit.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** Increases Elemental Mastery by 80.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** After casting an Elemental Skill, increases the wearer's Elemental Mastery by 16 for 10 seconds. This effect can be triggered once every 12 seconds. When the wearer triggers an elemental reaction, the Elemental Mastery bonus is increased by 20 for 10 seconds. This effect can be triggered once every 12 seconds.
    *   **Reasoning:** The 4-piece bonus of Peak Patrol Song significantly boosts Xilonen's Elemental Mastery, which in turn amplifies the "Night Soul" debuff from her Elemental Skill, increasing the Pyro DMG taken by enemies. Furthermore, the set bonus provides a substantial increase to her Elemental Mastery when she triggers reactions, which is common given her Pyro application. This directly translates to higher personal damage for her Skill and a stronger debuff for her team.

*   **4pc Obsidian Codex (4-piece):** A strong alternative that offers a different approach to damage amplification.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** Increases ATK by 18%.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** Upon hitting an opponent with an Elemental Skill, the ATK of all nearby party members is increased by 20% for 12 seconds. This effect can be triggered once every 15 seconds and can occur even when the wearer is not on the field.
    *   **Reasoning:** While not directly boosting her debuff's potency through EM, the 4pc Obsidian Codex provides a significant ATK buff to the entire team. This ATK buff complements her RES Shred from her Burst, leading to a substantial overall increase in team damage. The fact that it can trigger off-field makes it very convenient for her support role.

*   **2pc Obsidian Codex / 2pc Emblem of Severed Fate:** A viable hybrid option.
    *   **2pc Obsidian Codex:** Increases ATK by 18%.
    *   **2pc Emblem of Severed Fate:** Increases Energy Recharge by 20%.
    *   **Reasoning:** This combination offers a balance between increasing her team's ATK and ensuring she has enough Energy Recharge to consistently use her Elemental Burst. The 80-cost Burst is crucial for her RES Shred, so ER is a valuable stat.

### Weapon Choices

*   **Peak Patrol Song (5-star Polearm):** Her signature weapon, offering a significant boost to Elemental Mastery and ATK, further enhancing her debuff and personal damage.
*   **Staff of the Scarlet Sands (5-star Polearm):** Another excellent option, providing a substantial amount of Crit Rate and converting Elemental Mastery into ATK, which benefits both her debuff and her personal damage.
*   **Favonius Lance (4-star Polearm):** A strong F2P option that provides Energy Recharge and generates Elemental Particles on Crit hits, significantly helping with her Burst uptime. The Crit Rate requirement for particle generation means building some Crit Rate on her is beneficial.
*   **Kitain Cross Spear (3-star Polearm):** A decent budget option that provides Elemental Mastery and increases Elemental Skill DMG, with a passive that regenerates Energy.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** Elemental Mastery or ATK% (depending on artifact set and weapon)
*   **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus or Elemental Mastery (if using EM-focused sets/weapons)
*   **Circlet:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (for Favonius Lance users) or Elemental Mastery
*   **Substats:** Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge (aim for ~180-200% ER) > ATK% > Crit Rate/DMG

The goal is to balance her ability to apply Pyro consistently, enhance her debuff's potency through Elemental Mastery, and ensure her Elemental Burst is available as often as possible.

## Role and Team Synergies

Xilonen's primary role is that of an **enabler and amplifier** for Natlan-based team compositions, particularly those that rely on elemental damage and reactions. She provides unparalleled support through her unique combination of Pyro application, enemy debuffing via "Night Soul," and her Elemental Burst's massive team-wide RES Shred.

### Best Support in Natlan Teams

Xilonen is arguably the premier support character for Natlan teams due to her kit's direct synergy with the nation's thematic focus on Pyro and combat. Her ability to shred Elemental RES across all elements makes her a universal damage amplifier for any DPS character, but she particularly shines when paired with characters who have strong Pyro infusion capabilities or benefit from consistent Pyro application for their own reactions.

Her "Night Soul" debuff from her Elemental Skill directly increases Pyro DMG taken by enemies, which is a significant boost for any Pyro DPS or characters who trigger Pyro-related reactions. When combined with the substantial RES Shred from her Elemental Burst, enemies become incredibly vulnerable. This dual debuffing capability allows her teammates to deal significantly more damage without needing to invest as heavily in enemy RES reduction themselves.

### Enabling Natlan Team Compositions

Xilonen's kit is designed to unlock the full potential of Natlan's roster. Many Natlan characters are expected to be Pyro-based or have mechanics that synergize with Pyro. Xilonen's consistent Pyro application ensures that these characters can reliably trigger their elemental abilities and reactions.

*   **RES Shred:** The 20-30% Elemental RES Shred from her Burst is a game-changer. This allows DPS characters to focus more on their offensive stats like Crit Rate, Crit DMG, and ATK, as a significant portion of their damage amplification comes directly from Xilonen. This is especially impactful in endgame content where enemies have high elemental resistances.
*   **Pyro Infusion:** Her Elemental Skill's Pyro infusion allows her to act as a reliable Pyro applicator, enabling reactions like Vaporize, Overload, Burgeon, and Melt for other characters on the team. This is crucial for teams that rely on these reactions for their primary damage output.
*   **Team Buffs:** While her primary focus is debuffing enemies, the temporary Elemental RES buff from her Burst adds a layer of survivability, allowing the team to withstand more damage during the damage-dealing window.

**Example Team Compositions:**

*   **Natlan Pyro DPS + Xilonen + Flex 1 + Flex 2:** This is the core structure. The Flex slots can be filled with other Natlan characters, off-field damage dealers, or characters that provide utility like healing or shielding.
    *   **Example:** A hypothetical Natlan Pyro DPS (e.g., a strong Pyro Sword user) + Xilonen + a Hydro applicator (for Vaporize) + a shielder/healer. Xilonen enables the Pyro DPS to hit harder with her debuffs, while the Hydro applicator enables Vaporize, further amplified by Xilonen's RES Shred.
*   **Mono-Pyro Team:** Xilonen + another Pyro character (e.g., Bennett, Xiangling) + Anemo support (for VV shred) + Flex. In this scenario, Xilonen's RES Shred stacks with other forms of resistance reduction, leading to astronomical damage numbers.
*   **Reaction-Focused Teams:** Xilonen can enable characters who rely on specific reactions. For instance, if a Natlan character has strong Electro application, Xilonen could enable Overload teams, with her RES Shred amplifying the damage of both the Pyro and Electro components.

In essence, Xilonen acts as the linchpin for many Natlan teams, transforming them from competent damage dealers into overwhelming forces. Her ability to provide consistent Pyro, amplify damage through debuffs, and ensure high uptime on her powerful Elemental Burst makes her an indispensable asset for any player looking to maximize their team's offensive potential within the context of Natlan's unique combat and elemental landscape.
