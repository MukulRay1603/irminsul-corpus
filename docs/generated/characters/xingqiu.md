# Xingqiu

## Lore and Background

Xingqiu is the second son of the Feiyun Commerce Guild, a powerful trading organization based in Liyue Harbor. Despite his privileged upbringing and expected role in managing the family business, Xingqiu harbors a deep passion for literature and martial arts. He is a voracious reader, often found engrossed in ancient texts and chivalric tales, which have profoundly shaped his worldview and his aspirations. This scholarly pursuit is not merely a hobby; it reflects his intellectual curiosity and his desire to understand the world beyond the confines of commerce.

His dedication to martial arts is equally significant. Xingqiu practices the Guhua Clan's sword techniques, a discipline known for its elegance and effectiveness. This training complements his scholarly nature, providing him with both physical prowess and a refined sense of discipline. He views his martial arts training not just as a means of self-defense but as a path to self-improvement and a way to embody the ideals of justice and righteousness found in his beloved stories.

A notable aspect of Xingqiu's personal life is his close friendship with Chongyun, the exorcist. Their bond is one of genuine camaraderie, often characterized by playful banter and mutual respect. Xingqiu frequently teases Chongyun, particularly about his supposed "pure Yang" constitution, which Chongyun believes makes him susceptible to demonic influence and requires him to eat popsicles to stay grounded. Despite the teasing, Xingqiu deeply values Chongyun's loyalty and earnestness. Their contrasting personalities – Xingqiu's cunning and scholarly demeanor versus Chongyun's straightforward and earnest nature – create a dynamic and endearing friendship that is a recurring theme in their in-game interactions and story quests. Xingqiu's actions, such as subtly guiding Chongyun or intervening in situations where Chongyun's earnestness might lead him astray, demonstrate his protective and caring nature towards his friend.

Xingqiu's dual life as a budding businessman and a devoted scholar-martial artist often leads him to sneak away from his responsibilities, much to the chagrin of his father. However, his father, while outwardly stern, seems to recognize and perhaps even tacitly approve of Xingqiu's independent spirit and his pursuit of knowledge and self-discipline, understanding that these qualities will ultimately make him a capable leader. Xingqiu's character embodies a blend of tradition and individuality, a young man striving to forge his own path while honoring his heritage.

## Kit Mechanics

Xingqiu's primary utility stems from his Elemental Skill, **Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen**, and his Elemental Burst, **Guhua Sword: Raincutter**. These abilities work in tandem to provide consistent off-field Hydro application, significant damage reduction, and minor healing, making him one of the most valuable 4-star characters in the game.

### Elemental Skill: Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen

When activated, Xingqiu conjures two **Rain Swords** that orbit the active character. These Rain Swords provide several benefits:

*   **Damage Reduction:** Each Rain Sword reduces the damage taken by the active character from enemy attacks and environmental damage. The damage reduction is calculated as 24% per sword, for a total of 48% when both swords are active. This significant reduction makes characters much more survivable, especially those with low HP or who are prone to taking damage.
*   **Healing:** When the Rain Swords' duration ends or they are shattered (by taking too much damage), they heal the active character for 6% of Xingqiu's Max HP each. While this healing is not substantial enough to replace a dedicated healer, it provides passive sustain over time.
*   **Hydro Application:** The Rain Swords are destroyed when the active character takes damage. Upon destruction, they perform a **Hydro Infusion** on the enemy. This application is crucial for enabling Hydro-based reactions.

The cooldown for Guhua Sword: Fatal Rainscreen is 8 seconds, and its duration is 15 seconds. This long duration relative to its cooldown allows for near-constant uptime of the Rain Swords when managed correctly, especially when paired with his Elemental Burst.

### Elemental Burst: Guhua Sword: Raincutter

Xingqiu's Elemental Burst is the cornerstone of his off-field Hydro application. Upon activation, he deals **Hydro DMG** with his sword and creates the **maximum number of Rain Swords** (three). While this initial hit applies Hydro, the true power lies in the subsequent effects:

*   **Sword Rain Attacks:** While Guhua Sword: Raincutter is active, the active character's Normal Attacks will trigger consecutive sword rain attacks. These attacks deal **Hydro DMG** and apply **Hydro** to enemies.
*   **Increased Rain Sword Count:** The Burst increases the number of Rain Swords orbiting the active character to three, providing the maximum damage reduction (48%) and healing potential.
*   **Hydro Application ICD:** The sword rain attacks from the Burst apply Hydro with an internal cooldown (ICD). This means Hydro is applied every 2.5 seconds or on every third hit of the sword rain, whichever comes first. This ICD is critical for understanding reaction consistency. For example, with a fast-hitting character like Hu Tao or Yoimiya, the Hydro application will trigger on nearly every other attack, ensuring consistent Vaporize reactions.

The Elemental Burst has a cost of 80 Energy and a duration of 18 seconds. The cooldown is 20 seconds. This 2-second gap between the Burst's end and its potential reactivation necessitates significant Energy Recharge to ensure consistent uptime.

### Synergy and Role

Xingqiu's kit is designed for off-field support. His ability to consistently apply Hydro through Normal Attacks makes him an indispensable enabler for numerous elemental reactions, most notably Vaporize and Freeze. The damage reduction provided by his Rain Swords significantly enhances team survivability, reducing the need for dedicated healers in many scenarios. His passive healing further contributes to sustain.

His reliance on Normal Attacks for Hydro application means he pairs exceptionally well with characters who deal rapid Normal Attacks, such as Hu Tao, Yoimiya, Ayato, and even certain builds of Diluc or Keqing. The consistent Hydro application from his Burst allows these characters to reliably trigger Vaporize, doubling their Pyro damage.

## Builds and Artifacts

Xingqiu's build focuses on maximizing his Hydro damage, Energy Recharge, and Crit stats to enhance the effectiveness of his Rain Sword attacks and Burst.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **Sacrificial Sword (5-star):** This is widely considered Xingqiu's BiS weapon, especially at R1 or higher. Its secondary stat provides Energy Recharge, which is crucial for Xingqiu's high Burst cost. More importantly, its passive ability has a chance to reset the cooldown of his Elemental Skill upon hitting an enemy with it. This allows Xingqiu to use his Skill twice in quick succession, generating significantly more particles, regaining energy faster, and applying more Hydro through his Skill's Rain Swords. This double-E setup drastically reduces his Energy Recharge requirements and increases his personal damage output.

### Strong Alternatives

*   **Primordial Jade Cutter (5-star):** Offers a substantial Crit Rate bonus and a significant ATK boost based on his Max HP. While it doesn't help with Energy Recharge, it greatly boosts his personal damage if energy needs are met through other means.
*   **Mistsplitter Reforged (5-star):** Provides Crit DMG and Elemental DMG Bonus. Its passive can be difficult to fully utilize for Xingqiu due to his off-field nature, but the raw stats are still very strong.
*   **Favonius Sword (4-star):** An excellent F2P option. It provides Energy Recharge as a secondary stat, and its passive generates Elemental Particles for the team when the wielder scores a CRIT hit. This significantly aids team-wide energy needs, often allowing Xingqiu to reach comfortable uptime even with lower personal ER. However, it requires Xingqiu to have a decent Crit Rate (around 60-70%) to reliably trigger its passive.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This is Xingqiu's premier artifact set.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** Energy Recharge +20%.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of Energy Recharge. A maximum of 200% ER can provide a 50% Burst DMG Bonus. This set perfectly synergizes with Xingqiu's high ER requirements and his Burst-focused kit, significantly boosting his damage output.
*   **2-piece Noblesse Oblige / 2-piece Heart of Depth / 2-piece Tenacity of the Millelith:** A combination of these sets can be used if 4pc Emblem is unavailable. 2pc Noblesse boosts Burst DMG by 20%, 2pc Heart of Depth boosts Hydro DMG by 15%, and 2pc Tenacity boosts HP by 20% (benefiting his Rain Sword healing and damage reduction if HP scaling is considered).

### Artifact Main Stats

*   **Sands:** Energy Recharge or ATK%. ER is generally preferred unless Sacrificial Sword is used and energy needs are met by other team members or substats. ATK% is viable if ER requirements are comfortably met (e.g., 180-200% ER with Sacrificial Sword).
*   **Goblet:** Hydro DMG Bonus.
*   **Circlet:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (depending on what is needed to balance the ratio).

### Artifact Substats Priority

1.  Energy Recharge (until threshold is met)
2.  Crit Rate / Crit DMG
3.  ATK%
4.  HP% (minor benefit for healing and damage reduction)

### Energy Recharge Threshold

Xingqiu's Elemental Burst costs 80 Energy and has a 20-second cooldown. To ensure near-constant uptime on his Burst, a significant amount of Energy Recharge is required.

*   **With Sacrificial Sword:** Approximately **180-200% ER** is generally sufficient, especially if the team generates particles effectively or if the double-E passive is consistently triggered.
*   **Without Sacrificial Sword (e.g., Favonius Sword, other weapons):** The requirement increases significantly, often needing **200-230% ER** or even higher, depending on the team composition and particle generation. Using Favonius Sword can lower this personal ER requirement by providing team-wide energy.

## Team Compositions

Xingqiu's versatility allows him to fit into a wide array of team compositions, primarily those that benefit from consistent off-field Hydro application and damage reduction.

### National Team Variations

The "National Team" is a highly popular and effective F2P-friendly team composition that often includes Xingqiu.

*   **Core:** Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu.
*   **Fourth Slot:** Can be Sucrose (for VV shred and grouping), Kazuha (for VV shred, grouping, and DMG buff), Raiden Shogun (for Electro-Charged synergy and Burst uptime), or Chongyun (for Cryo infusion and Burst synergy).
*   **Role:** Xingqiu enables Xiangling's Pyronado to consistently Vaporize, significantly boosting the team's damage output. His damage reduction also helps the team survive while Xiangling and the fourth member deal damage.

### Hu Tao Teams

Xingqiu is almost mandatory for most Hu Tao teams due to his ability to enable consistent Vaporize reactions.

*   **Typical Comp:** Hu Tao, Xingqiu, Yelan, Zhongli.
    *   **Hu Tao:** Main DPS, triggers Vaporize.
    *   **Xingqiu:** Off-field Hydro applicator, damage reduction, minor healing.
    *   **Yelan:** Off-field Hydro applicator, DMG buff (via A4 passive), and personal damage. The Hydro resonance also boosts HP for both Hu Tao and Yelan.
    *   **Zhongli:** Provides a strong shield, universal RES shred, and interruption resistance, allowing Hu Tao to attack uninterrupted.
*   **Alternative:** If Yelan is unavailable, other characters like Kazuha or Sucrose can be used for grouping and elemental damage buffs, or a shielder like Layla or Diona for Freeze/Shatter variations.

### Other Pyro Vaporize Teams

Xingqiu is the premier Hydro enabler for any team aiming to consistently Vaporize Pyro damage.

*   **Diluc:** Diluc's fast Normal Attacks combined with Xingqiu's Burst allow for consistent Vaporize procs, making them a classic pairing.
*   **Yoimiya:** Yoimiya's Normal Attacks during her Skill duration benefit greatly from Xingqiu's Hydro application, enabling reliable Vaporize.
*   **Klee:** While Klee's attacks are slower and more AoE-focused, Xingqiu can still enable Vaporize for her charged attacks and Normal Attacks.
*   **General Structure:** Typically involves a Pyro DPS, Xingqiu, an Anemo unit (Kazuha, Sucrose) for VV shred and grouping, and potentially a shielder or healer for survivability.

### Freeze Teams

While less common than Vaporize teams, Xingqiu can also function in Freeze compositions.

*   **Core:** Cryo DPS (e.g., Ayaka, Ganyu), Xingqiu, Anemo unit (Kazuha, Venti), and a second Cryo unit or healer.
*   **Role:** Xingqiu's Hydro application, combined with Cryo application, can maintain the Frozen status, especially when paired with an Anemo unit that can swirl both elements. However, characters like Mona or Kokomi often offer superior Hydro application for Freeze due to their wider AoE or faster application rates.

## Why Xingqiu Remains Meta

Despite being a 4-star character released early in Genshin Impact's lifecycle, Xingqiu consistently remains one of the most powerful and meta-defining characters in the game. His enduring relevance stems from several key factors:

*   **Unmatched Off-Field Hydro Application:** Xingqiu's Elemental Burst provides consistent, reliable off-field Hydro application through Normal Attacks. This level of sustained Hydro application is difficult to replicate, even with some 5-star characters. This makes him the premier enabler for Vaporize, the highest damage-multiplication reaction in the game.
*   **Synergy with Vaporize:** Vaporize is a reaction that multiplies the damage of the triggering Pyro attack by 1.5x (when Pyro triggers on Hydro) or 2x (when Hydro triggers on Pyro). Xingqiu's Hydro application allows Pyro DPS characters like Hu Tao, Xiangling, Diluc, and Yoimiya to reliably trigger the 2x multiplier, leading to massive damage spikes.
*   **Damage Reduction and Survivability:** The 48% damage reduction from his Rain Swords is incredibly potent. It significantly increases the survivability of the entire team, allowing DPS characters to take more hits, reducing the reliance on dedicated healers, and enabling more aggressive playstyles. This utility is invaluable in challenging content like the Spiral Abyss.
*   **Low Investment, High Return:** Xingqiu's kit scales well with investment, but he provides significant utility even at lower Constellation levels (C0-C2). His C2, in particular, further enhances his Burst's duration and Hydro application, making him even more potent. His damage scales with ATK, Crit, and Hydro DMG, but his primary role as an enabler and buffer means his utility remains high even if his personal damage isn't fully optimized.
*   **Energy Recharge Solutions:** While his Burst has a high cost, the widespread availability of weapons like Sacrificial Sword and Favonius Sword, along with artifact sets like Emblem of Severed Fate, provides clear and achievable pathways to meet his Energy Recharge needs. The Sacrificial Sword's double-E passive is particularly game-changing, drastically lowering ER requirements and boosting his offensive capabilities.
*   **Versatility:** Xingqiu fits into a vast number of team archetypes across multiple elements. He is a staple in Pyro Vaporize teams, can function in Freeze teams, and is a key component of many powerful synergistic compositions like the National Team. This adaptability ensures his relevance as new characters and teams are introduced.
*   **F2P Accessibility:** As a 4-star character, Xingqiu is more accessible to all players compared to 5-star units. This widespread availability means a large portion of the player base can access his powerful kit, contributing to his perceived meta status.

In essence, Xingqiu provides a unique and powerful combination of off-field Hydro application, significant damage reduction, and team-wide utility that remains highly sought after. His kit is fundamental to enabling some of the game's strongest offensive strategies, particularly those centered around the Vaporize reaction, solidifying his position as a cornerstone of many top-tier teams for years to come.
