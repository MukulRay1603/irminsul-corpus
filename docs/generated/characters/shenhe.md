# Shenhe

## Lore and Background

Shenhe's past is one of profound trauma and isolation, shaping her into the reserved and emotionally distant individual she is today. As a young child, she was abandoned by her parents and subsequently taken in by the adeptus Cloud Retainer. This upbringing, while providing her with physical protection and training in the ways of the adepti, was devoid of genuine emotional connection. Cloud Retainer's tutelage focused on rigorous discipline and the suppression of human emotions, which Shenhe internalized to an extreme degree. This emotional suppression was a survival mechanism, a way to cope with the lingering pain of her abandonment and the harsh realities of her upbringing.

Her time with Cloud Retainer instilled in her a deep understanding of adeptal arts and combat prowess, but it also left her with a significant emotional void. The adepti, by their nature, are detached from mortal concerns, and Cloud Retainer's methods, while effective for survival, were not conducive to fostering healthy emotional development. Shenhe's inability to process her feelings led to a dangerous accumulation of negative emotions, manifesting as a dark aura that threatened to consume her. It was only through the intervention of other adepti, particularly Mountain Shaper and Moon Carver, that she was guided towards a path of emotional recovery.

This recovery is a slow and ongoing process. Shenhe struggles to connect with others, often misinterpreting social cues or expressing herself in ways that appear cold or uncaring. Her experiences have left her with a deep-seated fear of hurting those she cares about, leading her to maintain a deliberate distance. Despite this, she possesses a strong sense of justice and a protective instinct, particularly towards those who are vulnerable or suffering. Her connection to Xiao, the Vigilant Yaksha, is a notable example of this. Both share the burden of immense suffering and isolation, and they find a quiet understanding in each other's presence, a shared acknowledgment of their unique burdens.

Shenhe's relationship with the Liyue adepti is complex. While they rescued her and provided her with a means of survival, her upbringing was not one of warmth. She respects their power and their role in protecting Liyue, but she also carries the scars of their detached tutelage. Her eventual integration into the mortal world, albeit as a protector and advisor, represents a significant step in her journey towards healing and finding a place for herself, bridging the gap between the ethereal world of the adepti and the bustling human society of Liyue. Her presence serves as a reminder of the adepti's long history and their enduring, albeit often subtle, influence on the mortal realm.

## Combat Mechanics and Kit

Shenhe is a 5-star Cryo Polearm user designed primarily as a dedicated Cryo damage buffer. Her kit revolves around her unique **Icy Quill** ability, which grants a flat damage bonus to her allies' attacks.

### Elemental Skill: Spring Spirit Summoning

Shenhe's Elemental Skill, **Spring Spirit Summoning**, has two distinct activation modes:

*   **Press:** Shenhe dashes forward, dealing Cryo DMG to enemies in her path.
*   **Hold:** Shenhe commands the surrounding ice to attack, dealing Cryo DMG in a larger AoE.

Crucially, after using **Spring Spirit Summoning**, Shenhe grants all nearby party members the **Icy Quill** effect. This effect adds a flat amount of Cryo DMG to attacks that deal Cryo DMG. The amount of bonus DMG added scales with Shenhe's **ATK**. The **Icy Quill** effect has a limited number of **Quills** per character. Each instance of damage that triggers the **Icy Quill** consumes one stack.

The number of **Quills** granted depends on Shenhe's Ascension Passives:
*   **Ascension 1 Passive (Clarifying Winds):** Grants 5 **Quills** to active characters.
*   **Ascension 4 Passive (Concentrated Blizzards):** Grants an additional 5 **Quills** if **Spring Spirit Summoning** is activated by holding.

The flat damage bonus from **Icy Quill** is calculated as follows:
`Flat DMG Bonus = (Shenhe's ATK * A) + B`
Where `A` and `B` are scaling values based on Shenhe's Skill Level.

**Example:** At Talent Level 10, the flat damage bonus per Quill is `227.37% ATK + 197.07`. This bonus is applied to attacks that deal Cryo DMG. Importantly, this bonus damage is considered separate from the base damage of the attack and can critically hit if the triggering attack critically hits. However, the **Icy Quill** bonus itself does not have its own CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG.

The **Icy Quill** effect lasts for 15 seconds, or until all **Quills** are consumed. This duration is generally long enough to cover the damage windows of most main DPS characters.

### Elemental Burst: Divine Maiden's Deliverance

Shenhe's Elemental Burst, **Divine Maiden's Deliverance**, unleashes a **Skyfrost Mantle** in an AoE, dealing Cryo DMG. It also applies a **Cryo** status to enemies hit.

The **Skyfrost Mantle** has the following effects:
1.  **Damage:** Deals Cryo DMG at the moment of activation and periodically thereafter.
2.  **Cryo RES Shred:** Decreases the **Cryo RES** of enemies within the AoE by 20% for 10 seconds.
3.  **Normal Attack DMG Buff:** Increases the **Normal Attack DMG** of active characters within the AoE by 15% for 10 seconds.

The **Cryo RES Shred** is a significant debuff that directly amplifies the damage of Cryo-focused teams. The **Normal Attack DMG Buff** is less impactful for most Cryo DPS characters who rely heavily on Charged Attacks (like Ganyu) or Elemental Skills/Bursts (like Ayaka).

### Energy Recharge Requirements

Shenhe's Elemental Burst has an energy cost of 80. Due to its importance for her debuff and buff, achieving sufficient **Energy Recharge (ER)** is crucial for maintaining Burst uptime. The required ER depends heavily on team composition and the presence of other Cryo characters or Favonius weapons.

*   **Solo Cryo:** Without other Cryo characters or Favonius weapons, Shenhe might need upwards of 200-220% ER.
*   **With another Cryo (e.g., Rosaria, Diona):** ER requirements can drop to around 180-200%.
*   **With Favonius Lance:** This can significantly reduce ER needs, potentially bringing them down to 150-170% depending on crit rate and team setup.

### Pure Cryo Team Buffer

Shenhe's kit is almost exclusively designed to buff Cryo damage. The **Icy Quill** effect only applies to attacks that deal Cryo DMG. Her Burst's **Cryo RES Shred** also specifically targets Cryo damage. While her Burst provides a general Normal Attack DMG buff, its primary utility in a Cryo team comes from the RES shred. This makes her a highly specialized buffer, excelling in teams where all primary damage sources are Cryo. She does not offer significant buffs to other elements, making her less versatile than general buffers like Bennett or Kazuha in multi-elemental teams.

## Builds

Shenhe's build focuses on maximizing her ATK stat to amplify the flat damage bonus from **Icy Quill**, while also ensuring sufficient Energy Recharge for Burst uptime.

### Weapons

*   **Calamity Queller (5-Star Signature):** This is Shenhe's best-in-slot weapon. It provides a high base ATK and a significant ATK% substat. Its passive further increases ATK after using an Elemental Skill and grants an Elemental DMG Bonus when off-field, synergizing perfectly with her role.
*   **Engulfing Lightning (5-Star):** While a strong ATK weapon with ER substat, its passive is more geared towards the wielder being the primary damage dealer, making it less optimal than Calamity Queller but still a powerful option if available.
*   **Staff of Homa (5-Star):** Offers a large CRIT DMG substat and HP scaling for ATK, which can be beneficial, but its passive is less synergistic with Shenhe's off-field buffing role compared to Calamity Queller.
*   **Skyward Spine (5-Star):** Provides a good base ATK and ER substat, making it a solid option for meeting ER requirements while boosting ATK.
*   **Favonius Lance (4-Star):** This is an excellent **Free-to-Play (F2P)** and general utility option. It provides ER% substat, crucial for Burst uptime, and its passive generates Elemental Particles on critical hits, further aiding team energy needs. It requires a decent CRIT Rate to proc its passive effectively.
*   **Deathmatch (4-Star Battle Pass):** Offers CRIT Rate substat and an ATK% passive, which can be useful if energy needs are met through other means.
*   **Kitain Cross Spear (4-Star Craftable):** Provides Elemental Mastery and ATK%, with a passive that generates energy. While not ideal for her ATK scaling, it can be a budget option if ER is a major concern.

### Artifacts

*   **4-Piece Noblesse Oblige:** This is generally her best-in-slot artifact set. The 2-piece bonus provides 20% Elemental Burst DMG, and the 4-piece bonus increases all party members' ATK by 20% for 12 seconds after using her Elemental Burst. This ATK buff stacks multiplicatively with other ATK buffs and complements her own ATK-scaling abilities.
*   **2-Piece Gladiator's Finale / Shimenawa's Reminiscence + 2-Piece Noblesse Oblige / Tenacity of the Millelith:** A good alternative if a full 4-piece Noblesse set is difficult to farm or if focusing purely on her personal ATK. The 2-piece ATK% bonuses are strong.
*   **2-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith + 2-Piece Noblesse Oblige:** Provides HP% and Burst DMG, with the Tenacity set offering a slight ATK buff to the team if her Elemental Skill hits an enemy. Less optimal than pure ATK focus.

### Artifact Main Stats

*   **Sands:** ATK%
*   **Goblet:** ATK%
*   **Circlet:** ATK%

While a Cryo DMG Bonus goblet might seem tempting for her Burst damage, Shenhe's primary role is buffing, and her own damage contributes less than the amplified damage of her teammates. Maximizing her ATK stat is paramount for the **Icy Quill** bonus.

### Artifact Substats Priority

1.  **Energy Recharge:** Until the required threshold is met (typically 150-170% with Favonius Lance, potentially higher without).
2.  **ATK%:** The most important stat for maximizing her buff.
3.  **Flat ATK:** Also contributes to her buff.
4.  **CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG:** For her personal damage, especially if using Favonius Lance to ensure procs.

## Team Compositions

Shenhe is a highly specialized buffer, making her most effective in teams built around maximizing Cryo damage. Her presence is almost mandatory for achieving the highest damage ceilings in top-tier Cryo DPS teams.

### Ganyu Teams

Shenhe is a cornerstone of Melt and Freeze Ganyu teams, significantly boosting her Charged Attack damage.

*   **Melt Ganyu:**
    *   **Ganyu (Main DPS):** Focuses on Charged Shots.
    *   **Shenhe (Buffer):** Provides Icy Quill and Burst debuffs.
    *   **Bennett (Support):** Provides massive ATK buff and healing.
    *   **Xiangling / Kazuha / Zhongli:** Xiangling for Pyro resonance and off-field damage, Kazuha for VV shred and grouping, or Zhongli for shield and RES shred.
    *   **Role:** Shenhe's Icy Quill adds substantial flat damage to Ganyu's Cryo Charged Shots, which then benefit from Melt. Her Burst's RES shred further amplifies Ganyu's damage.

*   **Freeze Ganyu:**
    *   **Ganyu (Main DPS):** Focuses on Charged Shots.
    *   **Shenhe (Buffer):** Provides Icy Quill and Burst debuffs.
    *   **Mona / Kokomi (Hydro Applicator/Support):** Provides Hydro for freezing and Omen debuff (Mona) or healing/Hydro (Kokomi).
    *   **Kazuha / Venti (Crowd Control/VV Shred):** Groups enemies and shreds Cryo RES with Viridescent Venerer.
    *   **Role:** Shenhe's buffs are crucial for Ganyu's damage output in a Freeze scenario where Melt is not triggered. The Icy Quill adds significant damage to every Cryo hit.

### Ayaka Teams

Shenhe is indispensable for optimizing Ayaka's damage, particularly her Elemental Burst.

*   **Standard Freeze Ayaka:**
    *   **Kamisato Ayaka (Main DPS):** Focuses on her Elemental Burst.
    *   **Shenhe (Buffer):** Provides Icy Quill and Burst debuffs.
    *   **Kazuha / Venti (Crowd Control/VV Shred):** Groups enemies and shreds Cryo RES.
    *   **Kokomi / Mona / Diona (Hydro Applicator/Healer/Shielder):** Provides Hydro for freezing, healing, or shielding. Kokomi is preferred for consistent Hydro application and healing. Diona offers a shield, healing, and Cryo resonance.
    *   **Role:** Shenhe's **Icy Quill** adds flat damage to every hit of Ayaka's Elemental Burst, and her Burst's Cryo RES shred significantly increases the overall damage output of Ayaka's Burst. The Normal Attack buff from her Burst is less relevant for Ayaka's primary damage source but still contributes.

### Mono-Cryo Teams

Shenhe excels in teams that forgo other elements to maximize Cryo application and buffs.

*   **Mono-Cryo:**
    *   **Cryo DPS (e.g., Ayaka, Ganyu, Wriothesley):**
    *   **Shenhe (Buffer):**
    *   **Kazuha (Support):** Provides grouping, Elemental DMG bonus, and VV shred (if applicable, though less crucial in Mono-Cryo).
    *   **Rosaria / Diona / Layla (Cryo Battery/Support):** Provides additional Cryo particles for Shenhe, Cryo resonance, and utility like shielding or healing. Rosaria can also provide CRIT Rate buffs.
    *   **Role:** In these teams, Shenhe's buffs are amplified by Cryo resonance and Kazuha's buffs, leading to extremely high Cryo damage outputs. The lack of elemental diversity means Shenhe's specialized buffs are utilized to their fullest potential.

### Unique Flat Damage Bonus Mechanic

Shenhe's **Icy Quill** mechanic is unique in Genshin Impact. Unlike most buffs that provide percentage increases to ATK, DMG%, or CRIT stats, **Icy Quill** adds a **flat amount of Cryo DMG** to attacks. This flat damage bonus scales directly with Shenhe's ATK.

**Key aspects of Icy Quill:**

*   **Scales with ATK:** Higher ATK on Shenhe means a larger flat damage bonus.
*   **Applied to Cryo Hits:** Only attacks that deal Cryo DMG trigger the **Icy Quill** bonus.
*   **Consumes Quills:** Each instance of damage consuming one stack. Multi-hit attacks consume multiple stacks if they have enough.
*   **Separate Damage Instance:** The bonus damage is calculated separately and benefits from CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG of the triggering attack.
*   **Additive with Other Buffs:** The flat damage is added to the base damage calculation and then benefits from multipliers like CRIT, DMG%, and RES shred.
*   **Team Amplification:** It significantly boosts the damage of characters who have many fast-hitting attacks (like Ayaka's Burst) or high base multipliers on single hits (like Ganyu's Charged Shots).

This mechanic makes Shenhe exceptionally powerful in Cryo-centric teams, allowing her to dramatically increase the damage output of characters like Ayaka and Ganyu by adding a substantial chunk of damage to every single hit. The effectiveness of **Icy Quill** is particularly pronounced against enemies with high DEF or when combined with other damage-boosting effects.
