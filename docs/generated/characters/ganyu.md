# Ganyu

## Lore and Background

Ganyu is a half-human, half-qilin Adeptus who has served as the general secretary of the Liyue Qixing for approximately 3,000 years. Her unique heritage, a blend of mortal and divine, has shaped her existence, leading to an ongoing internal struggle between her human and Adeptus natures. This duality manifests in her personality: she possesses a deep sense of duty and responsibility towards Liyue, often working tirelessly to the point of exhaustion, yet she also yearns for a simpler life, a desire stemming from her human side. Her qilin heritage grants her immense power and longevity, but also a sense of detachment from the fleeting lives of mortals, a perspective she constantly grapples with.

Her long tenure with the Qixing has made her an indispensable figure in Liyue's administration. She meticulously manages countless documents, handles complex negotiations, and ensures the smooth functioning of the entire region. Despite her immense power and divine lineage, she chooses to remain in the mortal realm, dedicating her existence to the prosperity and safety of Liyue. This commitment is driven by a profound love for the land and its people, a sentiment that has grown over millennia of observation and interaction.

Ganyu shares a complex relationship with other Adepti. She is particularly close to Cloud Retainer, another powerful Adeptus known for her technological prowess and somewhat eccentric personality. Cloud Retainer often expresses concern for Ganyu's well-being, urging her to rest and find balance. Ganyu also has a connection with Xiao, the Yaksha, another Adeptus who guards Liyue. Their relationship is one of mutual respect and understanding, forged through shared experiences of protecting the mortal realm. Both carry the weight of their long lives and the burdens of their duties, finding solace in each other's quiet companionship.

The Lantern Rite festival holds particular emotional significance for Ganyu. During these events, the memories of her past, her dual nature, and her place in the world are brought to the forefront. She often reflects on the passage of time, the lives she has witnessed, and the sacrifices made by both mortals and Adepti. Her experiences during the Lantern Rite often highlight her internal conflict, as she navigates the joys of the festival alongside the melancholic realization of her own unique and often solitary existence. This emotional arc underscores her journey of self-discovery and acceptance, as she strives to reconcile her divine heritage with her human heart.

## Combat Kit Mechanics

Ganyu's combat effectiveness revolves around her powerful Cryo application and devastating Charged Attacks, amplified by her Elemental Skill and Burst.

### Elemental Skill: Trail of the Qilin

Trail of the Qilin is Ganyu's Elemental Skill, which allows her to dash backward a significant distance, leaving behind an Ice Lotus. This Ice Lotus taunts enemies within a certain radius, drawing their aggro. At the end of its duration, or if destroyed, the Ice Lotus explodes, dealing Cryo DMG to all enemies within its AoE. The damage scales with Ganyu's ATK and has a cooldown of 10 seconds. This skill serves multiple purposes: it provides a crucial escape mechanism, allowing Ganyu to reposition and create distance for her Charged Attacks, and it acts as a potent source of off-field Cryo application and AoE damage, setting up enemy groups for her subsequent attacks. The taunt effect is particularly useful for grouping enemies and ensuring her Frostflake Arrows hit their targets effectively.

### Elemental Burst: Celestial Shower

Celestial Shower is Ganyu's Elemental Burst, an ability that summons a Sacred Cryo Pearl, which continuously rains down shards of ice over a large AoE. These ice shards deal Cryo DMG to enemies caught in the area. The Burst has a duration of 15 seconds and a cooldown of 20 seconds, with an energy cost of 60. Celestial Shower provides sustained off-field Cryo application and significant AoE damage, making it an excellent tool for setting up reactions like Freeze and Melt, or for simply chipping away at enemy HP while Ganyu repositions or prepares her Charged Attacks. The sheer number of ice shards can overwhelm groups of enemies, especially when combined with crowd-controlling abilities from other characters. The ICD (Internal Cooldown) on the ice shards means they don't trigger elemental application on every single hit, but the overall rate is sufficient for consistent Cryo application.

### Normal Attack: Flourish of the Glazed Moon

Ganyu's Normal Attack, Flourish of the Glazed Moon, consists of up to six consecutive shots. However, her true damage potential lies in her **Charged Attack**. When Ganyu holds her Normal Attack, she can charge it to fire an **Frostflake Arrow**. Upon hitting an enemy, the Frostflake Arrow blooms, dealing Cryo DMG in a small AoE. The **key mechanic** is the **Level 2 Charged Arrow**, which unlocks after a short charge time. This arrow deals significantly increased Cryo DMG and, crucially, **always critically hits** any enemy affected by Cryo. This guaranteed crit on the bloom damage is a cornerstone of Ganyu's damage output, allowing players to focus on other stats like ATK, Cryo DMG Bonus, and CRIT DMG. The bloom effect also has a secondary AoE damage component that applies Cryo.

## Character Builds

Ganyu's build focuses on maximizing her ATK, CRIT DMG, and Cryo DMG Bonus, with CRIT Rate being less of a priority due to her Level 2 Charged Arrow's guaranteed crit.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **Amos' Bow:** This 5-star bow is Ganyu's undisputed BiS. Its passive grants a significant ATK% bonus and further increases Normal and Charged Attack DMG based on the arrow's flight distance. At higher refinement levels, the damage bonus from distance becomes enormous, synergizing perfectly with Ganyu's playstyle of attacking from afar. The ATK% substat also directly boosts her overall damage.

### Strong Free-to-Play (F2P) Options

*   **Prototype Crescent:** This 4-star craftable bow is an excellent F2P option. Its passive grants increased Movement SPD and ATK% when hitting enemies with weak points. If the enemy has a weak point, the ATK bonus is substantial, and it also provides an ATK% substat. This bow requires players to aim for weak points to maximize its potential, but the payoff is significant.
*   **Hamayumi:** Another craftable 4-star bow, Hamayumi provides a high base ATK and an ATK% substat. Its passive increases Normal and Charged Attack DMG, with a further bonus after using an Elemental Skill. This bow is a solid choice if Prototype Crescent's weak point mechanic is difficult to manage, offering consistent damage increases.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Blizzard Strayer:** This is Ganyu's premier artifact set, especially in Freeze compositions. It provides a 15% Cryo DMG Bonus from the 2-piece effect. The 4-piece effect grants a massive 20% CRIT Rate when attacking an enemy affected by Cryo, and an additional 20% CRIT Rate when the enemy is Frozen. This set effectively provides up to 40% CRIT Rate, allowing players to invest heavily in CRIT DMG and ATK, as the CRIT Rate requirement is largely met by the set bonus.
*   **2-piece Blizzard Strayer + 2-piece ATK% Set (e.g., Gladiator's Finale, Shimenawa's Reminiscence):** This combination is a strong alternative, particularly for Melt compositions or when a full Blizzard Strayer set is difficult to farm. The 2-piece Blizzard Strayer provides 15% Cryo DMG Bonus, while the 2-piece ATK% set grants an 18% ATK bonus. This offers a balanced stat distribution.
*   **4-piece Shimenawa's Reminiscence:** This set can be powerful if Ganyu consistently uses her Charged Attacks. Its 2-piece effect grants 18% ATK. The 4-piece effect, when triggering an Elemental Skill, consumes 15 Energy to increase Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attack DMG by 50% for 10 seconds. This significantly boosts Ganyu's Charged Attack damage but requires careful Energy management, as it can make using her Elemental Burst more difficult.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** ATK%
*   **Goblet:** Cryo DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet:** CRIT DMG
*   **Substats:** CRIT DMG > ATK% > CRIT Rate (until cap is reached) > Energy Recharge (if needed for Burst uptime)

For a 4-piece Blizzard Strayer set in a Freeze team, the CRIT Rate substat becomes less important, allowing for a higher focus on CRIT DMG and ATK%. Aim for a CRIT Ratio of around 1:2 (e.g., 20% CRIT Rate, 40% CRIT DMG) before the set bonuses, and then stack CRIT DMG and ATK%.

## Team Compositions

Ganyu excels in both Freeze and Melt compositions, leveraging her Cryo application and high damage potential.

### Freeze Compositions

Freeze teams focus on immobilizing enemies with the Frozen reaction, allowing Ganyu to freely unleash her Charged Attacks without interruption.

*   **Ganyu / Kokomi / Kazuha / Shenhe:** This is a top-tier Freeze team.
    *   **Kokomi:** Provides consistent Hydro application for Freeze, healing, and can use Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers for an ATK buff.
    *   **Kazuha:** Offers crowd control, grouping enemies for Ganyu's AoE, and provides a significant Cryo DMG Bonus through his Elemental Absorption and passive talent.
    *   **Shenhe:** A dedicated Cryo support who significantly buffs Ganyu's Cryo DMG through her Skill and Burst, especially with her Quills passive. Her Burst also provides a RES Shred.

*   **Ganyu / Mona / Kazuha / Diona:** A strong alternative when Shenhe is unavailable.
    *   **Mona:** Provides Hydro application and a powerful Omen debuff that increases DMG taken by enemies.
    *   **Kazuha:** As above, for grouping and Cryo DMG buffs.
    *   **Diona:** Offers healing, a Cryo shield for interruption resistance, and a CRIT Rate buff with her Burst.

### Melt Compositions

Melt teams capitalize on Ganyu's Cryo damage triggering the Melt reaction with Pyro, dealing 1.5x damage (when Cryo hits Pyro) or 2x damage (when Pyro hits Cryo). Ganyu's Charged Attacks are ideal for triggering Melt consistently.

*   **Ganyu / Xiangling / Bennett / Zhongli:** A powerful Melt team that balances damage and survivability.
    *   **Xiangling:** Her Pyronado provides consistent off-field Pyro application, allowing Ganyu's Charged Attacks to trigger Melt.
    *   **Bennett:** Provides a massive ATK buff with his Elemental Burst and healing. His Pyro application can also be utilized.
    *   **Zhongli:** Offers an unbreakable shield, providing immense interruption resistance for Ganyu's Charged Attacks and reducing enemy RES to all elements.

*   **Ganyu / Xiangling / Bennett / Thoma:** A more accessible Melt team.
    *   **Thoma:** Provides a Pyro shield for interruption resistance and can contribute to Pyro application. His shield can be stacked for increased durability.

## Ceiling and Role

Ganyu is widely regarded as one of the highest AoE DPS characters in Genshin Impact, particularly excelling in situations where enemies can be grouped or are naturally clustered. Her Frostflake Arrow's bloom damage, combined with the extensive AoE of her Elemental Burst, allows her to clear large groups of enemies with remarkable efficiency. In Freeze compositions, her damage ceiling is further amplified by the guaranteed CRIT Rate from Blizzard Strayer and the DMG bonuses from supports like Kazuha and Shenhe. The ability to continuously freeze enemies ensures she can maintain optimal positioning and damage output without interruption.

In Melt compositions, while requiring more precise execution to ensure Ganyu's hits trigger the stronger Melt variant (Pyro hitting Cryo), she can achieve exceptionally high single-target and AoE damage numbers. The synergy with Bennett's ATK buff and Xiangling's off-field Pyro application makes her a formidable force.

Her high damage ceiling is further cemented by her reliance on Charged Attacks, which scale exceptionally well with ATK, CRIT DMG, and DMG Bonuses. The guaranteed crit on her Level 2 Charged Arrow bloom significantly simplifies gearing, allowing players to focus on maximizing other damage stats. While she can be played as an off-field Burst DPS, her true potential is realized when she is the primary on-field damage dealer, utilizing her Charged Attacks to their fullest. The investment required to build her and her supporting cast is substantial, but the payoff is one of the highest damage outputs achievable in the game, especially in AoE scenarios.
