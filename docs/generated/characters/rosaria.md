# Rosaria

## Lore and Background

Rosaria is a prominent figure within the Church of Favonius in Mondstadt, serving as a Sister. However, her outward piety and role within the church mask a far more complex and shadowed past. Before her affiliation with the Church, Rosaria was a member of a clandestine organization, likely a group of assassins or spies, where she honed skills in combat, deception, and infiltration. This past has left an indelible mark on her personality and methods, leading her to adopt an unorthodox approach to her duties as a Sister. She often operates outside the strictures of traditional religious doctrine, prioritizing pragmatism and efficiency over moralistic pronouncements.

Her motivations for joining the Church of Favonius are shrouded in mystery. While she professes loyalty to the Anemo Archon and her duties, there are strong hints that her integration into the Church was a strategic move, possibly to escape her former life, gather intelligence, or pursue a hidden agenda. Her Story Quest, "The Shadow of the Past," delves into this enigmatic background, revealing fragments of her former life and the circumstances that led her to Mondstadt. She exhibits a cynical worldview and a detached demeanor, often observing the world with a keen, critical eye. Despite her outward coldness, she occasionally displays a protective streak towards those she deems worthy or those who are genuinely in need, though her methods remain unconventional and often involve a degree of coercion or manipulation. Her relationships within Mondstadt are complex; she maintains a professional, albeit distant, relationship with figures like Jean, the Acting Grand Master, and often clashes with more devout individuals due to her pragmatic and sometimes morally ambiguous actions. Her interactions with other characters, particularly those with darker pasts or those who operate in the shadows, reveal a shared understanding and a grudging respect.

## Combat Kit and Mechanics

Rosaria's combat effectiveness stems from her unique Cryo abilities, focusing on burst damage, crowd control, and a distinctive party-wide Critical Rate buff.

### Elemental Skill: Unexpected Grace

Unexpected Grace is Rosaria's Elemental Skill, which allows her to swiftly move behind an opponent and deliver a series of Cryo-infused attacks. This skill has two charges, enabling consecutive dashes and attacks. The initial strike deals Cryo DMG, and upon hitting an opponent, Rosaria will perform a final attack that deals more Cryo DMG. The key mechanic of Unexpected Grace lies in its repositioning capability. By dashing behind an enemy, Rosaria can exploit enemy AI and positioning to her advantage, setting up for follow-up attacks or enabling her Elemental Burst more effectively. The cooldown for each charge of Unexpected Grace is 6 seconds, with a total duration of 13 seconds.

### Elemental Burst: Ravaging Confession

Ravaging Confession is Rosaria's Elemental Burst, a powerful Cryo ability that deals significant damage and provides crucial utility. Upon activation, Rosaria swings her weapon to create a Raging Dance, dealing Cryo DMG to opponents in front of her. This initial strike is followed by a series of Ice Lance strikes that deal Cryo DMG to a random opponent within the field. The Raging Dance lasts for 4 seconds, during which Ice Lance strikes occur every 1.5 seconds.

The most significant aspect of Ravaging Confession is its **Critical Rate sharing mechanic**. When the Raging Dance hits an opponent, Rosaria increases the Critical Rate of all **on-field party members** by 12% of Rosaria's own Critical Rate. This buff lasts for 10 seconds and is only applied when Rosaria is on the field. Crucially, this buff is **not** applied to Rosaria herself. The Critical Rate buff is calculated based on Rosaria's base Critical Rate at the moment her Burst is cast. Any changes to her Critical Rate after the Burst is deployed will not affect the buff's value. The duration of the Raging Dance is 4 seconds, and the buff lasts for 10 seconds, meaning the buff persists for 6 seconds after the Raging Dance concludes. The energy cost for Ravaging Confession is 60, and its cooldown is 15 seconds.

### Passive Talents

*   **Night Conviction:** When Rosaria uses Ravaging Confession, her own Critical Rate is increased by 15% for 10 seconds. This is a personal buff and does not contribute to the party-wide Critical Rate share.
*   **Secret Ways:** When Rosaria hits an opponent with a Normal Attack, she gains a stack of Secret Ways for 5 seconds. Each stack increases her Physical DMG by 10%. This passive is most relevant when Rosaria is built for physical damage, but it also benefits her overall damage output when she is not primarily a Cryo support.
*   **Shadow's Embrace:** Decreases the Physical RES of opponents within the Raging Dance field by 20% for 10 seconds. This is a significant debuff that directly amplifies the damage of physical attackers in the party, making Rosaria an excellent support for physical damage dealers like Eula.

## Role and Team Compositions

Rosaria excels as a **Cryo Battery** and a **Critical Rate Support**, particularly within physical damage and Melt-focused teams. Her ability to generate Cryo particles, her Burst's Critical Rate sharing, and her Physical RES shred make her a versatile addition to various team archetypes.

### Cryo Battery

Rosaria's Elemental Skill generates Cryo particles, and her Burst, while not directly generating particles, contributes to consistent Cryo application. When built with sufficient Energy Recharge, she can reliably funnel energy to her teammates, especially those who require a significant amount of energy for their Bursts, such as Eula. The Cryo infusion on her Normal Attacks and Skill also allows for consistent Cryo application for elemental reactions.

### Critical Rate Support

The core of Rosaria's support capability lies in her Elemental Burst's unique mechanic: sharing a percentage of her Critical Rate with the active party members. This allows teams to reach higher Critical Rate thresholds more easily, boosting overall damage output. The effectiveness of this buff is directly tied to Rosaria's own Critical Rate. A common goal for Rosaria's build is to reach a Critical Rate of 100% to maximize the party-wide buff, providing a flat 12% Critical Rate to her teammates.

### Team Archetypes

*   **Physical Damage Teams:** Rosaria is a premier support for physical damage dealers like Eula. Her Elemental Burst provides a substantial Critical Rate boost, which is crucial for physical damage, and her passive talent, Shadow's Embrace, shreds enemy Physical RES. Her Cryo application can also enable Superconduct reactions, further reducing enemy Physical RES and dealing AoE Cryo DMG. A typical Eula team might include Eula (Main DPS), Rosaria (Support), Raiden Shogun or Fischl (Electro for Superconduct and Battery), and Zhongli or another shielder/healer for survivability.

*   **Melt Teams:** Rosaria can also function effectively in Melt teams, especially with Pyro characters who can consistently apply Pyro. Her Cryo application from her Skill and Burst allows for frequent Melt reactions. In these teams, she acts as a sub-DPS and a Critical Rate buffer. A sample Melt team could involve Xiangling (Off-field Pyro DPS), Bennett (Healer/Buffer), Rosaria (Cryo Sub-DPS/Support), and an Anemo character like Kazuha or Sucrose for Swirl and grouping.

*   **Freeze Teams:** While not her primary role, Rosaria can contribute to Freeze teams by providing consistent Cryo application and Critical Rate buffs. However, characters like Ganyu or Ayaka often take precedence as the primary Cryo DPS in dedicated Freeze compositions.

## Mechanics of Critical Rate Share

Rosaria's unique ability to share Critical Rate is a powerful support mechanic. When her Elemental Burst, Ravaging Confession, is active, it grants a buff to all **on-field party members** that increases their Critical Rate by **12% of Rosaria's own Critical Rate**. This buff lasts for 10 seconds.

**Key Points:**

*   **Calculation:** The buff is calculated based on Rosaria's Critical Rate at the moment she casts her Elemental Burst. Any subsequent changes to her Critical Rate will not alter the buff's value.
*   **Target:** The buff applies to all characters **currently on the field**. Characters not on the field do not receive the buff.
*   **Self-Exclusion:** Rosaria herself **does not** benefit from this party-wide Critical Rate share. Her own Critical Rate must be built independently.
*   **Maximization:** To maximize the effectiveness of this buff, players often aim to build Rosaria with 100% Critical Rate. This would grant a flat 12% Critical Rate to her teammates.
*   **Synergy:** This mechanic synergizes exceptionally well with characters who have high base Critical Rate, or who benefit greatly from consistent Critical Hits, such as physical damage dealers or Melt/Vaporize DPS units.

**Example:**
If Rosaria has 70% Critical Rate when she casts her Elemental Burst, her teammates on the field will gain an additional 12% of 70% = 8.4% Critical Rate. If Rosaria has 100% Critical Rate, her teammates will gain 12% of 100% = 12% Critical Rate. This 12% is added to their existing Critical Rate. For instance, if a teammate has 50% Critical Rate, they would then have 50% + 12% = 62% Critical Rate while Rosaria's buff is active.
