# Keqing

## Lore

Keqing is the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, one of the seven powerful figures who govern the nation of Liyue. As the Yuheng, she holds significant authority and responsibility, overseeing matters related to geology, mining, and land development within Liyue Harbor and its surrounding territories. Her position places her at the forefront of Liyue's economic and infrastructural progress.

Unlike many in Liyue who revere the Geo Archon, Rex Lapis (Zhongli), Keqing is a staunch pragmatist and a skeptic of divine authority. She believes that humanity's future and prosperity should be determined by their own efforts and ingenuity, rather than relying on the whims or interventions of gods. This belief stems from her deep-seated conviction in human self-determination and the power of collective action. She views divine protection as a crutch that can stifle innovation and weaken the resolve of mortals.

This philosophy was starkly tested during the Rite of Descension, where Rex Lapis appeared to die. While many Liyue citizens were plunged into despair and uncertainty, Keqing saw it as an opportunity. She rallied the Liyue Qixing and the people, urging them to take charge of their own destiny and prove that Liyue could thrive without its divine protector. Her leadership during this crisis solidified her reputation as a decisive and forward-thinking individual, even if her views on divinity were controversial. She actively worked to maintain order and ensure the continued functioning of Liyue, demonstrating her commitment to the nation's independence from divine reliance.

Keqing's dedication to Liyue is unwavering, but her methods and beliefs often set her apart. She is known for her tireless work ethic, often seen poring over documents and plans late into the night. She values efficiency and direct action, sometimes appearing impatient with bureaucracy or those who rely too heavily on tradition or superstition. Her personal philosophy is rooted in the idea that true strength comes from within, and that the people of Liyue are capable of achieving greatness through their own hard work and intelligence. This drive for self-sufficiency is a core tenet of her character and influences her approach to governance and her interactions with others.

## Kit Mechanics

Keqing's combat kit revolves around her Electro application, rapid mobility, and a powerful Elemental Burst. Her Normal and Charged Attacks deal Physical DMG by default, but her Elemental Skill and Burst allow for Electro infusion.

### Elemental Skill: Stellar Restoration

Stellar Restoration is a two-part ability. Upon activation, Keqing throws a Lightning Stiletto forward, dealing Electro DMG. This stiletto can be placed on the ground, and it persists for a set duration (10 seconds).

The second part of the skill is activated by holding the Elemental Skill button while the stiletto is active. Keqing will instantly teleport to the stiletto's location, dealing Electro DMG in a small AoE upon arrival. If the stiletto is placed directly above enemies, Keqing will perform a downward slash upon teleporting, dealing significant Electro DMG.

Alternatively, pressing the Elemental Skill button again while the stiletto is active will cause Keqing to dash towards the stiletto's position, launching a series of sword attacks that deal Electro DMG. This dash attack is faster than the hold-teleport but offers less mobility and doesn't allow for the downward plunge.

The cooldown for Stellar Restoration is 7.5 seconds. This skill is crucial for Keqing's mobility, allowing her to reposition, dodge attacks, and close the distance to enemies. It also serves as her primary method of applying Electro to herself for her Normal and Charged Attacks.

### Elemental Burst: Starward Sword

Keqing's Elemental Burst, Starward Sword, unleashes a flurry of rapid sword strikes. She performs a series of AoE slashes that deal Electro DMG. The Burst has a relatively low cooldown of 12 seconds and an energy cost of 50.

The Burst's damage scales with her ATK and Electro DMG Bonus. It is a powerful tool for clearing groups of enemies and dealing burst damage to single targets. The final slash of the Burst also deals significant damage.

### Transition from Physical to Electro Build

Initially, Keqing was often built as a Physical DMG dealer, utilizing artifacts like the 4-piece Gladiator's Finale to boost her Normal Attack DMG. This was due to the lack of strong Electro-focused artifact sets and the general strength of Physical DMG scaling in the early game. Her Electro infusion from her Elemental Skill was seen as a temporary buff.

However, with the introduction of artifact sets like Thundering Fury and later, the Dendro element and the Aggravate reaction, Keqing's viability as an Electro DPS skyrocketed. The Aggravate reaction, which amplifies Electro DMG, made focusing on Electro infusion and Electro DMG much more rewarding than Physical DMG. This shift led to the widespread adoption of Electro-focused builds, utilizing artifacts that boost Electro DMG and reaction damage.

## Builds

Keqing's build path has evolved significantly, with her current optimal builds focusing on maximizing her Electro DMG and reaction potential, particularly with Aggravate teams.

### Weapons

*   **Primordial Jade Cutter (5-Star):** This is Keqing's signature weapon and her Best-in-Slot (BiS) for Electro builds. Its high Base ATK and CRIT Rate substat provide a substantial boost to her damage output. The passive effect, which grants an ATK bonus based on a percentage of her Max HP and heals her when she takes damage, further enhances her survivability and damage.
*   **Mistsplitter Reforged (5-Star):** Another excellent 5-star option, Mistsplitter Reforged provides a significant CRIT DMG substat and an Elemental DMG Bonus passive. The passive stacks are easily obtainable for Keqing due to her frequent use of Elemental Skills and Bursts.
*   **Lacerate (5-Star - Limited Event Weapon):** This weapon offers a high CRIT Rate substat and an ATK% passive, making it a strong choice for boosting Keqing's overall damage.
*   **Harbinger of Dawn (3-Star):** A surprisingly strong budget option, especially at R5. It provides a substantial CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG bonus when Keqing's HP is above 90%. This requires careful management of her HP but can yield impressive results for a 3-star weapon.
*   **Iron Sting (4-Star):** A craftable weapon that provides Elemental DMG Bonus as a substat and an ATK% bonus upon dealing damage. It's a solid F2P option that synergizes well with her Electro-focused kit.
*   **Amenoma Kageuchi (4-Star):** Another craftable option that offers ATK% as a substat. Its passive provides Energy Recharge and ATK bonuses after using her Elemental Burst, which can be beneficial for maintaining Burst uptime.

### Artifacts

*   **4-piece Thundering Fury:** This set is Keqing's BiS for Aggravate teams.
    *   2-piece bonus: +15% Electro DMG Bonus.
    *   4-piece bonus: Increases Electro-Charged, Overloaded, Superconduct, and Hyperbloom DMG by 40%, and the DMG Bonus of Aggravate and Spread by 20%. Triggering these reactions also reduces the Elemental Skill cooldown by 1s. This effect can be triggered once every 0.8s.
    This set significantly boosts her Electro DMG and, crucially, reduces the cooldown of her Elemental Skill, allowing for more frequent teleports and Electro infusions, which directly translates to more Aggravate procs.
*   **4-piece Gladiator's Finale:** While less optimal for Aggravate, this set can still be a strong choice for general Electro DPS or if you have well-substatted pieces.
    *   2-piece bonus: +18% ATK.
    *   4-piece bonus: If the wielder uses a Sword, Claymore or Polearm, increases Normal Attack DMG by 35%.
    This set boosts her Normal Attack DMG, which is still relevant, but it doesn't amplify reaction damage as effectively as Thundering Fury.
*   **2-piece Thundering Fury + 2-piece ATK% Set (e.g., Gladiator's Finale, Shimenawa's Reminiscence):** A viable option if you lack a complete 4-piece Thundering Fury set with good substats. This combination provides a good balance of Electro DMG and ATK.
*   **2-piece Thundering Fury + 2-piece Noblesse Oblige:** This can be considered if you are prioritizing her Burst damage, but it's generally less optimal for sustained Aggravate damage.

### Main and Substats

*   **Sands:** ATK% or Elemental Mastery (EM) if running a heavily reaction-focused team with significant EM buffs from teammates. For most Aggravate builds, ATK% is generally preferred.
*   **Goblet:** Electro DMG Bonus. This is crucial for maximizing her Electro damage.
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG. Aim for a 1:2 ratio of CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG.
*   **Substats Priority:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Elemental Mastery, Energy Recharge (ER).
    *   **ER Threshold:** For Aggravate teams, Keqing generally needs around 130-140% ER to consistently use her Elemental Burst. This can vary depending on team composition and rotation.

## Team Compositions

Keqing's viability has been dramatically enhanced by the introduction of Dendro and the Aggravate reaction. Her ability to consistently apply Electro makes her an excellent trigger for this powerful reaction.

### Aggravate: Keqing + Nahida + Fischl + Baizhu

This is arguably Keqing's strongest and most popular team composition.

*   **Keqing (Electro DPS):** The primary on-field damage dealer. Her Electro infusion and rapid attacks benefit greatly from Aggravate.
*   **Nahida (Dendro Applicator/Sub-DPS):** Provides consistent off-field Dendro application with her Elemental Skill (All Schemes to Know), enabling Quicken and Aggravate. Her Elemental Burst also buffs Keqing's EM and damage based on team composition. Nahida's EM also directly buffs Keqing's Aggravate damage.
*   **Fischl (Electro Sub-DPS/Battery):** Oz provides consistent off-field Electro application, further enabling Quicken and Aggravate. Her Elemental Burst also generates a significant amount of Electro particles, helping to battery Keqing. Fischl's A4 passive, particularly when Keqing triggers Aggravate, adds substantial personal damage.
*   **Baizhu (Healer/Shielder/Dendro Support):** Baizhu provides crucial healing and interruption resistance through his Elemental Burst and Skill. His Dendro application also contributes to Quicken uptime. His passive buffs Keqing's Electro DMG based on his Max HP, further enhancing her damage.

**Rotation Example:**
1.  Baizhu Skill & Burst
2.  Nahida Skill & Burst
3.  Fischl Skill & Burst
4.  Keqing Skill (place stiletto), swap to Normal Attacks, use Charged Attacks, Skill (teleport/dash), Normal Attacks, Burst. Repeat.

### Other Viable Teams

*   **Keqing + Nahida + Kazuha + Bennett:** A classic Hyperbloom/Aggravate hybrid. Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Electro DMG, while Bennett provides ATK buffs and healing. This team relies on Kazuha's crowd control and elemental absorption for enhanced damage.
*   **Keqing + Dendro Traveler + Fischl + Kuki Shinobu:** A more budget-friendly Aggravate team. Dendro Traveler provides Dendro application, Fischl offers off-field Electro, and Kuki Shinobu provides healing and Electro application for Electro-Charged/Hyperbloom if applicable.
*   **Keqing + Yae Miko + Kazuha + Zhongli:** A strong Electro-focused team that can perform well in situations where Dendro isn't strictly necessary. Yae Miko provides significant off-field Electro DMG, Kazuha buffs it, and Zhongli offers unparalleled shielding and RES shred.

## The Aggravate Transformation

The introduction of the Dendro element and the subsequent reactions it enables, particularly Quicken and its amplified state, Aggravate, has fundamentally transformed Keqing's viability in Genshin Impact. Before Dendro, Keqing struggled to compete with other top-tier DPS characters. Her Electro application, while decent, was often insufficient to consistently trigger powerful reactions like Superconduct or Overload to her full potential, and her Physical builds were outclassed by dedicated Physical DPS units.

The Quicken reaction, triggered by Electro and Dendro interacting, creates an "aura" on the enemy. When an enemy affected by Quicken is hit by Electro DMG, the Aggravate reaction occurs, directly increasing the Electro DMG dealt by a percentage based on the triggering character's Elemental Mastery (EM) and the base multiplier of the Electro attack. Conversely, if the Quicken-affected enemy is hit by Dendro DMG, the Spread reaction occurs, amplifying Dendro DMG.

Keqing excels in this new meta for several reasons:

1.  **Consistent Electro Application:** Her Elemental Skill, particularly the teleport-dash, allows her to rapidly apply Electro to herself and enemies. Her Normal and Charged Attacks, when infused with Electro, can trigger Aggravate multiple times per rotation.
2.  **Synergy with EM:** Aggravate's damage scaling with EM makes characters who can build EM and benefit from it highly valuable. Keqing, when built with EM substats or benefiting from team-wide EM buffs (like from Nahida's Burst), sees a significant boost to her damage output.
3.  **Artifact Synergy:** The 4-piece Thundering Fury artifact set, which was previously a niche choice, became Keqing's BiS. Its 4-piece bonus directly buffs Aggravate damage and, crucially, reduces her Elemental Skill cooldown upon triggering Electro-related reactions. This allows for more frequent teleports, more Electro infusions, and thus more Aggravate procs, creating a powerful feedback loop.
4.  **Team Building Flexibility:** The rise of Dendro characters like Nahida, Dendro Traveler, and Collei, alongside strong Electro supports like Fischl and Kuki Shinobu, and Dendro healers/buffers like Baizhu, has created robust and synergistic team compositions centered around Keqing. These teams provide consistent Dendro and Electro application, healing, shielding, and buffs, allowing Keqing to unleash her full potential.

In essence, Aggravate provided Keqing with a damage multiplier that her kit was previously missing. It shifted her focus from a hybrid Physical/Electro DPS to a dedicated Electro reaction specialist, allowing her to finally fulfill her potential as a top-tier DPS character. The reaction's inherent damage amplification, combined with Keqing's fast-paced Electro application and the synergy with artifact sets and team members, has revitalized her role in the game's combat landscape.
