# Arlecchino

## Lore

Arlecchino, known as the Knave and the Fourth-Ranked Fatui Harbinger, is the current Director of the House of the Hearth. This orphanage, ostensibly a charitable institution, serves as a brutal training ground and recruitment center for the Fatui, molding orphaned children into loyal and highly skilled operatives. Arlecchino ascended to directorship through a combination of cunning, ruthlessness, and an uncanny ability to inspire both fear and a twisted form of loyalty in her charges. The circumstances of her taking over from the previous director are shrouded in mystery, but it is implied that she orchestrated the former director's downfall, demonstrating her ambition and capacity for decisive action.

Her relationship with the operatives, whom she refers to as her "children," is complex and deeply unsettling. While she provides them with a structured environment, education, and training, her methods are unforgiving. She views them not as individuals to be cherished, but as assets to be honed for the Fatui's agenda. Yet, she also exhibits a possessive and fiercely protective nature towards them, seeing their successes as her own and their failures as personal affronts. This maternalistic facade, however, is underpinned by a pragmatic understanding that their survival and effectiveness are paramount to her own standing and the continued operation of the House of the Hearth. She instills in them a sense of belonging and purpose, but this is inextricably linked to their unwavering obedience and dedication to her and the Fatui's goals. Key figures among her "children" include Columbina (the third-ranked Harbinger, whose relationship with Arlecchino is particularly close and enigmatic) and Lyney, Lynette, and Freminet, the trio of illusionists who operate as a specialized unit. Arlecchino views them as extensions of her will, and their individual growth is a testament to her leadership, even as their lives are forfeit if necessary for the Fatui's mission. Her motivations are rooted in a desire for power and influence within the Fatui hierarchy, but also a warped sense of responsibility for the children under her care, ensuring their survival in a harsh world by making them the harshest.

## Kit Mechanics: Bond of Life and HP Drain

Arlecchino's combat kit revolves around her unique **Bond of Life** system and a self-damaging mechanic that fuels her offensive capabilities. She is a Polearm-wielding Pyro character designed to function as a primary on-field DPS.

### Bond of Life System

Bond of Life is a debuff that prevents the target from being healed. Arlecchino, however, can possess Bond of Life herself, which directly influences her damage output. She gains Bond of Life primarily through her Elemental Skill, **"All is Ash."** When activated, Arlecchino deals Pyro DMG in a fan-shaped area and applies a **"Masque of the Red Death"** to enemies hit. This Masque lasts for 30 seconds and can be triggered by Arlecchino's Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks.

When Arlecchino hits an enemy afflicted with Masque of the Red Death with a Normal Attack, Charged Attack, or Plunging Attack, she will absorb the Masque, dealing Pyro DMG and **gaining Bond of Life equal to 30% of her Max HP**. This is the primary method for Arlecchino to accumulate Bond of Life on herself. The amount of Bond of Life she can gain this way is capped at 30% of her Max HP per Masque. She can only have one Masque of the Red Death active on an enemy at a time.

### HP Drain and Damage Bonus

Arlecchino's damage scales significantly with her Bond of Life. Her **Charged Attacks** are particularly empowered by this system. When Arlecchino has 30% or more of her Max HP as Bond of Life, her Charged Attacks will transform into **"Allenamento della Morte"** (Training of Death). These empowered Charged Attacks deal increased damage and will not consume Stamina. Crucially, when performing an Allenamento della Morte, Arlecchino will **drain her own HP by 4.5% of her Max HP** for each instance of damage dealt. This HP drain mechanic is central to her playstyle, as it allows her to continuously fuel her Bond of Life and empower her attacks.

The damage bonus Arlecchino receives is directly tied to her Bond of Life percentage. Her **Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks** gain a Pyro DMG Bonus based on her Bond of Life. The formula for this bonus is: **Pyro DMG Bonus = 0.2% * Bond of Life Percentage**, with a maximum bonus achievable when her Bond of Life reaches 30% of her Max HP. This means that at 30% Bond of Life, she gains an additional 6% Pyro DMG Bonus. This bonus is further amplified when she enters her Burst state.

### Elemental Burst: "Balemoon Rising"

Arlecchino's Elemental Burst, **"Balemoon Rising,"** is a powerful AoE Pyro attack that also enhances her combat capabilities. Upon activation, she deals significant Pyro DMG in a large area around her, and **cleanses all Masques of the Red Death from enemies**. More importantly, after casting her Burst, Arlecchino enters the **"Order of the Burning Shadows"** state for 10 seconds.

During the Order of the Burning Shadows state:
*   Her **Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks are converted to Pyro DMG**. This means all her attacks will benefit from her Pyro DMG Bonus and any other Pyro-specific buffs.
*   The **damage dealt by her Normal Attacks is increased by 100% of her ATK**.
*   The **damage bonus from her Bond of Life is doubled**. This means the Pyro DMG Bonus from her Bond of Life becomes **0.4% * Bond of Life Percentage** per instance of damage, capping at 12% Pyro DMG Bonus when her Bond of Life is at 30%.
*   Her **resistance to interruption is increased**.

The Order of the Burning Shadows state is where Arlecchino truly shines as a DPS. The combination of Pyro infusion, a significant damage multiplier on Normal Attacks, and the doubled Bond of Life bonus makes her devastating. The HP drain from her empowered Charged Attacks continues to function during this state, allowing her to maintain and even increase her Bond of Life, further boosting her damage.

### Why No Traditional Healers

Arlecchino's kit is designed to function without traditional healers. Her Bond of Life mechanic prevents her from being healed by external sources when she possesses it. Furthermore, her own HP drain mechanic means she actively reduces her own HP pool. Introducing a traditional healer would be counterproductive, as their healing would be negated by her Bond of Life, and it would not synergize with her damage amplification which relies on her *own* Bond of Life. Instead, characters who can provide shields, offer off-field support, or buff her damage are preferred. Characters who can reliably trigger her Masque of the Red Death or apply debuffs are also highly valuable. Her self-sustain comes from her ability to quickly reapply Bond of Life and deal massive damage, effectively "out-damaging" incoming enemy damage.

## Builds

Arlecchino's build focuses on maximizing her ATK, Crit Rate, Crit DMG, and Pyro DMG Bonus, with a significant emphasis on her Bond of Life mechanics.

### Signature Weapon

*   **Crimson Moon's Semblance:** This is Arlecchino's signature 5-star Polearm. It directly synergizes with her kit by providing a substantial ATK boost and increasing her Bond of Life damage bonus. Its passive grants ATK based on the Bond of Life percentage she possesses, up to a certain threshold. It also increases the damage of her Charged Attacks when her Bond of Life is present. This weapon is her BiS (Best in Slot) and offers the most significant damage increase.

### Strong Alternatives

*   **Staff of Homa:** A powerful 5-star Polearm that provides a massive Crit DMG boost and a significant ATK increase based on Max HP. It also grants an additional ATK bonus when Arlecchino's HP drops below 50%, which synergizes well with her HP drain mechanic, though it doesn't directly interact with Bond of Life as much as her signature weapon. It remains a top-tier choice due to its raw stats.
*   **Deathmatch:** A 4-star Battle Pass weapon that offers Crit Rate as its secondary stat. Its passive provides an ATK bonus that increases based on the number of enemies present, making it a solid choice for general use, especially in scenarios with multiple opponents.
*   **Ballad of the Fjords:** A 4-star weapon from the Fontaine Battle Pass. It provides Crit Rate and an Elemental Mastery bonus that scales with the number of different Elemental types in the party. While Arlecchino is Pyro, this weapon can be surprisingly effective in specific reaction-based teams where EM is beneficial.
*   **Missive Windspear:** A 4-star event weapon. It provides ATK% and an Elemental Mastery buff after triggering an Elemental Reaction. This can be a decent F2P option if available, especially for reaction teams.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy:** This is Arlecchino's BiS artifact set. The 2-piece bonus grants a 15% Pyro DMG Bonus. The 4-piece bonus is where it truly shines: it increases the wearer's ATK by 8% for 6s when their HP increases or decreases. This effect can be triggered once every 0.3s and can be stacked up to 4 times. Since Arlecchino constantly manipulates her HP through her Bond of Life system (HP drain and gain), this set provides a massive ATK% buff that is almost always active.
*   **4-piece Crimson Witch of Flames:** A strong alternative, especially if Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy is difficult to farm. The 2-piece bonus grants a 15% Pyro DMG Bonus. The 4-piece bonus increases Overload and Burning DMG by 40% and increases Melt and Vaporize DMG by 15%. Crucially, it also increases the 2-piece bonus of the Crimson Witch of Flames set by 50% for 10s after using an Elemental Skill, stacking up to 3 times. This provides a significant boost to her Pyro damage and reaction damage.
*   **2-piece Pyro DMG Bonus + 2-piece ATK%:** A viable budget or transitional set. Combinations like 2-piece Crimson Witch of Flames and 2-piece Gladiator's Finale or Shimenawa's Reminiscence can provide a decent stat boost while farming for the BiS sets.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** ATK% or HP% (ATK% is generally preferred, but HP% can be competitive if using weapons that scale off HP or if lacking good ATK substats).
*   **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus.
*   **Circlet:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio).
*   **Substats:** Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, HP%. ER (Energy Recharge) is generally not a high priority unless struggling to Burst consistently, as her kit focuses on her Normal Attacks during her Burst state.

## Team Compositions

Arlecchino excels in teams that can support her on-field DPS role, provide buffs, debuffs, or enable strong reactions. Due to her inability to be healed by traditional healers and her self-HP drain, shielders and characters who can provide consistent off-field damage or utility are preferred.

### Mono Pyro / Pyro Resonance Teams

These teams focus on maximizing Arlecchino's Pyro damage.

*   **Arlecchino / Bennett / Kazuha / Xiangling:** A classic Pyro powerhouse team. Bennett provides massive ATK buffs and healing (which Arlecchino can ignore when she has Bond of Life), Kazuha groups enemies and provides Elemental DMG buffs and RES shred, and Xiangling offers significant off-field Pyro damage with her Pyronado. This team maximizes Arlecchino's personal damage.
*   **Arlecchino / Bennett / Kazuha / Zhongli:** Replaces Xiangling with Zhongli for unparalleled shielding and universal RES shred. This provides Arlecchino with extreme survivability and allows her to focus entirely on attacking without interruption.

### Vaporize Teams

Arlecchino can trigger Vaporize with consistent Pyro application.

*   **Arlecchino / Yelan / Xingqiu / Kazuha:** A double Hydro setup with Kazuha for grouping and buffs. Yelan and Xingqiu provide consistent off-field Hydro application for Vaporize and significant personal damage, while Kazuha buffs Pyro damage and groups enemies. This team offers high damage potential and good survivability with the Hydro Resonance.
*   **Arlecchino / Furina / Kazuha / Baizhu:** Furina provides a massive DMG buff through her Fanfare stacks, and Baizhu offers healing (which is partially negated by Arlecchino's Bond of Life but can still be useful for the team) and Dendro application for occasional Burgeon or if paired with other Dendro characters. Kazuha provides grouping and Pyro DMG buffs.

### Overload Teams

While not her primary focus, Overload can be viable, especially with characters who can mitigate its knockback.

*   **Arlecchino / Fischl / Beidou / Kazuha:** A strong Electro-Charged/Overload hybrid. Fischl and Beidou provide excellent off-field Electro damage and application, enabling Overload reactions. Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Pyro damage. This team can struggle with enemy knockback from Overload if not managed well.
*   **Arlecchino / Raiden Shogun / Kazuha / Bennett:** Raiden Shogun can contribute significant off-field Electro damage. Bennett provides ATK buffs. Kazuha groups and buffs. This team can be potent but requires careful positioning to manage Overload's knockback.

### Melt Teams (Less Common)

Melt is generally less favored due to the difficulty of consistently applying Cryo to Arlecchino's rapid Pyro attacks.

*   **Arlecchino / Rosaria / Kazuha / Bennett:** Rosaria can provide off-field Cryo application and Crit DMG buffs. Kazuha groups and buffs Pyro. Bennett provides ATK buffs. This team requires precise timing for Melt procs.
