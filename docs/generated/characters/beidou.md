# Beidou

## Lore and Background

Beidou is the formidable and charismatic Captain of the Crux Fleet, a renowned maritime trading company based in Liyue. Her reputation precedes her, built not only on her business acumen but also on her sheer strength and daring. She famously defeated the colossal sea beast Haishan barehanded, a feat that cemented her legend and earned her the title of "Ruler of the Seas." This act of bravery, while seemingly a display of raw power, also highlights her deep connection to the ocean and her willingness to protect Liyue's shores from monstrous threats.

Her relationship with her crew is one of deep loyalty and mutual respect. Beidou leads by example, often participating in the most dangerous endeavors alongside her subordinates. This camaraderie is particularly evident in her interactions with the eccentric Electro character, Fischl. While Fischl presents herself as the "Prinzessin der Verurteilung" with her raven Oz, Beidou treats her with genuine affection and understanding, recognizing the person beneath the elaborate persona. This dynamic showcases Beidou's ability to see through facades and connect with individuals on a personal level, fostering a strong sense of family within the Crux. Other notable crew members include the stoic swordsman Kazuha, who serves as a vital member of the Crux, and the skilled navigator Ningguang, who, despite her status as a Tianquan of the Liyue Qixing, maintains a close working relationship with Beidou.

Beyond her maritime exploits, Beidou is also a prominent figure in Liyue's underground fighting scene. She frequents the fighting rings in the lower districts of the city, where she not only participates in matches but also acts as a patron and organizer. This aspect of her life reveals a more rugged, street-smart side, demonstrating her comfort in various social strata and her appreciation for honest competition and strength. Her presence in these circles further solidifies her image as a powerful and influential individual who commands respect across Liyue.

## Combat Kit Mechanics

Beidou is a 4-star Electro Claymore user whose kit revolves around her defensive counter-attack and her powerful off-field Electro damage.

### Elemental Skill: Tidecaller

Tidecaller is Beidou's signature defensive and offensive tool. Upon activation, she enters an Electro-charged state, gaining a shield that absorbs damage up to a certain percentage of her Max HP. The shield's strength scales with Beidou's Max HP.

The core mechanic of Tidecaller lies in its counter-attack. If Beidou is hit by an enemy attack while the shield is active, she will unleash a powerful Electro slash. The damage of this slash scales with her ATK and is further amplified by the number of enemies hit.

*   **Normal Activation:** If the skill duration ends or is manually canceled without taking damage, Beidou performs a single Electro slash.
*   **Taking Damage:** If Beidou takes damage while the shield is active, she immediately unleashes an Electro slash.
*   **Perfect Parry:** If Beidou activates Tidecaller precisely when she is hit by an enemy attack, she gains the "Electro-Fanfare" effect. This effect significantly boosts the damage of the subsequent Electro slash, allowing it to hit up to 10 enemies and deal damage equivalent to a fully charged attack. This "perfect parry" is crucial for maximizing Beidou's damage output with her skill. The damage multiplier for the perfect parry slash is substantially higher than the normal activation or taking damage activation.

The cooldown for Tidecaller is 25 seconds, and its duration is 2 seconds. The shield strength scales with Beidou's Max HP, making HP a valuable stat for her survivability and skill damage.

### Elemental Burst: Stormbreaker

Stormbreaker is Beidou's Elemental Burst, providing significant off-field Electro damage and utility. When activated, it deals Electro damage and grants Beidou the "Stormbreaker" state.

While Stormbreaker is active, Beidou's Normal and Charged Attacks will trigger lightning discharges that deal Electro damage. These lightning strikes can hit up to two enemies. Crucially, when an enemy affected by the lightning discharge is hit by another Electro-Charged enemy or is within a certain range of another Electro-Charged enemy, the lightning will jump between them. This bouncing effect allows Stormbreaker to hit multiple enemies simultaneously, making it exceptionally potent in multi-target scenarios.

The damage of these lightning discharges scales with Beidou's ATK. Furthermore, the Electro damage dealt by Stormbreaker is considered Elemental Burst damage.

A unique aspect of Stormbreaker is its interaction with Beidou's Elemental Skill, Tidecaller. When Beidou's Normal or Charged Attacks trigger a lightning discharge while Stormbreaker is active, and if Tidecaller's perfect parry was successfully executed before the Burst was activated, the damage of the lightning discharge is increased.

Stormbreaker has an energy cost of 80, a duration of 15 seconds, and a cooldown of 20 seconds. This high energy cost necessitates significant Energy Recharge for consistent uptime. The lightning discharges have a base cooldown of 2.4 seconds, but they can trigger more frequently if Beidou performs multiple Normal/Charged Attacks rapidly.

## Builds and Artifacts

Beidou's build focuses on maximizing her Electro damage, particularly her Elemental Burst, and ensuring consistent Burst uptime.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **Serpent Spine (Battle Pass Weapon):** This 4-star Claymore is often considered Beidou's BiS due to its Crit Rate substat and the passive ability that increases DMG dealt by 6% every 4s when on the field, stacking up to 5 times. While it requires Beidou to stay on-field to maintain stacks, the damage bonus is significant. However, it also increases DMG taken by 2% per stack, necessitating a strong shielder or careful positioning.

### Strong 4-Star Options

*   **The Unforged (5-star):** While a 5-star, its ATK% substat and strong shield-based ATK buff make it a potent choice if paired with a shielder like Zhongli.
*   **Skyward Pride (5-star):** Provides Energy Recharge and a passive that increases Elemental Burst DMG and creates a vacuum blade effect.
*   **Prototype Archaic (Craftable):** A decent free-to-play option with ATK% substat and a chance to deal additional damage.
*   **Blackcliff Slasher (Starglitter Shop):** Offers Crit DMG substat and an ATK% buff after defeating enemies, useful in multi-wave encounters.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This is overwhelmingly Beidou's best-in-slot artifact set. The 2-piece bonus grants 18% Energy Recharge, which is crucial for her high-cost Elemental Burst. The 4-piece bonus increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of Energy Recharge, with a maximum of 75% bonus DMG. This set synergizes perfectly with Beidou's high ER needs and her Burst-focused damage.
*   **2-Piece Noblesse Oblige + 2-Piece Thundering Fury/Gladiator's Finale:** A viable alternative if Emblem of Severed Fate pieces are scarce. 2-Piece Noblesse Oblige provides a 20% Elemental Burst DMG bonus, while 2-Piece Thundering Fury increases Electro DMG by 15%, and 2-Piece Gladiator's Finale increases ATK by 18%.
*   **2-Piece Noblesse Oblige + 2-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** Another strong hybrid option, providing both Burst DMG and ER.

### Artifact Main Stats

*   **Sands:** Energy Recharge (if below ER threshold) or ATK%
*   **Goblet:** Electro DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG

### Artifact Substats Priority

Crit Rate/Crit DMG > Energy Recharge > ATK% > HP% (for shield scaling)

## Team Compositions

Beidou excels in teams that can consistently trigger her Elemental Burst's lightning bounces, making her a premier choice for multi-enemy content.

### Core Synergy: Fischl

Beidou and Fischl form a powerful "Electro Duo." Fischl's off-field Electro application with her Oz, coupled with Beidou's Stormbreaker, creates a constant stream of Electro damage and triggers. This synergy is particularly potent against multiple enemies, as both characters' abilities can hit multiple targets.

### Recommended Team Archetypes

*   **Taser (Electro-Charged) Teams:** Beidou is a staple in many "Taser" compositions, which focus on triggering the Electro-Charged reaction.
    *   **Example:** Beidou, Fischl, Xingqiu/Yelan, Kazuha/Sucrose. Xingqiu or Yelan provide Hydro application for Electro-Charged, while Kazuha or Sucrose group enemies and provide Elemental DMG buffs and VV shred.
*   **Double Electro with Physical DPS:** Beidou can provide consistent off-field Electro application to enable Superconduct reactions for a Physical DPS character.
    *   **Example:** Beidou, Fischl, Eula/Razor, Diona/Zhongli. Diona or Zhongli provide shields and potentially Cryo application (Diona) for Superconduct.
*   **Mono-Electro Teams:** While less common, Beidou can function in teams with other Electro characters, though this often sacrifices elemental reaction diversity.
    *   **Example:** Beidou, Fischl, Kuki Shinobu, Kazuha. Kuki Shinobu provides healing and Electro application.

### Energy Recharge Threshold and Burst Uptime

Beidou's Elemental Burst, Stormbreaker, has a high energy cost of 80. To ensure consistent Burst uptime, especially when she is the primary off-field damage dealer, a significant amount of Energy Recharge is required.

*   **Against 1 Enemy:** Without a second Electro character or Favonius weapons, Beidou typically needs around **200-220% Energy Recharge**.
*   **Against 2+ Enemies:** With the increased energy particle generation from hitting multiple enemies with her Burst and potentially a second Electro unit (like Fischl), the ER requirement can be lowered to **170-190%**.
*   **With Favonius Weapons/Battery:** If Beidou is paired with a character wielding a Favonius weapon or another strong Electro battery (like Fischl with a Favonius Bow), her ER requirements can drop further, potentially to **150-170%**.

It is crucial to test Beidou's ER in practice within her specific team composition to determine the exact threshold needed for consistent 100% Burst uptime. The presence of other characters with Favonius weapons or Electro resonance can significantly reduce her personal ER needs.
