# Itto

## Lore

Arataki "Number One Hally-gooly" Itto, the self-proclaimed "Greatest Oni of the Arataki Gang," is a boisterous and charismatic figure from Inazuma. Despite his imposing Oni appearance, complete with horns and a powerful physique, Itto possesses a surprisingly gentle and even childlike nature, often prioritizing fun and friendship over strict adherence to rules. He is the undisputed leader of the Arataki Gang, a group of misfits and delinquents who, under his leadership, have carved out a niche for themselves through various, often unconventional, endeavors. Itto's origin story is tied to the Oni of Oni-Goroshi, a mountain steeped in legend, where he honed his strength and developed his unique personality.

His relationship with Kuki Shinobu, the gang's vice-leader, is a cornerstone of the Arataki Gang's functional chaos. Shinobu, a disciplined and pragmatic individual, acts as the perfect foil to Itto's impulsive and flamboyant style. She handles the gang's administrative tasks and often bails Itto out of trouble, demonstrating a deep loyalty and understanding of his true character. Ushi, a young Oni calf who Itto raised, is another significant figure in his life. Ushi is fiercely loyal to Itto and often accompanies him, showcasing Itto's paternal and caring side.

Itto shares a notable rivalry with Kujou Sara, a formidable Tengu general of the Tenryou Commission. Their animosity stems from a past incident where Sara defeated Itto in a duel, an event that Itto, despite his bravado, seems to take to heart. Their encounters are often filled with playful taunts and competitive spirit, hinting at a grudging respect beneath the surface. This rivalry is a recurring theme in Inazuma's narrative, highlighting the clash between Itto's free-spirited nature and the rigid order represented by the Tenryou Commission.

Behind the bravado and the love for beetle fighting and gambling, Itto harbors a surprisingly gentle and empathetic heart. He genuinely cares for his gang members and those he considers friends, often going out of his way to help them, albeit in his own chaotic manner. His desire for recognition and his boisterous personality are, in part, a way to mask a deeper insecurity and a longing for acceptance, a trait that endears him to many.

## Kit Mechanics

Itto is a 5-star Geo Polearm user whose kit revolves around his unique Superlative Superstrength mechanic and his Raging Oni King Elemental Burst.

### Superlative Superstrength

Itto gains stacks of Superlative Superstrength primarily through his Normal Attacks. When performing Normal Attacks, there is a chance to gain a stack. The chance increases with the number of hits in a Normal Attack string. Specifically, the first hit has a 50% chance, the second hit has a 50% chance, the third hit has a 100% chance, and the fourth hit has a 100% chance to grant one stack. Each stack can be consumed to perform a unique Charged Attack known as Arataki Kesagiri.

### Arataki Kesagiri

Arataki Kesagiri is Itto's signature Charged Attack. When Itto consumes Superlative Superstrength stacks, he unleashes a rapid series of Geo-infused slashes. This combo is significantly faster and more damaging than standard Charged Attacks and does not consume stamina. The number of slashes in the Arataki Kesagiri combo scales with the number of Superlative Superstrength stacks consumed, up to a maximum of four stacks. Consuming four stacks allows for the maximum number of devastating slashes. The animation for Arataki Kesagiri is unique and distinct from standard Charged Attacks, emphasizing its special nature.

### Elemental Skill: Masatsu Zetsugi: Invoker's Slash

Itto throws out Ushi, his young Oni friend, who deals Geo DMG to opponents and marks them with a "Taunt" effect. This Taunt draws enemy attacks. When Ushi is on the field, it gains 200% DEF as HP. When Ushi's HP falls below 20%, it will leap off the field. Ushi's Taunt effect lasts for 12 seconds. Ushi's HP scales with Itto's DEF. This skill has a cooldown of 10 seconds and generates 2-3 Geo particles upon hit.

### Elemental Burst: Royal Descent: Invoker's Strike

Itto enters the Raging Oni King state, transforming his Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks into Geo DMG. This state also converts his Normal Attack DMG Bonus to be based on his DEF. Crucially, during this state, Itto gains 1 Superlative Superstrength stack at the start of each of his Normal Attack combos, and each subsequent Normal Attack hit has a 50% chance to grant him 1 stack. This allows him to maintain his Arataki Kesagiri combo more easily during his Burst.

The Raging Oni King state also increases Itto's ATK based on his DEF. The conversion rate is 50% of his DEF. Additionally, his resistance to interruption is greatly increased. The Raging Oni King state lasts for 11 seconds, and the Elemental Burst has a cooldown of 18 seconds. The energy cost is 70.

The synergy between his Normal Attacks generating stacks and his Burst allowing for rapid Arataki Kesagiri usage is the core of his damage output. Players aim to maximize stacks before or during his Burst to unleash the full potential of his unique Charged Attack combo.

## Builds

Itto's build heavily prioritizes DEF due to his kit's scaling and the buffs provided by his Elemental Burst. Crit Rate and Crit DMG are also crucial for maximizing his damage output.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **Redhorn Stonethresher:** This 5-star Claymore is Itto's signature weapon and his BiS. It provides a massive base ATK stat, a high Crit DMG substat, and a unique passive that significantly boosts Normal Attack DMG based on Itto's DEF. The passive also increases his Charged Attack DMG by a percentage of his DEF. This weapon perfectly synergizes with his kit, amplifying his DEF-scaling damage.

### Strong 4-Star Options

*   **Serpent Spine:** This Battle Pass weapon offers a Crit Rate substat, which is highly valuable. Its passive increases the DMG dealt by the wielder as they take damage, stacking up to five times. While it requires careful management to maintain stacks, it provides a significant DMG boost and a reliable Crit Rate source.
*   **The Whiteblind:** This 4-star craftable Claymore is an excellent F2P option. It provides a DEF% substat, which directly benefits Itto's scaling. Its passive increases ATK and DEF when Normal or Charged Attacks hit enemies, stacking up to four times. This weapon is easily accessible and provides a solid all-around stat boost.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Husk of Opulent Dreams:** This is Itto's BiS artifact set. The 2-piece bonus grants 30% DEF. The 4-piece bonus, "Curiosity," triggers when the wearer is off-field or on-field and hits an opponent with a Geo attack. It grants a stack of "Embellish," which increases DEF by 6% and Geo DMG by 6%. This effect can stack up to 4 times and can be triggered once every 0.8 seconds. The maximum stacks grant a substantial 24% DEF and 24% Geo DMG Bonus. This set directly boosts Itto's DEF and Geo DMG, making it incredibly powerful.
*   **2-piece Husk of Opulent Dreams + 2-piece Archaic Petra:** A viable alternative if a full Husk set is difficult to farm. The 2-piece Husk provides 30% DEF, and the 2-piece Archaic Petra provides a 15% Geo DMG Bonus.
*   **2-piece Noblesse Oblige + 2-piece Archaic Petra:** Less optimal but usable if other sets are unavailable. The 2-piece Noblesse provides a 20% Elemental Burst DMG Bonus, and the 2-piece Archaic Petra provides a 15% Geo DMG Bonus.

### Main Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** DEF%
*   **Goblet:** Geo DMG Bonus%
*   **Circlet:** Crit Rate% or Crit DMG% (depending on substats to balance the ratio)

### Substat Priorities

1.  Crit Rate% / Crit DMG% (aim for a 1:2 ratio)
2.  DEF%
3.  Energy Recharge% (ER requirements vary based on team, but generally around 120-140%)
4.  Flat DEF

## Team Compositions

Itto excels in mono-Geo or double-Geo teams, where he can benefit from Geo Resonance and dedicated Geo support characters.

### The Standard Itto Team (Double Geo Support)

*   **Itto** (Main DPS)
*   **Gorou** (Dedicated Geo Support)
*   **Zhongli** (Shielder/Debuffer)
*   **Albedo** (Sub-DPS/Geo Battery)

**Explanation:** This is widely considered Itto's optimal team composition.
*   **Gorou:** His Elemental Skill provides DEF and Geo DMG buffs based on the number of Geo characters in the party. At C6, he also provides Crit DMG buffs to Geo characters. His presence is almost mandatory for maximizing Itto's potential.
*   **Zhongli:** His nigh-impenetrable shield provides immense survivability and interruption resistance, allowing Itto to freely unleash his combos. Zhongli's shield also shreds enemy RES to all elements by 20% with his skill, significantly boosting Itto's damage.
*   **Albedo:** His off-field Geo DMG from his Solar Isotoma provides consistent damage and generates Geo particles, helping Itto meet his Energy Recharge needs. His Skill also provides an Elemental Mastery buff for characters on-field when they take damage from his construct, though this is less relevant for Itto.

### Variations and Alternatives

*   **Triple Geo:** Replacing Zhongli or Albedo with another Geo character like Ningguang (for her Jade Screen's Geo DMG Bonus and her own damage) or a second Gorou (if C6) can further amplify Geo Resonance. However, this often sacrifices survivability or consistent off-field damage.
*   **Flex Slot:** In some cases, a non-Geo character might be used for utility, such as Bennett for ATK buffs (though his healing can interfere with Husk stacks if not managed) or Fischl for off-field Electro application and particle generation. However, these teams generally underperform compared to double-Geo setups.
*   **Itto + Gorou + Fischl + Bennett:** A more accessible team for players without Zhongli or Albedo. Fischl provides off-field damage and energy, while Bennett offers a significant ATK buff. However, this team lacks the RES shred from Zhongli and the consistent Geo particles from Albedo, potentially leading to higher ER requirements for Itto.

## DEF Scaling Explained

Defense (DEF) in Genshin Impact is a crucial stat that directly reduces incoming damage from enemies. However, for characters like Itto, DEF serves a dual purpose: it not only increases his survivability but also directly contributes to his offensive capabilities.

The formula for damage reduction based on DEF is as follows:
`Damage Reduction % = DEF / (DEF + 1395.5)`
This formula shows that as DEF increases, the percentage of damage reduction also increases, but with diminishing returns.

For Itto, his offensive scaling with DEF is primarily seen in two areas:

1.  **Elemental Burst (Raging Oni King):** During his Burst, Itto's ATK is increased based on his DEF. The conversion rate is 50% of his DEF. This means that every point of DEF Itto has directly translates into offensive power, effectively turning his defensive stat into offensive power. For example, if Itto has 2000 DEF, his Burst will grant him an additional 1000 ATK (2000 * 0.50).
2.  **Weapon Passives:** Weapons like Redhorn Stonethresher and The Whiteblind have passives that directly scale with DEF, further amplifying Itto's damage output. Redhorn Stonethresher, for instance, increases Normal Attack DMG by a percentage of DEF and Charged Attack DMG by a percentage of DEF.

This DEF scaling makes Itto unique. While most characters rely on ATK for damage, Itto's kit allows him to invest heavily in DEF, gaining both survivability and offensive power from a single stat. This is why artifacts with DEF% substats and the Husk of Opulent Dreams set are so vital for his build. The synergy between his DEF-based ATK conversion during his Burst and his DEF-scaling weapon passives creates a powerful offensive engine that is directly tied to his defensive capabilities.
