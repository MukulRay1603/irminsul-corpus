# Kazuha

Kaedehara Kazuha is a 5-star Anemo Sword character from Inazuma, renowned for his exceptional crowd control, elemental damage buffing capabilities, and significant role in meta-defining team compositions. His kit revolves around his ability to group enemies, trigger Swirl reactions, and convert his Elemental Mastery into team-wide elemental damage bonuses.

## Lore

Kazuha hails from the esteemed Kaedehara clan, a once-prominent family in Inazuma known for their wealth and influence. However, the clan fell into decline, leaving Kazuha with a legacy of both pride and hardship. During the Vision Hunt Decree, a period of intense oppression under the Shogunate where Visions were confiscated, Kazuha's close friend, a talented individual who possessed a Vision, was targeted. In a desperate attempt to protect his friend and resist the decree, Kazuha engaged in a duel with the Shogun's forces. Tragically, his friend was killed during this confrontation, an event that deeply scarred Kazuha and led him to question his own strength and purpose.

Overwhelmed by grief and guilt, Kazuha abandoned his homeland and embarked on a journey of self-exile. He boarded a ship bound for Liyue, eventually finding himself under the protection and camaraderie of the Crux Fleet, led by the formidable Captain Beidou. Beidou, recognizing Kazuha's unique skills and troubled spirit, offered him a place among her crew. Kazuha found a sense of belonging and purpose in sailing the seas with the Crux, contributing his combat prowess and keen observation to their endeavors. His travels across Teyvat allowed him to witness diverse cultures and confront various threats, slowly helping him to heal and find a new path forward, one where he could protect others and prevent similar tragedies. His past continues to weigh on him, but his experiences with the Crux have instilled in him a quiet resolve and a deep appreciation for freedom and the bonds of friendship.

## Kit Mechanics

Kazuha's kit is designed to maximize his utility as an Anemo support, focusing on grouping enemies, amplifying elemental damage through Swirl, and providing substantial buffs.

### Chihayaburu (Elemental Skill)

**Press:** Kazuha performs a swift slash, dealing Anemo DMG and pulling enemies and projectiles towards his position.
**Hold:** Kazuha enters an aiming mode, charging up before unleashing a more powerful Anemo DMG attack that pulls enemies and projectiles towards him. This charged attack creates a larger area of effect.

Upon using Chihayaburu, Kazuha enters the **Cloudstrider** state. In this state, his next Normal Attack within 5 seconds will be a **Midare Ranzan**, which deals Anemo DMG, increases his jump height, and generates 1 Wind Spirit effect. This Wind Spirit effect, when consumed by a Plunging Attack, will transform the Plunging Attack into a **Midare Ranzan** Plunging Attack. This special Plunging Attack deals Anemo DMG in a larger AoE and pulls enemies towards the center. The key benefit of Midare Ranzan is that if it comes into contact with Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, or Electro, it will perform a **Midare Ranzan** Swirl, dealing additional elemental DMG of that type.

The cooldown for Chihayaburu is 6 seconds for the press version and 9 seconds for the hold version. The Midare Ranzan state lasts for 5 seconds.

### Kazuha Slash (Elemental Burst)

Kazuha unleashes a single, powerful slash that deals Anemo DMG and creates a **'Saku'** (Autumn Whirlwind). The Saku is a large AoE field that continuously deals Anemo DMG to enemies within it.

Crucially, if the Saku comes into contact with Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, or Electro, it will **Swirl** that element, dealing additional elemental DMG of that type. This Swirl effect can occur once every 2 seconds. The Kazuha Slash has a duration of 8 seconds and a cooldown of 20 seconds. The initial slash deals 196.8% ATK DMG, and each pulse of the Saku deals 64.8% ATK DMG.

### A4 Passive: Poetics of Fuubutsu

This passive talent is Kazuha's most defining support ability. After Kazuha triggers a Swirl reaction, he grants his party members an **8% Elemental DMG Bonus** for the Swirled element for 8 seconds. This buff can stack up to two times, meaning he can grant buffs for two different elements simultaneously.

Furthermore, Kazuha's **Elemental Mastery** directly contributes to this buff. For every 1 point of Elemental Mastery Kazuha possesses, he grants an additional **0.04% Elemental DMG Bonus** to the corresponding element. This effect is additive to the base 8% and stacks additively. At a high Elemental Mastery value, such as 1000 EM, Kazuha would provide an additional 40% Elemental DMG bonus for the Swirled element. This makes building high EM on Kazuha paramount for maximizing his support potential.

### Constellations

While Kazuha is exceptionally powerful at C0, his constellations offer further enhancements:

*   **C1: Yamaarashi Tailwind:** Reduces the cooldown of Chihayaburu by 1.2 seconds. This allows for more frequent use of his Skill and thus more frequent application of his A4 passive.
*   **C2: Fujka Kage:** When Kazuha's Kazuha Slash (Burst) is active, it creates a vortex that pulls enemies towards Kazuha. Additionally, Kazuha gains 200 Elemental Mastery, and his Skill and Burst DMG are increased by 0.2% per point of his EM, up to a 60% increase. This significantly boosts his personal damage and further enhances his EM-based buffs.
*   **C4: Yurei: Kamishiro:** When Kazuha's Energy Recharge is 200% or higher, he gains 10% increased Movement SPD and 20% increased ATK SPD.
*   **C6: Samsara: Bloom:** Using Chihayaburu or Kazuha Slash resets the cooldown of the other. Additionally, for 5 seconds after using his Skill or Burst, Kazuha gains an Anemo Infusion, meaning his Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks deal Anemo DMG. This constellation transforms Kazuha into a potent Anemo DPS unit, capable of dealing significant damage while still providing his buffs.

## Builds

Kazuha's build is almost universally focused on maximizing his Elemental Mastery for his A4 passive, with secondary stats prioritizing Energy Recharge to ensure consistent Burst uptime.

### Artifacts

The **4-piece Viridescent Venerer** set is mandatory for Kazuha in virtually all team compositions. Its 2-piece bonus grants a 15% Anemo DMG Bonus, while its 4-piece bonus reduces the Elemental Resistance of enemies hit by the wearer's Swirl DMG by 40% for 10 seconds. This resistance shred is a massive damage increase for the elemental damage dealers in the party and is the primary reason Kazuha is so dominant.

For artifact main stats:
*   **Sands:** Elemental Mastery
*   **Goblet:** Elemental Mastery
*   **Circlet:** Elemental Mastery

If Energy Recharge needs are not met through substats or weapon, an ER% Sands can be used, but EM is strongly preferred.

For substats, the priority is:
1.  Elemental Mastery
2.  Energy Recharge (until comfortable uptime is achieved, typically around 180-200% ER depending on team and weapon)
3.  CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG (for personal damage, less important for pure support)

### Weapons

Kazuha's weapon choice significantly impacts his performance, with a clear BiS option and several strong alternatives.

*   **Best-in-Slot (BiS):** **Freedom-Sworn** (5-star Sword). This sword provides a large chunk of Elemental Mastery (200 at R5) and grants the "Oathsworn" buff upon triggering a Swirl reaction. This buff increases the wearer's ATK by 20% and provides a 32% Elemental DMG Bonus to all party members for 12 seconds. This weapon synergizes perfectly with Kazuha's kit, amplifying both his EM and his team's damage output.
*   **Strong 5-star Options:** **Primordial Jade Cutter** (CRIT Rate, ATK boost) and **Mistsplitter Reforged** (CRIT DMG, Elemental DMG bonus) can be used if Kazuha is built with some offensive stats, but they do not offer the EM utility of Freedom-Sworn.
*   **Best Craftable F2P:** **Iron Sting** (4-star Sword). This weapon provides Elemental Mastery as its secondary stat and grants an ATK bonus after dealing damage, which stacks up to 5 times. It's a very accessible and effective option for boosting Kazuha's EM and providing a decent personal damage boost.
*   **Other Strong 4-star Options:** **Favonius Sword** (4-star Sword) is excellent for team-wide energy generation, especially if the team struggles with ER. **Sacrificial Sword** (4-star Sword) can provide an extra Elemental Skill use, allowing for more frequent grouping and Swirls, but its EM substat is lower than Iron Sting.

### Team Role and Meta Impact

Kazuha is widely considered the single best elemental support character in Genshin Impact due to his unparalleled combination of crowd control, elemental damage amplification, and resistance shred. His ability to group enemies with his Elemental Skill and Burst makes him invaluable for dealing with large groups of smaller enemies, allowing AoE damage dealers to hit multiple targets simultaneously.

The core of his meta dominance lies in his A4 passive, **Poetics of Fuubutsu**, and the 4-piece Viridescent Venerer set bonus. The EM-to-elemental-DMG conversion, combined with the 40% elemental resistance shred from Viridescent Venerer, provides an enormous damage increase for the primary elemental damage dealers in the party. This makes him a near-universal pick for any team that relies on elemental reactions and Swirl.

Kazuha appears in more meta teams than almost any other character because his buffs are universally applicable to any elemental damage type. Whether it's a Pyro DPS like Hu Tao, an Electro DPS like Raiden Shogun, a Cryo DPS like Ayaka, or a Hydro DPS like Childe, Kazuha's ability to buff their respective elements and shred enemy resistances makes him an indispensable asset. His versatility allows him to slot into a vast array of team archetypes, including:

*   **Vaporize/Melt Teams:** Buffing Pyro or Cryo DPS units.
*   **Freeze Teams:** Grouping enemies and buffing Cryo/Hydro damage.
*   **Electro-Charged/Overload Teams:** Amplifying Electro and Pyro damage.
*   **Mono-Element Teams:** Providing significant buffs to a single element.
*   **Bloom-based Reaction Teams (Hyperbloom, Burgeon):** While not directly buffing Dendro, he can Swirl Hydro or Electro to enable reactions and group enemies.

His high base EM potential, coupled with Freedom-Sworn and his A4 passive, allows him to provide buffs that can rival or even surpass dedicated artifact sets for specific elements, all while offering crowd control and resistance reduction. This combination of utility makes him a cornerstone of many of the most powerful and efficient team compositions in the game, justifying his frequent appearance in high-level content and Spiral Abyss clear teams.
