# Raiden Shogun

## Lore

The Raiden Shogun is not a singular entity but a duality: the Electro Archon, Beelzebul (Ei), and her puppet, the Shogun. Ei, the twin sister of the former Electro Archon, Makoto, inherited the title of Archon after Makoto's death during the cataclysm 500 years prior to the game's events. Makoto, a benevolent Archon who sought to understand humanity, perished while protecting her people, leaving Ei with a profound sense of loss and a distorted understanding of eternity. This grief, coupled with the perceived weakness of humanity's transient nature, led Ei to pursue an absolute form of eternity: one devoid of change, suffering, and ultimately, free will.

The Shogun puppet was created by Ei as a vessel to rule Inazuma in her stead, allowing her to retreat into the Plane of Euthymia to meditate on eternity and overcome her trauma. The Shogun puppet, devoid of emotion and bound by strict protocols, enforced Ei's will with unwavering resolve. This led to the infamous Vision Hunt Decree, a policy aimed at confiscating all Visions in Inazuma, believing that only those chosen by the Archons, not by mortal ambition, were worthy of divine power. Ei saw Visions as a symbol of the fleeting desires and potential for suffering that she sought to eradicate.

Ei's isolation and the Shogun's rigid enforcement of the Decree caused immense suffering in Inazuma, culminating in a rebellion led by the resistance and the Traveler. The Traveler's intervention, coupled with the intervention of Yae Miko, Ei's long-time friend and the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine, forced Ei to confront the flaws in her pursuit of eternity. Yae Miko played a crucial role as Ei's anchor to the mortal world and a voice of reason, consistently challenging Ei's extreme ideologies and guiding her back from her self-imposed exile. Through her battles and conversations with the Traveler, Ei realized that true eternity lay not in stagnation but in the enduring spirit of humanity, their capacity for change, and the bonds they forge. She ultimately abolished the Vision Hunt Decree, returned all confiscated Visions, and began to rule Inazuma with a more compassionate understanding, acknowledging the value of mortal ambition and the importance of freedom. The Shogun puppet now serves as a symbol of her commitment to her people, a reminder of her past mistakes, and a tool for governance, rather than an instrument of absolute control.

## Kit Mechanics

Raiden Shogun's kit revolves around her Electro application, Burst damage, and team-wide energy regeneration. Her Elemental Skill, **Transcendence: Baleful Omen**, deals Electro DMG and applies a coordinated attack called "Eye of Stormy Judgment." This eye attacks opponents periodically when characters within its radius use their Elemental Burst, dealing Electro DMG based on Raiden Shogun's ATK. More importantly, it grants all nearby party members 1 stack of **Resolve** for every 2 energy they expend when using their Elemental Burst.

Her Elemental Burst, **Secret Art: Musou Shinsetsu**, is where her primary damage and utility shine. Upon activation, she enters the **Musou Isshin** state, dealing Electro DMG to all opponents in front of her and regenerating 8 Energy for all party members. During Musou Isshin, her Normal Attacks, Charged Attacks, and Plunging Attacks are infused with Electro DMG, cannot be overridden by other elemental infusions, and deal increased damage based on the number of Resolve stacks she has accumulated. Additionally, her resistance to interruption is increased, and she gains resistance to Electro-charged DMG. The Musou Isshin state lasts for 7 seconds.

The **Resolve** system is central to maximizing Raiden Shogun's Burst damage. Each stack of Resolve increases the DMG dealt by her Musou Isshin attacks. The maximum number of Resolve stacks is 60, and each stack is gained by party members expending 2 Energy for their Burst. This means a character with a 60-cost Burst will grant 30 Resolve stacks, an 80-cost Burst grants 40 stacks, and so on. The damage bonus from Resolve stacks scales linearly, with the maximum bonus achieved at 60 stacks.

A crucial utility aspect of Raiden Shogun's kit is her **team energy refund**. When the Musou Isshin state ends, Raiden Shogun triggers **"Will of One Strike,"** which restores Energy to all party members based on the total Energy Cost of her Burst attacks consumed during the Musou Isshin state. This restoration scales with her Energy Recharge stat, making high ER a crucial build component not just for her own Burst uptime but also for enabling her teammates to use their Bursts more frequently. This synergy makes her an exceptional battery and enabler for teams reliant on frequent Burst usage.

## Builds

### Signature Weapon

*   **Engulfing Lightning (5-star Polearm):** This is Raiden Shogun's BiS (Best in Slot) weapon. Its passive, **"Ashen Nightingale,"** grants ATK equal to 25% of the wielder's Energy Recharge over the base 100%. Additionally, it provides a 30% Energy Recharge bonus for 12 seconds after using an Elemental Skill. The substat is Energy Recharge, directly benefiting her kit. This weapon synergizes perfectly, as her damage scales with ATK and ER, and the passive directly converts ER into both.

### Free-to-Play (F2P) Best-in-Slot

*   **The Catch (4-star Polearm):** This weapon is obtained through fishing in Inazuma and is the premier F2P option for Raiden Shogun. Its passive, **"Hook the Catch,"** increases Elemental Burst DMG by 16% and Elemental Burst CRIT Rate by 12%. This directly boosts her primary damage source. Its ER substat also contributes to her energy needs and damage scaling.

### Artifacts

*   **4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This artifact set is overwhelmingly her best-in-slot.
    *   **2-piece bonus:** Energy Recharge +20%.
    *   **4-piece bonus:** Increases Elemental Burst DMG by 25% of Energy Recharge. A 4-piece set bonus of 80% based on 200% ER is achievable. This set directly amplifies her Burst damage, which is her main damage output, and the ER bonus further fuels her damage and energy refund capabilities.

### Artifact Main Stats

*   **Sands:** Energy Recharge (ER) or ATK%
*   **Goblet:** Electro DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG

The choice between ER and ATK% on the Sands depends on her total Energy Recharge. If she reaches the desired ER threshold (see below) with substats and her weapon, ATK% can be preferred for more raw damage. However, ER is generally safer and more impactful due to the "Will of One Strike" energy refund scaling.

### Artifact Substats Priority

1.  CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio)
2.  Energy Recharge (until threshold is met)
3.  ATK%
4.  Flat ATK

## Energy Recharge (ER) Threshold

To ensure consistent Burst uptime for herself and to maximize her energy refund to the team, Raiden Shogun requires a significant amount of Energy Recharge.

*   **Minimum Threshold:** Aim for **250% ER** if using Engulfing Lightning or a team with strong energy generation for her.
*   **Comfortable Threshold:** **270-300% ER** is generally recommended for most teams, especially if relying on her for significant energy restoration or if her teammates have high Burst costs.
*   **Higher ER:** If running a lower-cost Burst weapon like The Catch and not using Engulfing Lightning, or if her teammates have very high Burst costs (e.g., 80 cost), pushing ER beyond 300% can be beneficial for her personal damage and the team's energy refund.

## Team Compositions

Raiden Shogun's versatility allows her to fit into various team archetypes, primarily as a primary Electro DPS, a Burst support, or a Hyperbloom trigger.

### Raiden National

This is one of the most powerful and popular teams in Genshin Impact, featuring Raiden Shogun, Xiangling, Bennett, and Xingqiu.

*   **Roles:**
    *   **Raiden Shogun:** Electro DPS, Burst damage, team energy refund.
    *   **Xiangling:** Off-field Pyro DPS (Pyronado).
    *   **Bennett:** Healer, ATK buffer.
    *   **Xingqiu:** Off-field Hydro application, damage reduction, interruption resistance.
*   **Synergy:** Bennett buffs Xiangling and Raiden. Xingqiu applies Hydro for Vaporize reactions with Xiangling's Pyronado. Raiden triggers Electro-Charged and Overload, and her Burst's energy refund ensures Xiangling and Xingqiu can consistently use their high-cost Bursts. Raiden's Resolve stacks are easily filled due to the high energy costs of Xiangling and Xingqiu's Bursts.

### Raiden Hypercarry

This team focuses on maximizing Raiden Shogun's personal damage by providing extensive buffs and grouping.

*   **Core:** Raiden Shogun, Kujou Sara (C6), Kazuha, Bennett.
*   **Roles:**
    *   **Raiden Shogun:** Main DPS.
    *   **Kujou Sara:** ATK buffer (especially at C6, providing Electro DMG Bonus).
    *   **Kazuha:** Anemo support, crowd control, Elemental DMG Bonus buffer (via Swirl).
    *   **Bennett:** Healer, ATK buffer.
*   **Synergy:** Bennett provides a massive ATK buff. Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Electro DMG. Sara provides a significant ATK buff and Electro DMG Bonus. Raiden Shogun then unleashes her fully buffed Burst for massive damage. This team requires careful rotation management to maximize buff uptime.

### Hyperbloom Trigger

In Dendro-centric teams, Raiden Shogun can act as an excellent Hyperbloom trigger due to her fast Electro application during her Burst.

*   **Example Team:** Nahida (Dendro Archon), Xingqiu, Kazuha, Raiden Shogun.
*   **Roles:**
    *   **Nahida:** Dendro application, buffs.
    *   **Xingqiu:** Hydro application.
    *   **Kazuha:** Swirls Hydro and Electro, buffs.
    *   **Raiden Shogun:** Triggers Hyperbloom with her Electro application.
*   **Synergy:** Nahida and Xingqiu generate Dendro Cores. Raiden Shogun's Electro application from her Skill and Burst rapidly triggers Hyperbloom reactions, dealing significant Bloom DMG to enemies. Her fast Electro application ensures consistent Hyperbloom triggers, and her Burst's energy refund helps maintain team Burst uptime. While not her highest damage output role, it showcases her flexibility.
