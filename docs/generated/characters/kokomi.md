# Kokomi

## Lore

Sangonomiya Kokomi is the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island and the de facto leader of the Sangonomiya Resistance during the Vision Hunt Decree. Despite her young age, she possesses a brilliant strategic mind, orchestrating the military campaign against the Shogunate's forces with remarkable success. Her public persona is that of a gentle, benevolent leader, but this is a carefully constructed facade to maintain morale and inspire her people. In reality, Kokomi is a shrewd tactician burdened by the immense pressure of war and the well-being of her islanders. She often feels isolated by her responsibilities, finding solace in meticulous planning and the quiet contemplation of battle strategies.

Her relationship with Gorou, the General of the Watatsumi Island forces, is one of mutual respect and reliance. Gorou, a loyal and straightforward warrior, trusts Kokomi's strategic acumen implicitly, even when her plans seem unconventional or overly complex. Kokomi, in turn, values Gorou's unwavering dedication and his ability to inspire the troops on the front lines. She often relies on his direct feedback from the battlefield to refine her strategies. However, their differing personalities can sometimes lead to friction; Gorou's directness can clash with Kokomi's more indirect and secretive methods, though they always manage to find common ground for the sake of the Resistance.

Kokomi's opposition to the Shogunate's Vision Hunt Decree stems from her deep-seated desire to protect her people and their way of life. Watatsumi Island, with its unique culture and spiritual connection to the sea, was deeply threatened by the decree, which aimed to seize all Visions in Inazuma. Kokomi saw this as an act of oppression and a direct assault on the freedom and aspirations of Inazuma's citizens. She masterfully leveraged her position as the Divine Priestess, a figure revered by her people, to rally them against the Shogunate. Her strategies were not limited to direct military confrontation; she employed diplomacy, intelligence gathering, and psychological warfare to outmaneuver the Shogunate's forces, often anticipating their moves and exploiting their weaknesses. The immense mental toll of these constant calculations and the weight of leadership are significant, contributing to her frequent need for rest and her tendency to delegate tasks to trusted individuals like Gorou.

## Kit Mechanics

Kokomi's kit revolves around her Hydro application and healing capabilities, with her Elemental Skill and Elemental Burst forming the core of her utility.

### Elemental Skill: Kurage's Oath

Upon activation, Kokomi summons a **Bake-Kurage**, a "Geloo Jellyfish" that floats near the active character. The Bake-Kurage deals Hydro DMG to nearby enemies periodically and, more importantly, **heals the active character based on Kokomi's Max HP**. The healing pulses occur every 2 seconds and have a duration of 12 seconds. The Bake-Kurage's Hydro application is consistent, making it an excellent tool for enabling Hydro-related reactions like Freeze and Electro-Charged. The jellyfish can inherit the position of Kokomi when she uses her Elemental Burst, allowing for continuous healing and Hydro application. The cooldown for Kurage's Oath is 20 seconds.

### Elemental Burst: Nereid's Ascension

Kokomi dons a **Ceremonial Garment**, dealing Hydro DMG to surrounding enemies and **resetting the Bake-Kurage's cooldown**, allowing for immediate re-summoning if it was already active. While in this state, Kokomi's **Normal and Charged Attacks are converted to Hydro DMG**, and their damage scales with her **Max HP**. Crucially, during Nereid's Ascension, Kokomi gains a significant **Healing Bonus** and her **Movement Speed is increased**. The most unique aspect of her Burst is the **negative CRIT Rate** she suffers: her CRIT Rate is **decreased by 100%**, meaning her attacks will never critically hit. However, this is compensated by her Burst's damage scaling off Max HP and the substantial healing bonus she receives. The duration of the Burst is 10 seconds, with a cooldown of 20 seconds and an energy cost of 70.

### Passive Talents

*   **Princess's Descent:** While in the Ceremonial Garment state, Kokomi's Normal Attack DMG is increased based on her Max HP. This talent is key to her damage output during her Burst.
*   **Talent: True to the Master:** When Kokomi's HP is below 50%, her Healing Bonus is increased by 25%, and Hydro DMG Bonus is increased by 15% for 10 seconds. This talent encourages managing her HP, though her kit generally keeps her at high HP.
*   **Passive Talent: Debauchee:** Decreases the Stamina Consumption of her Charged Attacks by 60% and increases incoming healing by 20% when her HP is at 50% or below. This is a less impactful talent for most builds but can offer utility in specific scenarios.

## Builds

Kokomi's build focuses on maximizing her HP and Healing Bonus, with some consideration for Energy Recharge to ensure consistent Burst uptime. Her inherent -100% CRIT Rate makes CRIT stats on artifacts completely useless for her.

### Weapons

*   **Everlasting Moonglow (5-star):** Kokomi's signature weapon. It provides a substantial HP% bonus and, upon hitting enemies with attacks, regenerates Energy for Kokomi. Furthermore, it increases her Normal Attack DMG based on her Max HP, synergizing perfectly with her Burst. This is her BiS weapon.
*   **Prototype Amber (4-star):** A craftable weapon that is an excellent F2P option. It grants HP% and, upon casting her Elemental Burst, regenerates Energy for all party members and heals them over time. This significantly boosts her team utility and helps with energy needs.
*   **Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers (3-star):** A budget-friendly option that provides a massive HP% bonus. When swapped to another character after Kokomi, it grants a significant ATK buff (up to 48% at R5) to that character for 10 seconds. This makes Kokomi a premier buffer in certain team compositions, especially for characters who benefit greatly from ATK buffs.
*   **Other Options:** Sacrificial Fragments (if focusing on Skill damage and cooldown reset), Mappa Mare (for reaction-based teams if not using OHC).

### Artifacts

*   **4-piece Ocean-Hued Clam:** This set is generally considered her best-in-slot for maximizing her overall contribution. The 2-piece bonus grants HP%, and the 4-piece bonus converts a portion of her healing into a Physical DMG instance (up to 30,000 DMG) that explodes after a short delay. This allows Kokomi to deal significant damage even with her negative CRIT Rate, turning her healing into offensive potential.
*   **4-piece Tenacity of the Millelith:** If Kokomi's Bake-Kurage hits enemies consistently, this set provides an 80 ATK and 20 DEF buff to all party members when the skill's damage hits. The 2-piece bonus grants HP%, which is always beneficial. This is a strong option for teams that benefit from ATK buffs and where the Bake-Kurage can reliably hit enemies.
*   **2-piece Tenacity of the Millelith / 2-piece Heart of Depth:** A viable combination for a more balanced build, focusing on HP% and Hydro DMG Bonus.
*   **2-piece Tenacity of the Millelith / 2-piece Maiden Beloved:** Prioritizes healing and HP, useful if pure healing output is the absolute priority.

**Artifact Main Stats:**

*   **Sands:** HP%
*   **Goblet:** HP% (or Hydro DMG Bonus if using 4pc OHC and aiming for damage, but HP% is generally preferred for overall utility)
*   **Circlet:** HP% (or Healing Bonus if using 4pc OHC and prioritizing healing output over damage)

**Substats Priority:** HP% > Energy Recharge > Flat HP

**Energy Recharge Threshold:** Aim for approximately 180-200% ER if she is the sole Hydro applicator and healer, or if her Burst uptime is crucial. If other characters provide energy or her Burst is not strictly required for every rotation, lower ER can be acceptable.

## Role and Team Compositions

Kokomi's primary roles are **premier Hydro applicator** and **dedicated healer**. Her consistent off-field Hydro application from her Elemental Skill makes her invaluable for enabling reactions, particularly Freeze. Her Burst allows her to become a potent on-field Hydro DPS/healer, providing significant healing and Hydro damage while buffing the team.

### Hydro Application

The Bake-Kurage applies Hydro every 2 seconds to enemies within its radius. This consistent application is crucial for maintaining reactions. During her Burst, her Hydro-infused Normal Attacks provide rapid Hydro application, further solidifying her role as a top-tier Hydro enabler.

### Healing

Kokomi's healing scales directly off her Max HP. The Bake-Kurage provides consistent healing to the active character, while her Burst heals the entire party significantly through her Normal Attacks and the passive healing from the Bake-Kurage. Her healing output is so high that she often over-heals, making the damage conversion from Ocean-Hued Clam particularly effective.

### Freeze Teams

Kokomi is a cornerstone of many **Freeze teams**, especially those that do not utilize Mona or Barbara. Her consistent Hydro application from the Bake-Kurage allows Cryo characters to maintain the Frozen status on enemies reliably. When paired with a Cryo DPS like Ayaka or Ganyu, and an Anemo unit for crowd control and VV shred (e.g., Kazuha, Venti, Sucrose), Kokomi provides the necessary Hydro to keep enemies frozen while healing the team. Her Burst can also be used to deal damage and apply Hydro if enemies break free or if she needs to be on-field for a short period.

**Example Freeze Team:**

*   **Cryo DPS:** Kamisato Ayaka / Ganyu
*   **Hydro Support/Healer:** Sangonomiya Kokomi
*   **Anemo Support:** Kaedehara Kazuha / Venti / Anemo Traveler
*   **Flex Slot:** Diona (Shield/Heal/Cryo Resonance), Shenhe (Cryo DMG Buff), Rosaria (CRIT Rate Buff/Cryo Application)

In this setup, Kokomi's Bake-Kurage maintains Hydro aura for Ayaka or Ganyu to freeze enemies. The Anemo unit groups enemies and shreds Hydro RES. Kokomi's healing ensures the team's survivability. If using 4pc Ocean-Hued Clam, her Burst can contribute significant damage.

### Other Team Compositions

*   **Electro-Charged Teams:** Kokomi's Hydro application is excellent for Electro-Charged teams, especially when paired with Fischl, Beidou, or Raiden Shogun. Her healing and Hydro application provide a stable foundation for these reaction-heavy teams.
*   **Taser (Electro-Charged) Team Example:**
    *   **Electro DPS/Sub-DPS:** Raiden Shogun / Yae Miko
    *   **Hydro Applicator/Healer:** Sangonomiya Kokomi
    *   **Electro Support:** Fischl / Beidou
    *   **Anemo Support:** Kazuha / Sucrose
*   **Vaporize Teams (less common):** While not her primary role, Kokomi can provide Hydro for Pyro DPS characters like Hu Tao or Diluc, though characters like Xingqiu or Yelan are often preferred for their damage contribution and Hydro application during their Burst. If Kokomi is built with 4pc OHC, her Burst can contribute damage in these scenarios.
