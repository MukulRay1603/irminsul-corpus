# Baizhu

## Lore and Background

Baizhu is the enigmatic owner and proprietor of Bubu Pharmacy in Liyue Harbor, a prominent establishment known for its potent remedies and rare ingredients. He is a physician of considerable skill, renowned throughout Liyue for his expertise in herbal medicine and his unconventional, often secretive, methods. His calm demeanor and seemingly benevolent facade mask a deep-seated, personal quest that drives his every action: the pursuit of immortality. This ambition is not born of a desire for personal power or longevity for its own sake, but rather to remain by the side of Qiqi, a zombie under his care.

Qiqi, a former apprentice of the pharmacy, was once a victim of a powerful Adeptus's curse, resulting in her undeath and a severe case of amnesia. Baizhu took it upon himself to care for her, a commitment that has spanned years. To prolong Qiqi's existence and mitigate the detrimental effects of her condition, Baizhu has made significant sacrifices. The exact nature of these sacrifices is shrouded in mystery, but it is heavily implied that they involve his own life force or vitality, gradually draining him over time. This personal cost fuels his relentless search for a cure or a method to grant Qiqi true life, or at least a stable, enduring existence.

His constant companion is Changsheng, a large, sentient white snake that coils around his neck and shoulders. Changsheng is more than just a pet; she is an ancient being with her own wisdom and a deep connection to Baizhu. Their relationship is symbiotic, with Changsheng often acting as Baizhu's confidante, advisor, and even a conduit for his more esoteric abilities. She possesses a keen intellect and a pragmatic outlook, often tempering Baizhu's more idealistic or desperate pursuits. Their shared history is long and complex, hinting at a pact or understanding forged in the distant past, further emphasizing Baizhu's unique position within Liyue's more mystical circles. Baizhu's pursuit of immortality is a central theme in his character, showcasing his unwavering devotion to Qiqi and the profound lengths he will go to protect those he cares about, even at immense personal cost.

## Combat Kit and Mechanics

Baizhu's combat kit revolves around providing potent healing, Dendro application, and defensive utility through his unique mechanics. His Elemental Skill, **Universal Diagnosis**, and Elemental Burst, **Holistic Revivification**, work in tandem to achieve this.

### Elemental Skill: Universal Diagnosis

**Universal Diagnosis** is an instant cast ability that deals Dendro DMG to opponents in front of Baizhu. It then generates a Seamless Shield that absorbs Dendro DMG with 250% efficiency. This shield has a duration of 12 seconds and a cooldown of 10 seconds. Crucially, after casting, Baizhu unleashes a Spirit-Spinning attack that will launch 3 Spirit-Spinning attacks, each dealing Dendro DMG based on Baizhu's Max HP. These attacks will home in on opponents within a certain range, prioritizing those affected by a Burning, Bloom, Hyperbloom, or Quicken aura. Each Spirit-Spinning attack has a chance to generate a Dendro Co-op, which can trigger off-field Dendro application. The damage scaling of Universal Diagnosis is based on Baizhu's Max HP, making HP investment crucial for both his offensive and defensive capabilities.

### Elemental Burst: Holistic Revivification

**Holistic Revivification** is Baizhu's signature ability, providing substantial healing and continuous Dendro application. Upon activation, it heals all nearby party members for 5% of Baizhu's Max HP plus a flat amount (300 at talent level 10). This healing occurs at the start of the Burst and then every 2.5 seconds for a duration of 14 seconds.

The core of Holistic Revivification lies in its **Gossamer Sprite** mechanic. For the duration of the Burst, a Gossamer Sprite will exist. When Baizhu's Normal Attacks, Elemental Skills, or Elemental Bursts hit opponents, the Gossamer Sprite will initiate a Seamless Revivification, healing all nearby party members for 5% of Baizhu's Max HP plus a flat amount (60 at talent level 10) and dealing Dendro DMG. This healing and DMG effect can occur up to 7 times, with each instance having a cooldown of 2.5 seconds. The Spirit-Spinning attacks from Universal Diagnosis also contribute to triggering these Seamless Revivifications.

Furthermore, the Seamless Shields generated by Universal Diagnosis are enhanced by Holistic Revivification. When a Seamless Shield absorbs damage, its strength is increased by 20% for 6 seconds. This stacking effect can occur once every 6 seconds, providing a significant defensive boost when Baizhu's shields are active and he is actively triggering his Burst's effects. The combination of continuous healing, Dendro application, and shield enhancement makes Holistic Revivification a powerful tool for survivability and enabling Dendro-based reactions.

### Passive Talents

Baizhu's passive talents further enhance his supportive capabilities.

*   **All Things Must Pass** (Ascension 1): When Holistic Revivification is used, the DMG dealt by its Seamless Revivification attacks will be increased based on 8% of Baizhu's Max HP. This further amplifies his Dendro DMG output when his Burst is active.
*   **Second Measure** (Ascension 4): When Baizhu's Seamless Shields absorb damage, his Healing Bonus is increased by 8% for 6 seconds. This effect can be triggered once every 10 seconds. This synergy between his shields and healing further bolsters his role as a premier healer. Additionally, when a character under the effect of a Seamless Shield attacks, Baizhu's ATK SPD is increased by 10% for 5 seconds. This minor offensive buff can be beneficial in certain team compositions.
*   **Purity of Spirit** (Utility Passive): Displays the location of nearby resources unique to Liyue on the mini-map. When Baizhu uses the Pharmacy skill, he consumes 15% less HP. This is a quality-of-life passive that aids in exploration and resource gathering within Liyue.

## Best Builds and Artifacts

Baizhu's build primarily focuses on maximizing his Max HP to enhance his healing, shield strength, and Dendro damage. Crit Rate and Crit DMG are secondary considerations, especially if aiming for more offensive capabilities through his Dendro application. Energy Recharge is important to ensure consistent Burst uptime, as Holistic Revivification is central to his kit.

### Weapons

*   **Jadefall's Splendor (5-Star):** Baizhu's signature weapon is his Best-in-Slot. It provides a significant Max HP% bonus and, upon casting an Elemental Skill or Burst, grants the wearer a continuous healing effect. When the wearer's HP is above 80%, they gain the **Sea-Dyed Foam** effect, which increases Elemental Burst DMG by 0.008% of their Max HP. This effect can be triggered once every 14 seconds and can be stacked up to 30 times. The weapon's passive directly synergizes with Baizhu's HP scaling and healing capabilities.
*   **Prototype Amber (4-Star Craftable):** This is an excellent Free-to-Play (F2P) option. It provides a substantial Max HP% bonus. More importantly, upon casting an Elemental Burst, it regenerates 6 Energy for all party members every 2 seconds for 6 seconds, and the active character gains 0.8% HP every 2 seconds for 6 seconds. This helps with team-wide energy needs and provides additional minor healing.
*   **Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers (3-Star):** A budget-friendly option that significantly boosts the ATK of the next character to switch in after Baizhu uses his Elemental Skill. While it doesn't directly benefit Baizhu's personal stats, it provides a powerful team buff, making it a viable choice in reaction-focused teams where the buffed character is the primary damage dealer.
*   **Favonius Codex (4-Star):** If energy needs are a significant concern for the team, Favonius Codex can be considered. It provides Energy Recharge and has a chance to generate Elemental Particles upon landing critical hits, helping to fuel Baizhu's own Burst or the Bursts of other team members.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-piece Deepwood Memories:** This is Baizhu's premier artifact set for his role as a Dendro support. It provides a 15% Dendro DMG Bonus with its 2-piece effect. The 4-piece effect, when an Elemental Skill or Burst hits an opponent, the DMG dealt by the attack is reduced by 30% for 4 seconds. This effect can be triggered even if Baizhu is off-field. This set is crucial for maximizing the damage of Dendro-related reactions like Bloom, Hyperbloom, and Quicken.
*   **4-piece Tenacity of the Millelith:** If the team composition benefits more from Baizhu's shielding and HP-boosting capabilities, this set can be considered. Its 2-piece effect grants a 20% Max HP bonus. The 4-piece effect triggers when an Elemental Skill hits an opponent, increasing the ATK of all nearby party members by 20% and their Shield Strength by 30% for 3 seconds. This effect can be triggered once every 0.5 seconds and can still be triggered even if Baizhu is off-field. This set is particularly useful in teams that rely on sustained damage and strong shields.
*   **2-piece Tenacity of the Millelith / 2-piece Ocean-Hued Clam / 2-piece Vourukasha's Glow:** A combination of sets that focus on HP bonuses. 2pc Tenacity or 2pc Vourukasha's Glow provides a 20% Max HP bonus. 2pc Ocean-Hued Clam converts a portion of healing into damage, which can be beneficial if Baizhu is healing significantly. This option is good for maximizing Baizhu's personal healing output.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** HP%
*   **Goblet:** HP%
*   **Circlet:** HP% (for maximum healing and shield strength) or Healing Bonus% (if HP is sufficiently high and more healing is desired)
*   **Substats:** HP%, Energy Recharge%, Crit Rate%, Crit DMG%

Energy Recharge needs can vary depending on the team composition and weapon choice, but generally aiming for around 180-200% ER is recommended for consistent Burst uptime.

## Role in Teams

Baizhu functions primarily as a premier **Healer and Dendro Enabler** in various Dendro reaction teams. His unique kit allows him to provide substantial healing, consistent off-field Dendro application, and a degree of defensive utility through his shields, all while scaling off Max HP.

*   **Bloom/Hyperbloom/Burgeon Teams:** Baizhu's consistent Dendro application is vital for generating Dendro Cores. His healing ensures the on-field character survives the self-damage from Bloom or the explosive damage from Burgeon. His shields can also mitigate some of the damage. He pairs exceptionally well with Hydro characters like Xingqiu, Yelan, or Kokomi, and Electro characters like Raiden Shogun or Kuki Shinobu for Hyperbloom.
*   **Quicken/Aggravate/Spread Teams:** In teams focused on the Quicken reaction, Baizhu's Dendro application helps maintain the Quicken aura. His healing is crucial for characters who might take damage from enemy attacks or from their own abilities (e.g., Electro characters using their Burst). He works well with Electro characters like Fischl, Yae Miko, or Keqing, and Anemo characters like Kazuha or Sucrose for crowd control and elemental damage buffs.
*   **Nilou Bloom Teams:** Baizhu is an excellent choice for Nilou's Bountiful Core teams. His healing is essential to counteract the significant damage taken from Bountiful Cores, and his Dendro application ensures the constant generation of these cores. His shields also provide a layer of protection.

Baizhu's seamless healing and shield mechanics mean he can often fulfill multiple roles simultaneously, making him a highly valuable and versatile addition to many Dendro-focused team compositions. His ability to provide healing without requiring the player to actively switch to him to use his healing abilities is a significant quality-of-life improvement.
