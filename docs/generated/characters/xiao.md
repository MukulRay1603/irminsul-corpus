# Xiao

## Lore

Xiao, whose true name is Alatus, is a Yaksha, a warrior race bound to the service of the Geo Archon, Morax (Rex Lapis). He is the last surviving member of the five Yaksha Yaksha Guardians, a group tasked with eradicating demons and protecting Liyue. Xiao's existence spans over two millennia, a period marked by relentless combat and the immense suffering that comes with it. He has personally slain countless demons, a brutal and often soul-crushing endeavor that has left him with deep karmic debt. This debt manifests as a constant, gnawing pain and a burden of guilt, a consequence of the violent nature of his duties and the loss of his brethren.

The other four Yaksha Guardians – Indarias, Nepenthes, Kipatsu, and Menogias – all perished in their service, leaving Xiao as the sole inheritor of their legacy and their unfulfilled duties. Their deaths weigh heavily on him, fueling his solitary nature and his reluctance to form close bonds. He sees himself as a protector who can never truly find peace, forever haunted by the ghosts of his past and the endless cycle of violence. This internal torment is a defining aspect of his character, shaping his stoic demeanor and his preference for isolation.

Xiao's relationship with Morax is one of deep respect and loyalty, though tinged with the melancholy of their shared history and the sacrifices made. Morax, recognizing the immense suffering Xiao endures, has always shown him a degree of understanding and offered him solace, even if it is in the form of continued duty. However, it is the Traveler, the anomaly from another world, who offers Xiao a rare glimpse of genuine connection and a respite from his eternal burden. The Traveler's unwavering presence and acceptance, unburdened by the ancient conflicts of Teyvat, provides Xiao with a sense of peace he has not known for centuries. This burgeoning friendship is a significant development for Xiao, hinting at the possibility of healing and finding a purpose beyond his endless fight.

## Kit Mechanics

Xiao is a 5-star Anemo Polearm user renowned for his exceptional Plunging Attack damage and his unique Anemo DPS identity, largely independent of elemental reactions. His kit revolves around his Elemental Skill, **Lemniscatic Wind Cycling**, and his Elemental Burst, **Bane of All Evil**.

### Lemniscatic Wind Cycling (Elemental Skill)

Lemniscatic Wind Cycling is a swift, forward-lunging Anemo attack that deals Anemo DMG to enemies in its path. Crucially, it can be used twice consecutively in quick succession, allowing for rapid repositioning and damage application. The first cast has a cooldown of 10 seconds, and the second cast shares this cooldown. When used mid-air, it initiates a Plunging Attack upon completion. This skill is vital for Xiao's mobility and for generating energy particles, especially when paired with his Burst. The damage scales with Xiao's ATK.

### Bane of All Evil (Elemental Burst)

Bane of All Evil is the core of Xiao's offensive capability. Upon activation, Xiao dons his Yaksha mask, entering the Yaksha Mask state. In this state:
*   His Normal, Charged, and Plunging Attacks are converted to deal **Anemo DMG**. This conversion makes him a pure Anemo DPS, meaning he does not benefit from elemental infusions from characters like Bennett or Chongyun.
*   His **Plunging Attack DMG is significantly increased**. This is the primary source of his damage.
*   His **ATK is increased by 25%**.
*   His **jumping ability is greatly enhanced**, allowing him to leap to incredible heights.

However, this immense power comes at a cost. While the Yaksha Mask is active, Xiao continuously loses HP. This HP drain is **2.5% of his Max HP per 0.5 seconds**, totaling 5% of his Max HP per second. This mechanic necessitates a strong healer or shielder in his team to sustain his damage output over extended periods. The duration of Bane of All Evil is 15 seconds, with a cooldown of 18 seconds. The energy cost for the Burst is 70.

### Passive Talents

*   **"Conqueror of Evil: White Clouds at Dawn"**: This passive talent enhances Lemniscatic Wind Cycling. Using the skill in mid-air causes it to trigger a Plunging Attack. This synergizes directly with his Burst, allowing for immediate high-damage Plunging Attacks after casting his skill.
*   **"Transcendence: Gravity Ning"**: This talent further buffs Xiao's damage. While under the effects of Bane of All Evil, his Plunging Attack DMG is increased by an additional 35%.
*   **"Aeolia's Embrace"**: This talent provides a party-wide Elemental DMG Bonus. For every 2 seconds Xiao spends on the field while under the effect of Bane of All Evil, his Elemental DMG Bonus is increased by 4%, stacking up to 5 times for a maximum of 20% Elemental DMG Bonus. This bonus is only applied after the Burst has been activated.

Xiao's damage scaling is heavily reliant on his ATK, CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, and Anemo DMG Bonus. Due to his HP drain mechanic, having a high Max HP pool is also beneficial as it increases his survivability and the base damage of his Plunging Attacks (which scale with ATK, but the HP drain is a percentage of Max HP).

## Builds

Xiao's build focuses on maximizing his ATK, CRIT stats, and Anemo DMG Bonus, while also considering survivability due to his HP drain.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapons

1.  **Staff of Homa**: This 5-star Polearm is widely considered Xiao's BiS. It provides a massive CRIT DMG substat and a significant ATK boost. Its passive grants an ATK bonus based on the wielder's Max HP, and an additional ATK bonus when HP is below 50%. This synergizes perfectly with Xiao's HP drain mechanic, as it allows him to benefit from the lower HP ATK bonus while still being able to sustain himself.
2.  **Calamity Queller**: Another strong 5-star option, Calamity Queller offers a high Base ATK and an ATK% substat. Its passive grants an Elemental DMG Bonus after using an Elemental Skill, which stacks over time while the character is on the field. This provides a consistent damage boost for Xiao's Burst duration.

### Strong 4-Star Options

1.  **Deathmatch**: This Battle Pass exclusive 4-star Polearm provides a substantial CRIT Rate substat, which is crucial for Xiao. Its passive grants an ATK bonus based on the number of enemies present, making it versatile for both single-target and AoE scenarios.
2.  **Missive Windspear**: A free event weapon from the "Windblume's Breath" event, this weapon offers ATK% and an Elemental Mastery buff after triggering an Elemental Reaction. While Xiao doesn't rely on reactions, the ATK% is valuable, and the EM buff can be situationally useful if he's part of a team that can trigger Swirls.
3.  **Blackcliff Pole**: This Starglitter shop Polearm provides CRIT DMG as a substat. Its passive grants an ATK bonus after defeating enemies, which can be beneficial in AoE situations. However, its passive is less consistent than other options.

### Free-to-Play (F2P) Options

1.  **Favonius Lance**: This 4-star weapon provides Energy Recharge as a substat, which can help Xiao reach his Burst uptime more consistently, especially if his team lacks strong Anemo battery support. Its passive generates additional Energy Particles for the team upon CRIT hits.
2.  **Prototype Starglitter**: This craftable 4-star weapon offers Energy Recharge and increases Normal Attack DMG after using an Elemental Skill. While the Normal Attack DMG bonus is less impactful for Xiao compared to his Plunging Attacks, the ER substat is valuable for Burst uptime.

### Artifact Sets

1.  **4-piece Vermillion Hereafter**: This set is tailor-made for Xiao. Its 2-piece bonus grants ATK%, and its 4-piece bonus significantly increases his ATK after using an Elemental Skill, with further buffs stacking when his HP decreases. This set provides the highest potential damage output for Xiao.
2.  **4-piece Marechaussee Hunter**: This set offers a CRIT Rate bonus, making it easier to achieve a good CRIT ratio. Its 4-piece bonus further increases CRIT DMG when the wielder's HP changes. This set is excellent for Xiao, especially when paired with a healer that can consistently adjust his HP, like Baizhu or Jean.
3.  **2-piece Gladiator's Finale / 2-piece Shimenawa's Reminiscence / 2-piece Vermillion Hereafter + 2-piece ATK% set**: A combination of 2-piece bonuses that grant ATK% or Anemo DMG Bonus can be a viable option if a full 4-piece set is not available. For example, 2pc Vermillion Hereafter + 2pc Gladiator's Finale provides a substantial ATK boost.

**Artifact Main Stats:**
*   **Sands**: ATK% or Energy Recharge (if ER issues)
*   **Goblet**: Anemo DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet**: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (depending on substats)

**Artifact Substats Priority:** CRIT DMG > CRIT Rate > ATK% > Energy Recharge.

## Team Compositions

Xiao's unique Anemo DPS identity and his HP drain mechanic necessitate specific team compositions that focus on amplifying his damage and ensuring his survivability. He generally does not benefit from reactions like Vaporize or Melt, making teams centered around Swirl and Anemo DMG amplification more effective.

### Core Roles

*   **Anemo Battery/Support**: Provides energy for Xiao's Burst and can offer additional buffs or crowd control.
*   **Healer/Shielder**: Crucial for mitigating Xiao's HP drain and keeping him alive.
*   **Off-field Support/Buffer**: Characters who can provide buffs or deal damage off-field to maximize Xiao's limited on-field time during his Burst.

### Example Team Compositions

1.  **Xiao, Faruzan, Bennett, Zhongli**: This is a highly potent and popular team.
    *   **Faruzan (C6 especially)**: Provides Anemo RES Shred and Anemo DMG Bonus through her Elemental Skill and Burst. Her C6 is a significant damage increase for Xiao. She also acts as an Anemo battery.
    *   **Bennett**: Provides a massive ATK buff with his Elemental Burst and healing. While Xiao's attacks are Anemo, Bennett's buff still increases his ATK, which is a primary scaling stat.
    *   **Zhongli**: Offers an unbreakable shield, providing Xiao with immense survivability and preventing interruption during his Plunging Attacks. His shield also provides universal RES Shred.
    *   **Strategy**: Activate Zhongli's shield, Bennett's Burst, Faruzan's Burst, then Xiao's Burst and spam Plunging Attacks.

2.  **Xiao, Faruzan, Jean, Zhongli**: A strong Anemo-centric team.
    *   **Jean**: Provides healing and can act as an Anemo battery. Her Burst can heal Xiao and reset his Plunging Attack sequence if needed. She also offers some crowd control with her Burst.
    *   **Zhongli**: As above, provides shielding and RES Shred.
    *   **Faruzan**: As above, provides Anemo buffs and RES Shred.
    *   **Strategy**: Similar to the Bennett team, focusing on Anemo synergy and survivability.

3.  **Xiao, Faruzan, Albedo, Zhongli**: A team focused on consistent damage and survivability.
    *   **Albedo**: Provides consistent off-field Geo DMG and creates Elemental Skill constructs that can resonate with Anemo Swirls. His Elemental Burst also provides an Elemental Mastery buff.
    *   **Zhongli**: Provides shielding and RES Shred.
    *   **Faruzan**: Provides Anemo buffs and RES Shred.
    *   **Strategy**: This team relies on the synergy between Xiao's on-field damage and Albedo's off-field damage, with Zhongli ensuring safety.

4.  **Xiao, Sucrose, Bennett, Diona**: A more accessible F2P-friendly option.
    *   **Sucrose**: Can act as an Anemo battery and provide Elemental Mastery buffs (less relevant for Xiao directly but can boost Swirls) and crowd control. Her Constellations can also provide Anemo RES Shred.
    *   **Bennett**: Provides ATK buff and healing.
    *   **Diona**: Provides a shield and healing, offering crucial survivability.
    *   **Strategy**: This team relies on Sucrose for energy and crowd control, Bennett for ATK buffs and healing, and Diona for shielding and additional healing.

**Key Considerations for Team Building:**
*   **Energy Recharge**: Xiao needs around 130-140% ER if he is the sole Anemo character and doesn't have Favonius weapons on the team. With an Anemo battery like Faruzan or Sucrose, this requirement can be lowered.
*   **Survivability**: A strong healer or shielder is non-negotiable due to Xiao's HP drain. Zhongli's shield is highly recommended for uninterrupted Plunging Attacks.
*   **Buffs**: Characters who can provide ATK buffs (Bennett) or Elemental DMG buffs (Faruzan) are essential for maximizing Xiao's damage.

## Unique Anemo DPS Identity

Xiao stands out as one of the few characters in Genshin Impact who functions primarily as a pure Anemo DPS, largely independent of elemental reactions. Unlike characters like Hu Tao or Xiangling who rely on Vaporize or Melt, Xiao's damage output is primarily derived from his own Anemo damage, amplified by his Plunging Attacks during his Elemental Burst.

His kit is designed around maximizing the damage of these Anemo Plunging Attacks. The **Bane of All Evil** Burst not only converts his attacks to Anemo but also significantly boosts his Plunging Attack DMG and his jumping height, allowing him to chain devastating aerial assaults. This makes him exceptionally effective against groups of enemies, as his Plunging Attacks can hit multiple targets simultaneously, and his enhanced mobility allows him to reposition quickly.

The reliance on his own Anemo damage means that while he can trigger Swirl reactions, these are secondary to his direct damage output. Swirling other elements can provide some AoE elemental application and minor damage, but Xiao's core strength lies in the raw Anemo damage he deals. This self-sufficiency makes him a reliable damage dealer in various team compositions, as he doesn't require specific elemental enablers to function optimally. However, it also means that his damage ceiling is somewhat limited compared to reaction-based DPS characters unless supported by powerful Anemo-specific buffs, such as those provided by Faruzan.

The HP drain mechanic is a defining feature that forces a specific playstyle and team-building approach. Players must actively manage Xiao's health, ensuring he has sufficient healing or shielding to sustain his damage output over the 15-second duration of his Burst. This creates a dynamic gameplay loop where players balance aggressive damage dealing with careful resource management.

In essence, Xiao's unique identity lies in his role as a high-flying, self-contained Anemo DPS who excels at dealing consistent, high damage through his enhanced Plunging Attacks, albeit at the cost of his own HP. His gameplay is characterized by aerial combat, rapid attacks, and the strategic use of his Burst to decimate enemies.
