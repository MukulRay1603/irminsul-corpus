# Clorinde

## Lore

Clorinde is the Champion Duelist of Fontaine, a title that signifies her unparalleled skill in combat and her role as a protector of the nation. Her upbringing was marked by hardship and a profound, almost familial bond with Navia, the current leader of the Spina de Rosula. Clorinde was orphaned at a young age and taken in by Navia's family, developing a deep sense of loyalty and a protective instinct towards Navia. This bond is a cornerstone of Clorinde's character, driving many of her actions and her unwavering dedication to Fontaine's well-being.

A significant aspect of Clorinde's past is the "demonic pact" etched into her left arm. This pact, the specifics of which are not fully detailed but implied to be a source of immense power, came with a heavy price. It is a constant burden, a physical manifestation of a difficult choice or a dangerous bargain made in her youth. Clorinde lives with this pact not as a source of shame, but as a testament to her resilience and her willingness to bear any weight for the sake of her duty and those she cares about. The power derived from it is channeled through her combat prowess, making her a formidable opponent. This internal struggle and the power it grants contribute to her unique combat style and her stoic demeanor.

Her personality is characterized by extreme loyalty, precision, and a reserved, almost aloof, demeanor. Clorinde is a woman of few words, preferring action and demonstration over lengthy explanations. Her precision extends beyond her combat skills; she approaches every task with meticulous planning and execution, leaving no room for error. This dedication to perfection, coupled with her inherent sense of justice, makes her a formidable force for good in Fontaine, even if her methods can sometimes appear cold or detached. She embodies the Fontainian ideal of upholding justice, albeit through her own unique and often solitary path. Her experiences have forged her into a pragmatic and resolute individual, always prepared for the worst and striving for the best possible outcome, often at personal cost.

## Kit Mechanics

Clorinde's combat kit revolves around her unique "Hunter's Vigil" state, which is activated by her Elemental Skill, **"Impale the Night."** When activated, Clorinde enters the **"Hunt"** state, during which her Normal Attacks are infused with Electro and gain enhanced properties. Crucially, during this state, her **"Bond"** state is also active, allowing her to perform **"Hunter's Vigil"** attacks. These attacks consume **"Bond of Life"** to increase their damage. The higher the Bond of Life percentage, the greater the damage bonus. This mechanic necessitates careful management of her Bond of Life, often achieved through her Elemental Burst or specific team compositions.

Her Elemental Skill, **"Impale the Night,"** has two distinct uses. The first is a swift dash forward, dealing Electro DMG to enemies in her path. Upon reaching her destination, she enters the aforementioned **"Hunt"** state. The second use of the skill, when in the **"Hunt"** state, allows her to perform a powerful **"Piercing Gaze"** attack, which deals Electro DMG based on her ATK and the current Bond of Life percentage. This attack can heal Clorinde if it hits an opponent, with the healing amount also scaling with her Bond of Life. The cooldown for the initial dash is 12 seconds, and the cooldown for the **"Piercing Gaze"** attack is 8 seconds.

Clorinde's Elemental Burst, **"Ondes Dansantes,"** is a rapid series of attacks that deal Electro DMG in a wide area. This burst is crucial for both damage output and for replenishing her Bond of Life. Upon activation, Clorinde performs a swift, multi-hit attack, culminating in a final blow. The Burst restores a significant amount of Bond of Life to Clorinde, directly empowering her subsequent **"Hunter's Vigil"** attacks. The energy cost of her Burst is 60, and its cooldown is 15 seconds. The damage scaling of her Burst is based on her ATK.

The synergy between her Skill and Burst is paramount. The Burst provides the necessary Bond of Life to maximize the damage of her enhanced Normal Attacks and the **"Piercing Gaze"** attack within the **"Hunt"** state. Players must learn to weave these abilities together effectively, ensuring that her Bond of Life is consistently high when she is actively attacking. The **"Hunter's Vigil"** state lasts for 8 seconds, and the **"Hunt"** state lasts for 12 seconds, allowing for extended periods of Electro-infused damage.

## Builds

Clorinde's optimal build focuses on maximizing her ATK, CRIT Rate, and CRIT DMG, with a secondary emphasis on Elemental Mastery for reaction-based teams. Her unique mechanics, particularly the Bond of Life scaling on her attacks, make certain artifact sets and weapons exceptionally powerful.

### Signature Weapon

Her signature weapon, **Absolution**, is her Best-in-Slot (BiS). Absolution provides a substantial CRIT DMG substat, which is invaluable for her damage output. Its passive further enhances her ATK and, more importantly, increases the DMG dealt by her Normal Attacks based on her Bond of Life. This directly synergizes with her kit, making it the premier choice for maximizing her potential. The weapon's passive also grants a DMG bonus when the wielder's Bond of Life changes, further amplifying her damage during combat rotations.

### Artifact Sets

The **4-piece Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy** set is Clorinde's BiS artifact set. This set provides a significant ATK bonus and, crucially, increases the DMG dealt by Normal Attacks, Elemental Skills, and Elemental Bursts when the wearer's Bond of Life increases or decreases. This effect can stack up to 3 times and lasts for 6 seconds. Given that Clorinde's Burst replenishes her Bond of Life and her enhanced Normal Attacks consume it, this set perfectly complements her combat loop, providing a massive damage boost during her active on-field time.

A strong alternative for players who do not have access to Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy is the **4-piece Thundering Fury** set. This set is particularly effective in Electro-related reaction teams, such as Aggravate or Quickbloom. It increases Electro DMG dealt by 15% and reduces the cooldown of Elemental Skills when triggering Electro-related reactions. This allows Clorinde to use her Elemental Skill more frequently, potentially increasing her overall damage output and enabling more consistent application of Electro.

For a more accessible option, a **2-piece ATK% set (e.g., Gladiator's Finale, Shimenawa's Reminiscence)** combined with a **2-piece Electro DMG Bonus set (e.g., Thundering Fury, Heart of Depth)** can be a viable budget build. While not as potent as the dedicated sets, this combination offers a solid foundation for her ATK and Electro damage.

### Weapon Rankings

1.  **Absolution (5-Star Signature):** BiS due to its CRIT DMG substat and Bond of Life-scaling passive that directly buffs her Normal Attacks.
2.  **Mistsplitter Reforged (5-Star):** Offers CRIT DMG and an Elemental DMG Bonus, making it a strong generalist option.
3.  **Primordial Jade Cutter (5-Star):** Provides CRIT Rate and an ATK bonus based on HP, which can be indirectly beneficial.
4.  **Harbinger of Dawn (3-Star):** A surprisingly strong 3-star option, especially at high refinement. It offers CRIT Rate and, when HP is above 90%, significantly increases CRIT DMG. This requires careful HP management.
5.  **Amenoma Kageuchi (4-Star Craftable):** Offers ATK% and a passive that grants Energy after using an Elemental Skill, which can help with Burst uptime.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** ATK% or Elemental Mastery (if in a strong reaction team)
*   **Goblet:** Electro DMG Bonus
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio)
*   **Substats:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Elemental Mastery

## Team Compositions

Clorinde excels in teams that can consistently trigger Electro-related reactions, particularly Aggravate and Quickbloom, due to her high Electro application and damage potential. Her ability to deal significant damage while on-field makes her a potent main DPS.

### Aggravate/Quickbloom Teams

Clorinde functions exceptionally well as the on-field Electro DPS in Aggravate and Quickbloom teams. These teams leverage the Dendro element to create Quicken and Bloom reactions, which are then amplified by Electro (Aggravate) or benefit from Electro application (Quickbloom).

**Core Components:**

*   **On-Field Electro DPS:** Clorinde
*   **Dendro Applicator:** Nahida is the premier choice due to her consistent off-field Dendro application and the significant Elemental Mastery buff she provides to the active character. Other options include Dendro Traveler or Collei.
*   **Off-Field Electro Support/Sub-DPS:** Fischl or Yae Miko can provide additional Electro application and damage, further enabling Aggravate procs and benefiting from Electro Resonance.
*   **Flex Slot:** This slot can be filled by another Electro character for resonance and additional damage, a defensive unit like a shielder (e.g., Zhongli, Layla) for survivability, or a healer (e.g., Kuki Shinobu, Jean) to sustain the team. Kuki Shinobu is particularly valuable in Quickbloom teams as she can heal and trigger Hyperbloom if needed, though Clorinde's own healing from her Burst can sometimes suffice.

**Example Team Comp (Aggravate):**

*   **Clorinde** (On-field Electro DPS)
*   **Nahida** (Off-field Dendro Applicator/Buffer)
*   **Fischl** (Off-field Electro Sub-DPS/Battery)
*   **Kazuha** (Anemo Support/Buffer/Crowd Control) - Kazuha's ability to group enemies and buff Electro DMG is invaluable. Alternatively, Jean can provide healing and VV shred.

**Example Team Comp (Quickbloom):**

*   **Clorinde** (On-field Electro DPS)
*   **Nahida** (Off-field Dendro Applicator/Buffer)
*   **Xingqiu** (Off-field Hydro Applicator) - Xingqiu's consistent Hydro application is crucial for Bloom generation, and his damage reduction and interruption resistance are beneficial.
*   **Kuki Shinobu** (Healer/Electro Trigger for Hyperbloom) - While Clorinde can trigger Quickbloom, Kuki ensures survivability and can convert excess Dendro Cores into Hyperbloom damage if the team composition leans that way.

In these teams, Clorinde's role is to be on the field for her damage window, utilizing her **"Hunter's Vigil"** state to deal significant Electro damage. Nahida's Dendro application enables the Quicken aura, and Fischl/Yae Miko/Xingqiu provide consistent off-field elemental application to maintain the reaction. Kazuha or other supports can further enhance Clorinde's damage through buffs and crowd control. The high Elemental Mastery provided by Nahida benefits Clorinde's damage in Aggravate reactions.

### Other Potential Compositions

While Aggravate and Quickbloom are her strongest niches, Clorinde can also be considered in teams that focus on raw Electro damage or Superconduct if paired with Cryo units for physical damage reduction, though this is less optimal for her kit. However, her reliance on Bond of Life mechanics and her Electro infusion make reaction-based teams her most potent and synergistic environments. Teams that can reliably generate Hydro or Dendro are key to unlocking her full potential.
