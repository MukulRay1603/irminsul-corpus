# Yelan

## Lore and Background

Yelan serves as the mysterious Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, one of the seven prominent figures governing the bustling port city of Liyue. While her official title places her among the city's most influential leaders, her true activities lie far beyond the council chambers. Yelan operates as a clandestine agent, a master of espionage and intelligence gathering, working under the guise of a Ministry of Civil Affairs official. Her responsibilities involve delving into secrets, navigating the underworld, and maintaining a delicate balance of power through information and subtle manipulation.

Her background is shrouded in deliberate ambiguity, fueling speculation and intrigue. It is known that she possesses a deep understanding of Liyue's underbelly, having cultivated a network of informants and contacts across all strata of society. This allows her to anticipate threats, uncover conspiracies, and gather crucial intelligence that often benefits Liyue's stability, albeit through unconventional means. Her motivations are complex; while she demonstrably acts in Liyue's best interest, her methods are often morally gray, and her ultimate goals remain her own. She is a pragmatist, prioritizing effectiveness and discretion above all else.

Yelan's connection to the Ministry of Civil Affairs is a carefully constructed facade, providing her with a legitimate cover for her investigations. This position grants her access to official records and allows her to move through Liyue with a degree of impunity. However, her true allegiance and the extent of her authority are subjects of much speculation. She is known to be exceptionally skilled in combat, utilizing her Hydro Vision with lethal precision, and possesses an uncanny ability to disappear and reappear at will, further cementing her enigmatic reputation. Her interactions with other characters often reveal glimpses of her sharp wit, calculating nature, and a hidden, perhaps even playful, side that surfaces only in rare moments.

## Kit Mechanics

Yelan's combat kit revolves around her off-field Hydro application and a unique ramping damage buff, making her an exceptional Hydro enabler and damage amplifier.

### Elemental Skill: Lingering Lifeline

Yelan's Elemental Skill, **Lingering Lifeline**, allows her to rapidly move and entangle enemies. Upon activation, she enters a "Breakthrough" state and throws a Hydro-infused dice. This dice rapidly moves, dealing Hydro DMG to enemies it touches. Yelan can control the direction of the dice's movement during this period. After the dice's duration expires, it explodes, dealing Hydro DMG based on Yelan's Max HP.

The skill has two charges, allowing for more frequent use and Hydro application. The movement phase of Lingering Lifeline can be used to reposition Yelan or dodge attacks. The damage dealt during the movement phase is considered Elemental Skill damage, while the explosion damage is considered Elemental Skill damage. The dice's movement speed increases over its duration, and it can entangle enemies, pulling them towards its final location before exploding. The cooldown for each charge is 16 seconds.

### Elemental Burst: Depth-Clarion Dice

Yelan's Elemental Burst, **Depth-Clarion Dice**, is the cornerstone of her off-field damage and buffing capabilities. Upon activation, she deals Hydro DMG to all adjacent enemies and enters the "Concord with Water" state. In this state, Yelan's Normal Attacks are converted into Hydro DMG and deal Hydro DMG based on her Max HP. This effect triggers every second.

More importantly, the Burst summons an **Exquisite Throw** that follows the active character and attacks enemies. The Exquisite Throw initiates an attack when the active character uses a Normal Attack, dealing Hydro DMG based on Yelan's Max HP. It can also trigger independently when no Normal Attacks are used, attacking every 3 seconds. The Exquisite Throw's Hydro DMG is considered Elemental Burst damage.

The **Exquisite Throw** is the primary source of Yelan's off-field Hydro application. Crucially, the Burst also provides a ramping damage bonus to the active character. This buff, known as **Rampant Waterside**, increases the active character's DMG dealt by 1% to 50% over the 15-second duration of the Burst. The damage bonus increases by approximately 3.5% every second, reaching its maximum at around 15 seconds. This means characters who can consistently deal damage throughout the Burst's duration will benefit the most from the full buff.

### Passive Talents

*   **Adaptability:** When characters affected by the **Concord with Water** state deal damage, Yelan's own DMG dealt increases by a percentage of her Max HP. This passive further enhances Yelan's personal damage output.
*   **Calculated Signs:** The dice created by Lingering Lifeline deal 10% more DMG for each character in the party who doesn't exceed 30% HP. This passive is situational and generally less impactful than her other talents.
*   **The Rule of Fortune Implements:** Increases the party's CRIT DMG by 12% for 20s after using Lingering Lifeline. This is a significant buff to the team's overall damage potential.

## Builds

Yelan's builds heavily emphasize HP% and CRIT stats due to her HP scaling and the significant damage boost provided by her Elemental Burst.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Weapon

*   **Aqua Simulacra:** Yelan's signature weapon is her undisputed BiS. It provides a massive CRIT DMG substat (88.2% at Lv. 90) and an HP% bonus (16% at Lv. 90). Its passive further increases the wielder's HP by 16% and grants an additional DMG bonus based on the wielder's HP when near enemies. This synergy with her HP scaling makes it unparalleled.

### Strong Free-to-Play (F2P) Options

*   **Fading Twilight:** This event-exclusive bow from Version 4.5 is an excellent F2P option. It offers Energy Recharge (ER) as a substat, which is valuable for Yelan, and its passive provides an ATK% bonus and a DMG bonus based on the weapon's refinement level. While not as potent as Aqua Simulacra, it offers a well-rounded stat package.
*   **Sacrificial Bow:** At higher refinements (R3+), Sacrificial Bow can be a viable option due to its high Base ATK and the chance to reset the cooldown of her Elemental Skill. This allows for more frequent use of Lingering Lifeline, leading to more Hydro application and potentially more energy generation. However, its ER substat is less desirable than CRIT or HP%.
*   **Favonius Warbow:** A solid generalist option, Favonius Warbow provides Energy Recharge, which helps Yelan reach her Burst uptime requirements. Its passive generates an Elemental Orb when a CRIT hit occurs, further aiding team-wide energy needs. This is particularly useful in teams where energy is a concern.

### Artifact Sets

*   **4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This is Yelan's premier artifact set and her BiS. The 2-piece bonus grants 20% Energy Recharge. The 4-piece bonus provides a DMG bonus based on 25% of the wearer's Energy Recharge, with a maximum bonus of 75%. This set perfectly synergizes with Yelan's high ER requirements and HP scaling, significantly boosting her personal damage. Aim for around 200-220% ER with this set.
*   **2-Piece Noblesse Oblige + 2-Piece Heart of Depth / Tenacity of the Millelith:** A viable alternative if 4pc Emblem pieces are not optimal. 2pc Noblesse provides Burst DMG, 2pc Heart of Depth provides Hydro DMG, and 2pc Tenacity provides HP%. This combination offers a decent damage boost but falls short of the synergy provided by 4pc Emblem.
*   **2-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith + 2-Piece Heart of Depth:** Focuses on HP% and Hydro DMG, offering a solid personal damage increase, but lacks the ER benefits of Emblem.

### Stat Priorities

*   **Sands:** HP% or Energy Recharge (if ER needs are not met by substats/weapon).
*   **Goblet:** Hydro DMG Bonus.
*   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio).
*   **Substats:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, HP%, Energy Recharge.

**Energy Recharge Thresholds:**
*   With Aqua Simulacra and a Hydro Resonance: ~160-180% ER.
*   With Favonius Warbow or Sacrificial Bow: ~180-200% ER.
*   With 4pc Emblem of Severed Fate: ~200-220% ER.
These values can be reduced with a battery character like Fischl or another Hydro unit that generates particles.

## Team Compositions

Yelan excels in various team archetypes, primarily due to her off-field Hydro application and damage amplification.

### Double Hydro with Xingqiu

This is a classic and highly effective pairing. Yelan and Xingqiu provide consistent off-field Hydro application, enabling powerful Vaporize or Freeze reactions for the on-field DPS. The Hydro Resonance also grants a 25% Max HP bonus to all party members, further benefiting Yelan and other HP-scaling characters.

*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Xingqiu, Xiangling, Bennett. This team focuses on enabling Xiangling's Pyronado with consistent Hydro application for Vaporize. Bennett provides ATK buffs and healing, while Xingqiu and Yelan offer Hydro and some damage reduction/minor healing.
*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Xingqiu, Ganyu, Kazuha. A strong Freeze variant. Yelan and Xingqiu provide Hydro for constant freezing, Ganyu acts as the primary Cryo DPS, and Kazuha groups enemies and provides Elemental DMG buffs.

### Furina Teams

Yelan is an exceptional partner for Furina, as both characters scale with HP and benefit from each other's presence. Furina's Elemental Burst provides a significant DMG% buff to the entire team, which stacks exceptionally well with Yelan's own ramping buff. Yelan's consistent Hydro application also helps Furina's Fanfare stacks accumulate faster.

*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Furina, Raiden Shogun, Baizhu. This team leverages Furina's buffs and Yelan's damage amplification. Raiden Shogun provides Electro for Overload/Superconduct/Electro-Charged, and Baizhu offers healing and Dendro application for Bloom/Hyperbloom.
*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Furina, Neuvillette, Kazuha. A powerful Hydro-centric team where Yelan provides off-field Hydro and a damage buff for Neuvillette, Furina amplifies damage, and Kazuha groups enemies and provides buffs.

### Neuvillette Teams

Yelan is one of Neuvillette's best off-field Hydro applicators and damage buffers. Neuvillette's Charged Attack requires consistent Hydro application to generate Sourcewater Droplets, and Yelan's Burst provides this reliably. Her ramping damage buff also significantly boosts Neuvillette's already high damage output.

*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Neuvillette, Kazuha, Baizhu. This team focuses on maximizing Neuvillette's damage. Yelan provides Hydro and buffs, Kazuha groups enemies and buffs Hydro DMG, and Baizhu provides healing and Dendro for potential Bloom/Hyperbloom reactions if other elements are present.
*   **Example Comp:** Yelan, Neuvillette, Fischl, Sucrose. A more offensive variant. Fischl provides off-field Electro for Electro-Charged, and Sucrose groups enemies and provides Elemental Mastery buffs.

## Yelan as a Xingqiu Upgrade

Yelan is often considered an upgrade over Xingqiu for most teams, primarily due to her superior damage scaling and a more potent damage amplification buff.

*   **Higher Personal Damage:** Yelan's damage scales entirely off her Max HP. With proper artifact builds and weapons like Aqua Simulacra, her personal damage output from her Skill and Burst can significantly surpass Xingqiu's, even with his higher base multipliers. This means she contributes more damage to the team directly.
*   **Ramping Damage Buff:** Yelan's Elemental Burst provides a unique ramping DMG% buff that can reach up to 50%. This buff significantly amplifies the damage of the on-field DPS character, especially those who can maintain consistent damage output over the duration of Yelan's Burst. Xingqiu offers a smaller, flat DMG% buff to Normal Attacks, which is less impactful for most modern DPS characters who rely on Charged Attacks or Elemental Skills.
*   **Hydro Application:** While Xingqiu's Hydro application is excellent and his Burst provides damage reduction and minor healing, Yelan's Hydro application is generally sufficient for most reaction-based teams, especially when paired with another Hydro unit or a strong off-field elemental applicator. Her Burst's Exquisite Throw triggers frequently enough to enable Vaporize, Freeze, or Electro-Charged.
*   **Team Flexibility:** Yelan's HP scaling makes her easier to build for damage in teams that already provide sufficient healing and energy, allowing her to focus on offensive stats. While Xingqiu's damage reduction and minor healing are valuable, Yelan's higher damage contribution and superior buff often outweigh these benefits in pure damage scenarios.

However, Xingqiu still holds value in specific situations. His damage reduction and minor healing can be crucial for survivability in teams lacking dedicated healers, and his consistent Hydro application is sometimes preferred for specific reaction setups where Yelan's ramping buff might not be fully utilized. Nevertheless, for teams prioritizing raw damage output and seeking the highest possible buffs for their main DPS, Yelan generally represents a significant upgrade.
