# Melt Reaction

Melt is a transformative elemental reaction in Genshin Impact, triggered when Pyro and Cryo elements interact. It is one of the most powerful damage-amplifying reactions, alongside Vaporize, due to its ability to multiply the damage of the triggering attack. The damage multiplier depends on which element triggers the reaction, making aura management and elemental application crucial for maximizing its potential.

## Core Mechanics

### Triggering Melt

Melt can be triggered in two directions, each with a distinct damage multiplier:

*   **Pyro Trigger (Reverse Melt):** When a Pyro attack hits an enemy afflicted by a Cryo aura, the Pyro damage is multiplied by **2.0x**. This is generally the preferred direction due to its higher multiplier and more efficient elemental consumption.
*   **Cryo Trigger (Forward Melt):** When a Cryo attack hits an enemy afflicted by a Pyro aura, the Cryo damage is multiplied by **1.5x**. While still a significant damage boost, it is less commonly optimized for main DPS strategies compared to Reverse Melt.

The base damage multiplier is then further scaled by the triggering character's Elemental Mastery (EM) and other reaction-specific bonuses.

### Elemental Mastery Scaling

Elemental Mastery directly increases the damage multiplier of Melt. The formula for the final damage multiplier is:

`Final Multiplier = Base Multiplier * (1 + EM Bonus)`

Where `EM Bonus = (2.78 * EM) / (1400 + EM)`.

For example, at 0 EM, the multiplier is 2.0x or 1.5x. At 200 EM, the EM Bonus is approximately 0.
