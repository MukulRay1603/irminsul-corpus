# Vaporize Reaction

The Vaporize reaction is a powerful multiplicative elemental reaction in Genshin Impact, occurring when Hydro and Pyro elements interact. Unlike transformative reactions (e.g., Electro-Charged, Swirl), Vaporize directly amplifies the damage of the triggering attack, making it a cornerstone of many high-damage team compositions. The reaction consumes both the existing elemental aura on the target and the triggering element, with the damage bonus applied to the specific damage instance that causes the reaction.

## Directional Multipliers

The damage multiplier of Vaporize depends on which element triggers the reaction:

*   **Forward Vaporize (Hydro on Pyro):** When a Hydro attack hits a target afflicted by a Pyro aura, the Hydro damage is multiplied by **2.0x**. This direction is generally less common for sustained damage due to the difficulty of maintaining a Pyro aura against consistent Hydro application, but can be utilized for large burst damage instances
