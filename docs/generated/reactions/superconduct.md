# Superconduct Reaction

Superconduct is an Elemental Reaction in Genshin Impact triggered by combining the Cryo and Electro elements. Its primary and most significant effect is to reduce the Physical Resistance (RES) of affected enemies by a fixed 40%. This debuff lasts for a duration of 12 seconds from the moment of application. Unlike transformative reactions such as Electro-Charged or Overload, Superconduct deals a negligible amount of Cryo damage that scales with Elemental Mastery (EM) and character level, making its damage component largely irrelevant in practical combat scenarios. Its sole purpose and immense value lie in its Physical RES shred.

### Core Mechanics and Physical RES Shred

When an enemy is affected by both Cryo and Electro, Superconduct is triggered, applying the 40% Physical RES reduction. This reduction is a flat subtraction from the enemy's existing Physical RES. For example, an enemy with 10% Physical RES will have their Physical RES reduced to -30% after Superconduct. An enemy with 70% Physical RES will see it drop to 30%. This mechanism is particularly potent because reducing an enemy's resistance below 0% results in a multiplicative damage bonus, making the 40% shred a substantial damage increase for any Physical damage source. The 12-second duration is generous, allowing for consistent uptime with proper elemental application.

### Exclusive Relevance to Physical DPS

Superconduct is almost exclusively vital for teams centered around Physical Damage Per Second (DPS) characters. Other elemental reactions either deal direct damage (e.g., Overload, Electro-Charged, Bloom), amplify existing elemental damage (e.g., Vaporize, Melt), or provide utility (e.g., Swirl spreading elements). Superconduct stands apart as the only reaction that directly and significantly boosts Physical damage by reducing enemy defenses. Without Superconduct, Physical DPS characters struggle to compete with elemental damage dealers, especially against enemies with naturally high Physical RES, such as Ruin Guards, Ruin Graders, and many elite bosses. The consistent application of Superconduct is non-negotiable for optimizing Physical damage output.

### Efficient Triggering Characters

Maintaining consistent Superconduct uptime requires reliable off-field Cryo and Electro application. Characters who can apply their elements through skills or bursts that persist on the field are highly valued for this role.
