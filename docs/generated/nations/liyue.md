# Liyue — Nation of Contracts

Liyue, the northernmost of the Seven Nations, is a land steeped in tradition, commerce, and the enduring legacy of its Geo Archon, Rex Lapis, also known as Morax. Its civilization is founded upon the bedrock of contracts, a principle deeply ingrained in its culture and governance, reflecting the Archon's own philosophy of order and stability. This nation, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and bustling trade, is a testament to the harmonious, albeit sometimes strained, coexistence between humanity, the adepti, and the divine.

## Geography and Key Locations

Liyue's diverse geography ranges from the towering peaks of Jueyun Karst to the vibrant metropolis of Liyue Harbor, each area holding unique significance.

### Liyue Harbor

Liyue Harbor stands as the undisputed trade capital of Teyvat, a sprawling port city that pulsates with economic activity. Its docks are a constant hive of ships arriving from and departing to every corner of the world, facilitating the exchange of goods and ideas. The city is meticulously planned, with distinct districts catering to various commercial and residential needs. The **Fei-Yuan Teahouse** offers a glimpse into the social fabric, while the **Xingxing Palace**, the seat of the Liyue Qixing, overlooks the harbor, symbolizing their control over the city's affairs. The sheer volume of trade passing through Liyue Harbor is staggering, making it the economic engine of the nation and a crucial hub for global commerce. The city's architecture is a blend of traditional Liyuean styles and functional designs suited for a bustling port, with tiered roofs, intricate carvings, and sturdy stone structures.

### Jueyun Karst

To the north of Liyue Harbor lies Jueyun Karst, a mystical mountain range perpetually shrouded in mist and home to the adepti. These ancient beings, immortal protectors of Liyue, reside in secluded abodes amidst the jagged peaks and serene cloud seas. The **Cloud Retainer's Domicile**, **Mount Tianheng**, and **Mt. Aocang** are prominent landmarks within this region, each serving as a sanctuary for different adepti. The natural beauty of Jueyun Karst is unparalleled, with cascading waterfalls, ancient trees, and hidden grottos. It is here that the adepti, bound by their ancient contract with Morax, watch over Liyue, intervening only when the nation faces existential threats. The spiritual energy of this region is palpable, making it a place of both reverence and mystery.

### Qingce Village

Nestled within a verdant valley, Qingce Village is a picturesque rural settlement characterized by its traditional Liyuean farming practices and serene atmosphere. The village is known for its abundant rice paddies, meticulously cultivated by its inhabitants. The **Qingce Stream** flows through the heart of the village, providing life-giving water to the surrounding fields. It represents a more pastoral and traditional aspect of Liyuean life, a stark contrast to the bustling commerce of the harbor. The villagers maintain a close connection to the land and often rely on the wisdom of the adepti for guidance, particularly regarding agricultural matters.

### Stone Gate

The Stone Gate is a crucial mountain pass that serves as a natural barrier and a vital route connecting Liyue to Mondstadt. Its treacherous terrain and narrow passages have historically made it a strategic point, often subject to banditry and territorial disputes. The **Stone Gate Pass** itself is a formidable natural fortification, with sheer cliffs and winding paths. It marks the transition from the more settled regions of Liyue to the wilder frontiers, and its control has often been contested throughout history.

### Guyun Stone Forest

Off the coast of Liyue, the Guyun Stone Forest is a hauntingly beautiful archipelago of towering stone pillars that rise from the sea. This region is infamous as the site where the ancient sea god Osial was defeated and imprisoned by Morax millennia ago. The colossal stone spears that pierce the sky are said to be the petrified remains of Osial's colossal form and his followers, a stark reminder of the Archon's power and the ancient conflicts that shaped Liyue. The area is now a place of both awe and trepidation, a testament to Liyue's deep history and the primordial forces it has overcome.

## Governance and Society

Liyue's governance is a unique blend of merchantocracy and divine mandate, with the Liyue Qixing at its helm.

### The Liyue Qixing

The Liyue Qixing, meaning "Seven Stars of Liyue," is the governing body of Liyue, composed of seven influential figures from the nation's merchant guilds and business elite. They are responsible for the day-to-day administration of Liyue, including trade regulations, law enforcement, and economic policy. Each member represents a different facet of Liyue's commercial interests, ensuring that the nation's prosperity remains paramount. Their decisions are guided by the principles of contract law and the pursuit of collective wealth. The Qixing operates with a high degree of autonomy, managing Liyue's affairs under the overarching guidance of the Geo Archon, who historically provided ultimate authority and protection. Their pragmatic approach to governance has been instrumental in Liyue's sustained economic success.

### The Adepti and the Contract with Morax

The adepti are immortal beings of immense power who have served as Liyue's protectors for millennia. They entered into a sacred contract with Morax, the Geo Archon, pledging their loyalty and strength in exchange for his protection and the preservation of their way of life. This contract formed the bedrock of Liyue's security, with the adepti acting as guardians against external threats and internal chaos. Key figures among the adepti include **Cloud Retainer**, **Mountain Shaper**, and **Streetward Rambler**, each possessing unique abilities and domains. Their relationship with humanity is one of benevolent oversight, intervening only when the balance of Liyue is threatened. The contract was a foundational element of Liyue's stability, ensuring peace for thousands of years.

### The Rite of Descension and Rex Lapis Mythology

The Rite of Descension was an annual ceremony where the Geo Archon, Rex Lapis (Morax), would descend from the heavens to commune with his people and the Liyue Qixing, offering guidance and reaffirming his divine authority. This ritual was central to Liyuean belief, solidifying the Archon's role as the protector and guiding force of the nation. The mythology surrounding Rex Lapis portrays him as a wise and just ruler, a formidable warrior who defeated ancient gods, and the architect of Liyue's prosperity. His influence permeated every aspect of Liyuean society, from its legal system to its cultural practices. The Rite served as a tangible manifestation of the divine-human covenant.

### Annual Lantern Rite Celebration

The Lantern Rite is Liyue's most significant annual festival, a vibrant celebration held during the first full moon of the year. It is a time for families to reunite, to honor ancestors, and to send wishes and prayers into the sky via illuminated sky lanterns. The festival commemorates the Archon's victory over ancient evils and serves as a reminder of the enduring bonds between the people of Liyue. The **Sea of Lights**, a breathtaking spectacle of countless lanterns illuminating the night sky over Liyue Harbor, is the centerpiece of the celebration. It is a time of joy, reflection, and communal spirit, reinforcing Liyue's cultural identity.

## Cultural and Societal Pillars

Liyue's unique culture is shaped by its deep respect for tradition, its pragmatic approach to commerce, and its unique understanding of life and death.

### The Wangsheng Funeral Parlor

The Wangsheng Funeral Parlor plays a vital, albeit somber, role in Liyuean society. It is responsible for overseeing funeral rites and ensuring that the deceased pass on peacefully to the afterlife. This institution reflects Liyue's pragmatic and respectful approach to death, viewing it as a natural transition rather than an end. The parlor's director, **Hu Tao**, is a charismatic and eccentric figure who embodies the parlor's philosophy. Their services are essential for maintaining societal order and providing comfort to the living, ensuring that the cycle of life and death is respected.

### Osial's Backstory

Osial was a formidable ancient sea god, a primordial entity of immense destructive power who sought to conquer Liyue in the early days of its formation. He was one of the many ancient gods defeated by Morax during the Archon War. Osial's ambition was to drown Liyue and reclaim its lands for the primordial chaos. Morax, in a monumental battle, ultimately defeated Osial, shattering his power and imprisoning his essence within the Guyun Stone Forest. The colossal stone pillars that dominate the landscape are a testament to the scale of this ancient conflict and the power Morax wielded to protect his nascent nation.

### Trust and Contract Law

The entirety of Liyuean civilization is built upon the foundation of trust and contract law. Morax, understanding the inherent chaos of the world, established a society where agreements, both spoken and written, held the highest authority. This principle extends from the grand economic treaties brokered in Liyue Harbor to the simple promises made between individuals. The Liyue Qixing enforces these contracts rigorously, ensuring fairness and stability. This emphasis on contracts fosters a culture of reliability and accountability, making Liyue a beacon of order and prosperity in Teyvat. The concept of "trust" in Liyue is not merely a social nicety but a fundamental legal and economic principle that underpins their entire civilization.
