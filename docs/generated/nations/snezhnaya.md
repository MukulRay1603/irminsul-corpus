# Snezhnaya and the Fatui

Snezhnaya, the frigid northernmost nation of Teyvat, is the homeland of the Cryo Archon, the Tsaritsa. It is a land perpetually covered in snow and ice, characterized by its harsh climate and the resilience of its people. While officially a nation that values love and devotion, as proclaimed by its Archon, the reality of Snezhnaya's influence on the world stage is far more complex and often sinister, primarily through the actions of the Fatui. The Fatui, ostensibly the diplomatic corps and a military force of Snezhnaya, function as a vast global intelligence and military network, serving the Tsaritsa's mysterious agenda. Their operations span across all other nations, often involving espionage, sabotage, and the forceful acquisition of powerful artifacts, most notably the Gnoses of other Archons.

## The Fatui and Their Global Operations

The Fatui are the primary instrument through which Snezhnaya exerts its influence. Their public face is that of diplomats and merchants, but their true purpose is to serve the Tsaritsa's will, which has become increasingly focused on a grand, albeit poorly understood, objective. This objective involves the collection of all seven Gnoses, the divine symbols of authority and power held by each Archon. The Fatui are organized into various branches, each specializing in different forms of clandestine operations. These include intelligence gathering, economic manipulation, military intervention, and the direct apprehension of individuals or entities possessing significant power or knowledge.

Their operations are characterized by a ruthless efficiency and a willingness to employ any means necessary to achieve their goals. Examples include the Fatui's presence and interference in Liyue Harbor, where they attempted to steal the Geo Archon's Gnosis from Zhongli, and their involvement in Inazuma, where they exploited political instability and manipulated the Shogunate's policies to further their own ends, including the acquisition of the Electro Archon's Gnosis. The Fatui's economic power is also considerable, with their Skirmishers and Agents often seen in various cities, engaging in trade and seemingly legitimate business while simultaneously acting as eyes and ears for the organization. Their military might is also evident, with their elite soldiers, the Fatui Skirmishers, posing a significant threat to adventurers and national forces alike. These Skirmishers utilize elemental abilities, often in conjunction with specialized weaponry, making them formidable opponents.

## The Eleven Fatui Harbingers

At the apex of the Fatui hierarchy are the Eleven Fatui Harbingers, a council of powerful individuals who command the organization's vast resources and execute its most critical missions. Each Harbinger possesses a unique title and a significant degree of power, often derived from Delusions – artificial divine imprints that grant immense elemental abilities at a great cost. *   **3. The Regrator (Pantalone):** A shrewd businessman and economist who controls the Fatui's vast financial empire.
*   **4. The Puppet (Arlecchino):** The director of the House of the Hearth, an orphanage that serves as a recruitment and training ground for Fatui agents. She is known for her strict and unforgiving methods.

The exact nature of their power and influence is not fully detailed, but their actions consistently demonstrate a high level of competence and authority within the Fatui organization. The Harbingers are often dispatched to pursue specific objectives related to the Tsaritsa's Gnosis collection and her broader plans.

## The Tsaritsa's Motivation and the Gnoses

The Tsaritsa, the Cryo Archon, is the enigmatic leader of Snezhnaya and the ultimate authority behind the Fatui. Once known as the Archon of Love, her disposition has drastically changed, leading to her current pursuit of power and her apparent abandonment of her people's well-being in favor of her grand, destructive plan. The catalyst for this transformation is heavily implied to be a profound betrayal or loss, possibly connected to the Cataclysm that devastated Khaenri'ah millennia ago and the subsequent actions of the divine entity known as Celestia.

The Cataclysm, a world-altering event that led to the destruction of Khaenri'ah and the curse of immortality upon its people, is a significant historical turning point. It is theorized that Celestia, the divine power residing above Teyvat, may have orchestrated or allowed this destruction, leading to a deep disillusionment with the divine order among some Archons. The Tsaritsa's loss of love is often interpreted as a rejection of the current divine system and its perceived injustices.

Her current objective is to gather all seven Gnoses. The purpose behind this collection is not explicitly stated, but it is widely believed that she intends to use their combined power to wage war against Celestia and the divine order. The Gnoses are not merely symbols of authority; they are fragments of the original divine power that established the Seven Archons. By collecting them, the Tsaritsa aims to amass enough power to challenge the very heavens. This ambition positions her as a revolutionary figure, seeking to dismantle a corrupt and oppressive divine system, but her methods are undeniably destructive and cause immense suffering across Teyvat.

## Snezhnaya's Industrial and Military Might

The Fatui's global presence and their sophisticated technology, particularly their use of Delusions and advanced weaponry, point to Snezhnaya possessing a highly developed industrial and military capacity. Their ability to produce and distribute Delusions on a large scale, coupled with their well-equipped and disciplined soldiers, suggests a nation with advanced scientific knowledge and manufacturing capabilities. The Fatui's economic influence, as seen through their trade networks and financial operations, further underscores Snezhnaya's economic power. This industrial and military strength allows them to operate with impunity in many regions, often overwhelming local defenses and challenging even the most powerful entities. The existence of mechanical constructs and advanced alchemical processes utilized by the Fatui hints at a level of technological advancement that surpasses that of other nations, particularly in areas of military application and elemental manipulation.

## The Tsaritsa's True Nature: Villain or Revolutionary?

The question of whether the Tsaritsa is a villain or a necessary revolutionary remains one of the most compelling mysteries in Genshin Impact. Her actions, driven by a perceived betrayal and a desire to overthrow Celestia, have led to widespread conflict and suffering. The Fatui's ruthless tactics, the manipulation of nations, and the acquisition of Gnoses through coercion and violence paint her as an antagonist. However, her ultimate goal—to challenge a potentially unjust divine authority that may have caused immense suffering in the past—lends credence to the idea of her being a revolutionary figure fighting for a greater, albeit brutal, cause. The narrative presents her as a complex character whose motivations stem from deep-seated pain and a desire for cosmic justice, even if her methods are morally reprehensible. The Traveler's interactions with various Fatui members, particularly those who show glimpses of humanity or express doubts about their mission, further complicate this dichotomy, suggesting that the Fatui and the Tsaritsa's plan may not be entirely monolithic in their intent or execution. The ultimate outcome of her plan and her true role in the grand narrative of Teyvat are yet to be fully revealed.
